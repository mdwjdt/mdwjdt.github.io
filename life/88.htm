《新耶路撒冷教义之生活篇》 第88节

Life88.由于谎言与真理是两种对立的事物，所以可推知，任何人避说谎如罪到何等程度，就在何等程度上热爱真理。

Life (Dole translation 2014) 88

88. Since lying and truth are two opposite things, it follows that to the extent that we turn our backs on lying because it is a sin, we love truth.

Doctrine of Life (Rogers translation 2014) 88

88. Since lying and the truth are two opposites, it follows that insofar as someone refrains from lying as being a sin, so far he loves truth.

Doctrine of Life (Dick translation 1954) 88

88. As falsehood and truth are two opposites, it follows that, so far as any one shuns falsehood as sin, so far he loves truth.

Doctrine of Life (Potts translation 1904) 88

88. As lying and the truth are two opposite things, it follows that in proportion as anyone shuns lying as sin, in the same proportion he loves the truth.

Doctrina Vitae pro Nova Hierosolyma 88 (original Latin 1763)

88. Quoniam mendacium et veritas sunt duo opposita, sequitur, quod quantum quis fugit mendacium ut peccatum, tantum amet veritatem.
