《新教会教义纲要》 第51节

BE51.十一、从当今教会之信导致口头上的敬拜，而非生活上的敬拜；口头上的敬拜只有与生活相一致时才会被主接受。

A Brief Exposition of New Church Doctrine (Stanley translation 1953) 51

51. XI. From the Faith of the present Church there flows forth a worship of the mouth and not of the life; when yet the worship of the mouth is accepted by the Lord only so far as it accords with worship which is of the life.

A Brief Exposition of New Church Doctrine (Whitehead translation 1892) 51

51. XI. From the faith of the present church there results a worship of the mouth and not of the life, whereas the worship of the mouth is accepted by the Lord according to the worship of the life.

Summaria Expositio 51 (original Latin 1769)

51. XI. Quod ex Fide hodiernae Ecclesiae redundet Cultus oris et non vitae, cum tamen cultus oris acceptus est Domino secundum cultum vitae.
