《新教会教义纲要》 第99节

BE99.二十三、由主建立的新教会就是启示录第21和22章所说的“新耶路撒冷”，她还被称为“新妇”和“羔羊的妻”。

A Brief Exposition of New Church Doctrine (Stanley translation 1953) 99

99. XXIII. The New Church about to be established by the Lord is the New Jerusalem treated of in the Revelation, chapters 21 and 22, which is there called the Bride and Wife of the Lamb.

A Brief Exposition of New Church Doctrine (Whitehead translation 1892) 99

99. XXIII. The New Church about to be established by the Lord is the New Jerusalem, treated of in Revelation (chap. 21 and 22), which is there called "the Bride and the Wife of the Lamb."

Summaria Expositio 99 (original Latin 1769)

99. XXIII. 1Apocalypsi 21 Cap. et 22, agitur; quae ibi vocatur Sponsa et Uxor Agni.
