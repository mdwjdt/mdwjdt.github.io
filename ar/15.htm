《揭秘启示录》 第15节

（本 2019）

15.“耶稣基督”表神性人。在圣言中，“耶稣基督”和“羔羊”表主神性人，这一点可参看上文（6节）。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 15

15. And from Jesus Christ. (1:5) This symbolizes His Divine humanity.

It may be seen in no. 6 above that Jesus Christ and the Lamb in the Word mean the Lord in respect to His Divine humanity.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 15

15. [verse 5] 'And from Jesus Christ' signifies the Divine Human. That by 'Jesus Christ' and 'the Lamb' in the Word is understood the Lord as to the Divine Human may be seen above (6).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 15

15. Verse 5 (Revelation 1:5). And from Jesus Christ, signifies the Divine Human. That by "Jesus Christ" and by "the Lamb" in the Word, is meant the Lord as to the Divine Human, may be seen above, (6).

Apocalypsis Revelata 15 (original Latin 1766)

15. (Vers. 5.) "Et a Jesu Christo," significat Divinum Humanum. - Quod per "Jesum Christum" et per "Agnum" in Verbo intelligatur Dominus quoad Divinum Humanum, videatur supra (6).
