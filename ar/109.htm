《揭秘启示录》 第109节

（本 2019）

109.“我知道你的行为”表主立刻洞悉他们所有的内层和外层。这一点可见于前文（76节），那里解释了这句话。此处它表示主洞悉他们只重行为，不顾教义事物。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 109

109. "'I know your works.'" (2:13) This symbolically means that the Lord sees all a person's inner and outer qualities simultaneously, as may be seen in no. 97 above where the same words are explained - here that the Lord sees that the people meant now are concerned only with works and not with matters of doctrine.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 109

109. [verse 13] That 'I know thy works' signifies that the Lord sees all their interiors and exteriors at once may be seen above (76), where [these words] have been expounded. Here it signifies that the Lord sees that they are in works alone, and not in doctrinal things.

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 109

109. Verse 13. I know thy works; that this signifies that the Lord sees all their interiors and exteriors at once, may be seen above, (76), where the same is explained; here that the Lord sees that they are in works alone, and not in doctrinals.

Apocalypsis Revelata 109 (original Latin 1766)

109. (Vers. 13.) "Novi opera tua." - Quod significet quod Dominus videat omnia interiora et exteriora illorum simul, videatur supra (76), ubi explicata sunt; hic quod Dominus videat quod in solis operibus sint, et non in doctrinalibus.
