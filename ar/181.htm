《揭秘启示录》 第181节

（本 2019）

181. “看哪，我要从撒但会堂里”表那些在教义上处于虚假之人。这可参看上文（97节）。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 181

181. "'Behold, I will make those of the synagogue of Satan.'" (3:9) This symbolizes people who are caught up in falsities as regards doctrine, as may be seen in no. 97 above.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 181

181. [verse 9] 'And I will make (dabo) those of the synagogue of Satan' signifies those who as to doctrine are in untruths, [as] may be seen above (97).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 181

181. Verse 9. And I will give from the synagogue of Satan, signifies those who are in falsities as to doctrine, as may be seen above, (97).

Apocalypsis Revelata 181 (original Latin 1766)

181. (Vers. 9.) "Et dabo et synagoga satanae." - Quod significet illos qui in falsis quoad doctrinam sunt, videatur supra (97).
