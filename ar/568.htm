《揭秘启示录》 第568节

（本 2019）

568.“有七头”表由纯粹的虚假所产生的疯狂。和“龙的七头”一样（538节）。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 568

568. Having seven heads. This symbolizes the irrationality resulting from their absolute falsities, like the seven heads of the dragon (no. 538).

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 568

568. 'Having seven heads' signifies the insanity resulting from sheer untruths, in like manner as by the 'seven heads' of the dragon (538).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 568

568. Having seven heads, signifies insanity from mere falsities, in like manner as by "the seven heads of the dragon" (538).

Apocalypsis Revelata 568 (original Latin 1766)

568. "Habentem capita septem," significat insaniam ex meris falsis, similiter ac per "septem capita Draconis" (538).
