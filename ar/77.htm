77.“劳碌、忍耐”表他们的钻研和耐心。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 77

77. "'Your labor, and your patience.'" That this symbolizes their effort and perseverance is evident without explanation.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 77

77. That 'And thy labour, and thine endurance' signifies their devotion and patience is established without an exposition.

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 77

77. And thy labor and endurance; that this signifies their study and patience, is evident without explanation.

Apocalypsis Revelata 77 (original Latin 1766)

77. "Et laborem tuum et tolerantiam tuam." - Quod significet studium et patientiam illorum, constat absque explicatione.
