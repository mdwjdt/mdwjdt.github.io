《揭秘启示录》 第40节

（本 2019）

40.“寄给亚细亚的这些教会”表给那些基督教界处于来自圣言的真理之光的人。他们由“亚细亚的这些教会”来表示（参看10，11节）。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 40

40. "And send it to the churches which are in Asia." This symbolically means, for those in the Christian world who have the light of truth from the Word.

That these are meant by the churches in Asia may be see in nos. 10 and 11 above.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 40

40. 'And send to these Churches in Asia' signifies for those in Christendom who are in the light of the truth (veritas) out of the Word. That those are understood by 'the Churches in Asia' may be seen above (10-11).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 40

40. And send to the churches which are in Asia, signifies for those in the Christian world, who are in the light of truth from the Word. That they are meant by the "churches in Asia," may be seen above, (10, 11).

Apocalypsis Revelata 40 (original Latin 1766)

40. "Et mitte Ecclesiis, illis in Asia," significat pro illis in Christiano Orbe qui in luce veritatis ex Verbo sunt. -Quod illi per "Ecclesias in Asia" intelligantur, videatur supra (10, 11).
