Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 318

318. I heard the voice of the fourth living creature saying. This symbolically means, in accordance with the Divine truth in the Word, as in nos. 296, 303 above.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 318

318. 'I heard the voice of the fourth animal saying' signifies in accordance with the Divine Truth of the Word, as above (296, 303).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 318

318. I heard the voice of the fourth animal saying, signifies according to the Divine truth of the Word, as above, (296, 303).

Apocalypsis Revelata 318 (original Latin 1766)

318. "Audivi vocem quarti Animalis dicentem," significat secundum Divinum Verum Verbi, ut supra (296, 303).
