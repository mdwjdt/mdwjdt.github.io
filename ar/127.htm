（本 2019）

127.“我知道你的行为”表主立刻洞悉他们所有的内层和外层。这可参看上文（76节），那里解释这句话。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 127

127. "'I know your works.'" (2:19) This symbolically means that the Lord sees all these people's inner and outer qualities simultaneously, as may be seen in no. 76 above and the explanations there.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 127

127. [verse 19] That 'I know thy works' signifies that the Lord sees all their interiors and exteriors at once may be seen above (76), where [these words] have been expounded.

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 127

127. Verse 19. I know thy works, signifies that the Lord sees all their interiors and exteriors at once, as may be seen above, where these words are explained, (76).

Apocalypsis Revelata 127 (original Latin 1766)

127. (Vers. 19.) "Novi tua opera." - Quod significet quod Dominus videat omnia interiora et exteriora illorum simul, videatur supra (76), ubi explicata sunt.
