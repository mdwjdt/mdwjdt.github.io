《揭秘启示录》 第375节

（本 2019）

375.“阿们”表所有人的赞同。这一节以“阿们”开头，又以“阿们”结尾；开头的“阿们”表示真理和由此而来的确认（371节），结尾的“阿们”表示所有人都确认并赞同这就是真理。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 375

375. "Amen." This symbolizes the agreement of all.

This verse begins with the word "Amen," and now ends with it. When it is said at the beginning, it symbolizes truth and an affirmation in response to it (no. 371). But when it is said at the end, it symbolizes an affirmation and agreement by all that what has been said is the truth.

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 375

375. 'Amen' signifies the agreement of all. In this verse 'Amen' is said at the beginning, and now at its end When it is said at the beginning it signifies the Truth (veritas) and confirmation therefrom (371), but when said at the end it signifies the confirmation and agreement of all that it is the truth (veritas).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 375

375. Amen, signifies the consent of all. In this verse "Amen" is said at the beginning, and again at the end; when it is said at the beginning it signifies truth, and thence confirmation, (371); but when at the end, it signifies the confirmation and consent of all that it is the truth.

Apocalypsis Revelata 375 (original Latin 1766)

375. "Amen," significat consensum omnium. - In hoc versu dicitur "amen" in principio, et nunc in fine ejus; cum dicitur illi principio, significat Veritatem, et ex illa Confirmationem (371); cum autem in fine, significat Confirmationem et Consensum omnium quod veritas sit.
