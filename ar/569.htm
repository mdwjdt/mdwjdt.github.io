《揭秘启示录》 第569节

（本 2019）

569.“十角”表大有能力。和龙的角一样，龙的角也是十个（539节）。

Apocalypse Revealed (Rogers translation 2007) 569

569. And ten horns. This symbolizes their great power, like the horns of the dragon, of which there were likewise ten (no. 539).

Apocalypse Revealed (Coulson translation 1970) 569

569. 'And ten horns' signifies great power, the same as by the 'horns' of the dragon, which also were 'ten' (539).

Apocalypse Revealed (Whitehead translation 1928) 569

569. And ten horns, signifies much power, in like manner as the horns of the dragon, which also were ten, (539).

Apocalypsis Revelata 569 (original Latin 1766)

569. "Et cornua decem," significat potentiam multam, simile quod per cornua Draconis, quae etiam erant decem (539).
