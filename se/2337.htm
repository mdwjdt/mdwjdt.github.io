Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2337

2337. Altitude and distance are appearances, and deceptive ones. This has sometimes been demonstrated, as when those who were beneath the feet were all at once let down deep, even very deep, and then all at once were near the head. This made spirits wonder how it is done, and then they were told that these changes of place are illusions arising from fantasies. 1748, 16 June.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2337

2337. (That altitude and distance are appearances and fallacies is demonstrated sometimes, to wit, that those who are beneath the feet are immediately raised up on high, yea, the very highest, and forthwith near to the head, so that spirits wondered how it was done, and they were then told that such changes of place are fallacies arising from phantasies. - 1748, June 16.)

Experientiae Spirituales 2337 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2337. Quod altitudo et distantia sint apparentiae, et fallaciae, demonstratum est quandoque, nempe quod ii qui infra pedes, illico in altum imo in altissimum delati sint, et illico prope caput, quod mirati spiritus, quomodo id fit, et iis tunc dictum quod tales locorum mutationes sint fallaciae ex phantasiis oriundae. 1748, 16 Junius.
