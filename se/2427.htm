Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2427

2427. These matters are much more clearly visible by means of spiritual mental imagery than they are able to be seen by people on earth without it. Spirits grasp them much better than people on earth because their ideas are such that they have the capacity of understanding by means of spiritual mental imagery.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2427

2427. These things are much more obvious by means of spiritual ideas than could ever be seen by man without such an idea. Spirits apprehend these things much better than men because their ideas are of such a nature, and they, indeed, capable of perceiving by spiritual ideas. - 1748, June 28.

Experientiae Spirituales 2427 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2427. Haec per ideas spirituales multo magis conspicua sunt, quam ut ab homine absque tali idea videri possunt. Spiritus ea multo melius quam homines capiunt, quia eorum ideae sunt tales, et quidem capaces percipiendi per ideas spirituales. 1748, 28 Junius.
