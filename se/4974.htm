Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4974

4974. But all the government was from the love of self and the world, not from love for saving the human race, thus it was from diabolical love and not at all from Divine love.

4974. But this whole ruling was from the love of self and the world, not from the love of saving the human race; consequently, from diabolic love, and not at all from Divine love.

Experientiae Spirituales 4974 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4974. Sed omne regimen erat ex amore sui et mundi, non ex amore salvandi genus humanum, ita ex amore diabolico, et nullatenus ex amore Divino.
