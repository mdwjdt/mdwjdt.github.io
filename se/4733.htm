Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4733

4733. And a sword with a silver hilt is also given to them, together with a belt. And when they carry this sword, evil spirits dare nothing. The golden heart and sword with a silver hilt are symbolic of their inward associations with angels.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4733

4733. And there is also given to them a sword with a silver hilt, together with a belt. When they carry that sword, evil spirits dare do nothing. The golden heart, and the sword with the silver hilt, are representative of interior angelic consociations.

Experientiae Spirituales 4733 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4733. Et quoque gladius cum capulo argenteo illis donatur una cum cingulo, cum illum gladium portant, tunc mali spiritus nihil audent. Cor aureum et gladius argentei capuli est repraesentativum consociationum angelicarum interiorum.
