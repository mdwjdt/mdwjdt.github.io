Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4197

4197. There is a withholding

When I was among those being purged, or among the infernals, then I was repeatedly lifted up from them, and withheld there, so there is such a thing as being withheld, and again being let down, and again being elevated as it were and withheld. 1749, 3 April. 1

Footnotes:

1. Astrological symbol of the sun, meaning Sunday.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4197

4197. WHAT WITHHOLDING IS.

When I was among those who were vastated or among the infernals, I was everywhere elevated above them, and there destined or withheld, so that there might be a withholding, and then an immission or letting down, and again an elevation, as it were, and again a withholding. - 1749, April 3.

Experientiae Spirituales 4197 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4197. Quod sit detentio

Cum fui inter vastatos, vel inter infernales, tunc passim elevatus ab iis, et ibi detentus, sic ut sit detentio, et iterum immissio, et iterum elevatio quasi et detentio. 1749, 3 April.
