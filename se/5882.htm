Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5882

5882. About the new heaven

I saw that those who had been kept safe by the Lord were raised up, to the number of tens of thousands; and those who were raised up appeared like mist rising from the ground when heat from the sky comes over it after the rain.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5882

5882. THE NEW HEAVEN.

I saw that those who were reserved by the Lord were taken up, to the number of tens of thousands; and they who were taken up, appeared like a mist rising up from the ground, when the heat of heaven beats down upon it after rain.

Experientiae Spirituales 5882 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5882. De novo coelo

Vidi quod illi qui reservati a Domino elevati sint ad myriades, et apparuerunt qui elevati, sicut aereum exsurgens ex terra post pluviam calore coeli superveniente.
