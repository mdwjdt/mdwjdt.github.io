Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5331

5331. All of these, of whom there was in enormous number, were led down to the northern shore of the western sea, and were thrown into the sea. Also all the monasteries collapsed into dust, and [the whole region] went up in smoke and was carried down into the sea.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5331

5331. All of these - and their number was immense - were brought down to the northern shore of the western sea, and cast into the sea; and all the monasteries fell into dust; and [everything that was in them] vanished into smoke, and was carried down into that sea.

Experientiae Spirituales 5331 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5331. Omnes illi, quorum ingens numerus fuit, deducti sunt ad litus septentrionale maris occidentalis, et in mare conjecti sunt; etiam omnia monasteria in pulverem dilapsa, et [totus tractus] abivit in fumum et delatus est in id mare.
