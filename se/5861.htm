Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5861

5861. At the end the destruction proceeded to the Mohammedans who were in the west in front and were all hidden by the earth beneath them, for they looked to the Christian world, which was forbidden them.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5861

5861. At length, the destruction reached to the Mohammedans who were forwards at the west; and they were all swallowed up by the earth which was under them; for they looked to the Christian world, which was forbidden to them.

Experientiae Spirituales 5861 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5861. Demum pergebat destructio ad Mahumedanos qui in occidente antrorsum, et illi obtecti sunt omnes terra quae sub illis, nam spectabant ad orbem Christianum, quod illis vetitum fuit.
