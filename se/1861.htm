Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1861

1861. I was also allowed to feel the warmth of those who, while they do take pleasure in the Word of the Lord, are not much concerned with understanding it. This warmth was felt only in the right arm.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1861

1861. (It was moreover given me to perceive also the heat of those who are delighted in the Word of the Lord, but care little about understanding it; it was perceived only in the left arm.

Experientiae Spirituales 1861 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1861. Porro quoque mihi dabatur percipere calorem eorum, qui quidem delectantur Verbo Domini, sed ejus intellectum non ita curant, is percipiebatur solum in brachio dextro.
