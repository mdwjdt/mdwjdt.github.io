Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2051

2051. Spirits can also produce odors

It has happened several times that spirits produced odors, just as if the objects causing the odors were at hand, about which, I believe, I have spoken here and there before [323, 791, 1044-1047]. Today, during a conversation about flowers, specifically lilies, they produced a very real odor of lily flowers, a fact that it is permitted only to make note of. 1748, 22 May.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2051

2051. THAT SPIRITS CAN ALSO PRODUCE ODORS.

(It has sometimes happened that spirits have produced odors, just as if the objects of the odors had been present, concerning which, if I mistake not, I have frequently spoken before. Today, while discoursing of flowers and lilies, they produced a very perceptible odor of flowers and lilies, as to which, however, it is only necessary to notice the fact. - 1748, May 22.

Experientiae Spirituales 2051 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2051. Quod spiritus quoque odores producere possint

Aliquoties acciderat, quod spiritus odores, tanquam objecta odorum adessent, producerent, de quibus, ut reor, prius passim [323, 791, 1044-1047]; hodie, dum sermo erat de floribus et liliis, producebant florum seu liliorum odorem, satis vivum, quod solum annotare licet. 1748, 22 Maj.
