Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2982

2982. Moreover spirits in one place may each be in a different realm, for contact by location is of no effect, as in the other life, so neither in this one. This was likewise shown.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2982

2982. Besides that a spirit [may be] in one place, everyone can be in another sphere: for conjunction by means, of places effects nothing, no more in this life, than in the other life. This was also shown.

Experientiae Spirituales 2982 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2982. Praeterea spiritus in uno loco, quisque potest esse in sphaera alia nam conjunctio per loca, nihil efficit, sicut in altera vita, nec in hac: quod etiam ostensum est.
