Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5136

5136. About heaven

In heaven there are lands, mountains, rivers, and also a land of Canaan. Where the most upright spirits are, these flow in there out of heaven [from] the Lord, through the instrumentality of angels, when the Word is read. All things there are representatives of heavenly and spiritual things, as in the Word.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5136

5136. CONCERNING HEAVEN.

In heaven, there are lands, mountains and rivers; and there is, likewise, [in the world of spirits] a land of Canaan, where the most upright spirits are. Such [correspondences] inflow thither out of heaven [from] the Lord, by means of the angels, when the Word is read. All things there are representatives of celestial and spiritual things, as in the Word.

Experientiae Spirituales 5136 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5136. De coelo

In coelo sunt terrae, montes, fluvii, et quoque terra Canaan, ubi probissimi spiritus, illuc influunt e coelo, [a] Domino, per angelos, cum legitur Verbum, omnia ibi sunt repraesentativa coelestium et spiritualium, sicut in Verbo.
