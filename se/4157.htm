Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4157

4157. About a certain female resuscitated

A certain female acquaintance of mine while she was alive was in the other life, and I spoke with her about 4 or 5 weeks I believe after her death, and she was just like others. 1749, 1 Mar. 1

Footnotes:

1. Astrological symbol of Mercury, meaning Wednesday.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4157

4157. CONCERNING A CERTAIN FEMALE RESUSCITATED.

A certain female, known to me while she lived, was in the other life, and I spoke with her about four or five weeks, if I mistake not, after her decease. Her condition was like that of others. - 1749, March 1.

Experientiae Spirituales 4157 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4157. De quadam resuscitata

Quaedam mihi cum vixit, cognita, in altera vita fuit, et cum illa loquutus post mortem ejus circiter 4 vel 5 septimanarum, ut reor, talis sicut alii. 1749, 1 Mart.
