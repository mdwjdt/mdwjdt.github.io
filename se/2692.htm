Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2692

2692. When the bloody sphere appeared, soon there were seen what looked like little pieces of torn clothing, which made up the sphere, so that the sphere consisted of clothing torn up into tiny shreds.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2692

2692. When the bloody sphere appeared, immediately were also seen in the sphere, as it were particles of rent garments, which constituted the sphere, so that the sphere was [made up] of rent garments in minute [pieces].
