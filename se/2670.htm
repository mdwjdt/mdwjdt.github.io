Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2670

2670. [To understand] that each one contributes to the general idea or action, it must be known that they function like the muscular fibers, some from closest by, some being farther away, thus in a series, as may be well known. 1748, 23 July.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2670

2670. But that everyone contributes to the common idea or action, it should be known that they are circumstanced like the muscular fiber; some nearest, some more remote, consequently in a series as may be known. - 1748, July 23.

Experientiae Spirituales 2670 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2670. Quod autem unusquisque contribuat ad ideam communem, seu actionem, sciendum quod se habeant sicut fibrae musculi, quidam proxime, quidam remotiores, sic in serie, ut notum esse potest. 1748, 23 Julius.
