Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2534

2534. About Inward Spirits

There is a very great multitude of inward spirits who influence, and through whom angels influence, those who are outward spirits.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2534

2534. CONCERNING INTERIOR SPIRITS.

There is the greatest multitude of interior spirits who inflow, and by, [through] whom angels inflow into those who are exterior spirits.

Experientiae Spirituales 2534 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2534. De Spiritibus Interioribus

Spirituum interiorum maxima multitudo est, qui influunt, et per quos influunt angeli in eos qui spiritus exteriores sunt.
