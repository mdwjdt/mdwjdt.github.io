Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2285

2285. Personal things cannot be raised up into the heavens, nor be made conscious to them

"Personal things" are the words of each language, they are the names of men, of cities and the like, in the Word of the Lord. Because these are extremely limited and therefore not at all compatible with their mental imagery, they can never be raised up into the heavens, but only the things they symbolize.

Nor is the speech of angels one of words, of names, and such, which are personal specifics, and it is not understandable even to spirits, because it contains only the realities that are wrapped up in the speech of words. And the angels could not understand me speaking, except through intermediate spirits, by whom the mental images of personal specifics were transformed into universal images of the things symbolized.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2285

2285. THAT PARTICULARS CAN BY NO MEANS BE FETCHED [auferri] INTO THE HEAVENS, NOR BE UNDERSTOOD BY THEM.

Particulars are the words of every tongue, are the names of men, cities, and the like, which are in the Word of the Lord. These can by no means be fetched into the heavens, for they are the most finite things, therefore not at all suited to their ideas; wherefore only the significations of things [are thus suited]. The speech of angels is not one of words, names, and the like, which are particulars; yea, it is unintelligible to spirits, because it only contains things which the speech of words involves. Nor could angels understand me when speaking, save through mediate spirits, by whom the ideas of particulars were translated [transferrentur] into universal [ideas] and significations of things. Nor do angels have such a memory, to wit.
