Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1371

1371. One of their punishments by purging is being wrapped up in a kind of cloth, or woolen veil, and this goes to the point where their hands, feet and body are bound, and even their face covered over so that they cannot see. Thus the whole body is wrapped up.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1371

1371. One of their punishments of vastation consists in their being rolled up in a certain cloth or woolen veil, as it were, being so rolled up therein that they are bound as to their hands, feet and body. Even their face is covered over so that they do not see. Thus their body is enveloped.

Experientiae Spirituales 1371 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1371. Poena vastationis eorum una est, quod involvantur quodam quasi panno, seu velo laneo, ac ei involvantur aliquantum, sic ut ligati sint manibus, pedibus et corpore, tum etiam facie obscurati, ut non videant, ita involvuntur secundum corpus.
