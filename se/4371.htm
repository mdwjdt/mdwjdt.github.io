Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4371

4371. About the last judgment, that mankind has now become corrupt

It was perceived and stated that today there is but a small remnant of good in the understanding. When the will part had been destroyed in the people before the flood, good was inseminated in the understanding part, and this is now beginning to perish, so that there is no faith or charity. 1749, 23 Aug. 1

Footnotes:

1. Astrological symbol of Venus, meaning Friday.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4371

4371. CONCERNING THE LAST JUDGMENT; THAT MAN HAS NOW BECOME [EXCEEDINGLY] PERVERSE.

It was perceived and said that at this day there is very little of intellectual good remaining. The voluntary principle was destroyed with the antediluvians and good was then inseminated in the intellectual part, and now this begins to perish, so that there is no faith nor charity. - 1749, August 23.

Experientiae Spirituales 4371 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4371. De ultimo judicio, quod homo nunc perversus sit

Perceptum, et dictum, quod hodie parum reliquum sit de bono intellectuali, perditum erat in antediluvianis voluntarium, inseminatum bonum in parte intellectuali, et hoc nunc incipit perire, sic nulla fides seu charitas. 1749, 23 Aug.
