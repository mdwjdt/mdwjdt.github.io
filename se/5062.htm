Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5062

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5062

5062. Those there who were the first-born of Christians, on whom it was enjoined by the law there that they should become Janissaries, and so Mohammedans, said that they were still Christians in heart; but part of them were intermediate.

Experientiae Spirituales 5062 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5062. 1

Illi qui ibi erant primogeniti Chrisitanorum quibus ex lege ibi injunctum est ut Janitzares fierent, et sic Mahumedani, dicebant quod usque corde Christiani essent, sed pars eorum erant intermedii.
