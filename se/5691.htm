Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5691

5691. Most are also periodically let into inward [qualities] and into their outward ones, by means of which a conjunction and a separation takes place.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5691

5691. The majority, also, are let into their interiors and into their externals alternately; and by this means the conjunction and the separation take place.

Experientiae Spirituales 5691 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5691. Plerique etiam per vices mittuntur in interiora et in externa, per quod fit conjunctio et separatio.
