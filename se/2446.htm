Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2446

2446. The nude one symbolizes the regenerated person, or the regenerated person is portrayed by a nude human being, such as Adam and his Wife [Gen. 2:25]. 1748, 29 June.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2446

2446. The naked man signifies the regenerated man, or the regenerated man is represented by the naked man, like as Adam and his wife [represented the same]. - 1748, June 29.

Experientiae Spirituales 2446 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2446. Nudus homo significat hominem regeneratum, seu homo regeneratus repraesentatur per hominem nudum, sicut Adamus et ejus Uxor [Gen. II: 25]. 1748, 29 Junius.
