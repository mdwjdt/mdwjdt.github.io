Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5395

5395. The German Protestants appear more toward the north, the Swedes in the middle of the north and west, the Danes to the west, the Dutch toward the east and south, the English in the middle between them. All appear according to their nature as regards religion.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5395

5395. The German Protestants appear more towards the north; the Swedes, at the north and west, in the middle; the Danes, in the west; the Dutch, towards the east and south; the English, in the midst amongst them. All appear according to their genius as regards religion.

Experientiae Spirituales 5395 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5395. Germani Protestantes apparent versus septentrionem magis; Sveri ad septentrionem et occidentem in medio, Dani ad occidentem; Hollandi versus orientem et meridiem, Angli in medio inter illos, omnes apparent secundum genium quoad religionem.
