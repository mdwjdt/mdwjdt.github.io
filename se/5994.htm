Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5994

5994. About whether there is peace in the world

I saw a long line of triumphal chariots along a road. It was said that peace has been made on earth now. This was seen on 12 June [1760] in a state of bodily wakefulness.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5994

5994. PEACE IN THE WORLD: WOULD THAT IT MAY BE!

I saw chariots in a long train along a road. It was stated that peace has now been made on earth. This was seen on the 12th day of June [1760] in bodily wakefulness.

Experientiae Spirituales 5994 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5994. De pace in mundo si sit

Vidi currus longa serie per viam, dicebatur quod nunc pax facta sit in terris, hoc visum die 12 Junius in vigilia corporis.
