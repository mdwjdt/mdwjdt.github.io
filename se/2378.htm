Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2378

2378. In the case of brute animals, it is different. For they live in accordance with the order of their nature, and do not have need of any spirit to be with them.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2378

2378. As respects the brute animals it is different, for they live according to the order of their nature and have no need of any spirit to be with them.

Experientiae Spirituales 2378 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2378. Quod ad bruta animalia attinet, aliud est, nam vivunt secundum ordinem suae naturae, nec opus habent aliquo spiritu, qui apud eos.
