Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5139

5139. These on the left side press down on the upper gums; from there they press heavily on the enclosed teeth. Besides this, they cause pain on the left side of the head just above the forehead.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5139

5139. These press down on the upper gums of the left side. There is a sensation of heaviness from them, in the enclosed teeth. Moreover, they inflict pain on the left side of the head, just above the forehead.

Experientiae Spirituales 5139 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5139. Incumbunt in gingivas superiores, hi in sinistram partem, gravedo inde in dentes inclusos, praeterea dolorem inferunt parti capitis sinistrae mox supra frontem.
