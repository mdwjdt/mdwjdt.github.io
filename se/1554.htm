Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1554

1554. Afterwards there issued from the pit some almost black spirits, and the pit filled up with water to the top. It was a pit just like any well on earth.

1554. From the pit there issued, as it were, black spirits, and the pit was filled with water to its mouth, it being to appearance like any other pit on the earth.
