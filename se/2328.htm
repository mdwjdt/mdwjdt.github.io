Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2328

2328. When they recalled what they had been taught in life, namely, that the Lord is the giver of power, the Lord is the giver of life, and of strength, then they affirmed that it was true, that it was a true teaching, adding that this is the way it is with faith - people know, and yet do not believe. 1748, 15 June.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2328

2328. When they remembered that they had been instructed during life, to wit, that the Lord gives power, the Lord gives life and strength, then they affirmed that it is true, and a true doctrine; adding that it is so with faith, that they know and yet do not believe. - 1748, June 15.

Experientiae Spirituales 2328 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2328. Cum recordati sunt, quod ii instructi in vita, nempe quod Dominus det potentiam, Dominus det vitam, et vires, tunc affirmabant, quod verum, et quod vera doctrina, addentes, quod ita se habeat cum fide, quod sciant, et tamen non credant. 1748, 15 Junius.
