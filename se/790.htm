Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 790

790. No one reaches the very inward regions without being prepared, so an [unprepared] soul cannot ascend, but descend. 1748, 15 February.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 790

790. No one attains to more interior things, unless he has been prepared; thus a soul cannot ascend, but it can descend. 1748, Feb. 15.

Experientiae Spirituales 790 (original Latin 1748-1764)

790. Nemo ad intimiora pervenit, nisi praeparatus, ita non ascendere potest anima, sed descendere. 1748, 15 Febr.
