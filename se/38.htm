Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 38

38. 1Regeneration takes place in every individual case in the same way as it does collectively, namely in the Church, in the world of spirits, in heaven: there is a continual combat of inner elements with outward ones, thus of the Lord's angels with the spirits controlling the person's outer elements. This happens in different ways, depending on the nature of each individual, and their various states. See also Temptation. [Regeneration; Combat]

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 38

38. That there is a continual combat of internals with externals, thus of the angels of God Messiah with the spirits who rule man's externals, and this with all variety according to the nature of each man in his various states. (Pugna, Regeneratio.)
