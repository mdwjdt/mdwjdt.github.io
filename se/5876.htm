Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5876

5876. For a long time they were allowed to cross over through high places for the reason that all want to climb up high, but after this it was not granted to do this except to certain ones through whom societies are to be purified.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5876

5876. It was, for a long time, permitted them to cross over by the high places, for the reason that they all want to mount on high; but it was not so allowed afterwards, save to some through whom societies are to be purified.

Experientiae Spirituales 5876 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5876. Permissum diu ut transirent per edita loca, ex causa quia omnes volunt scandere in altum, sed postea non ita conceditur, nisi quibusdam per quos purificandae sunt societates.
