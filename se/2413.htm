Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2413

2413. In this man there was mercy, as I observed while reading of the deeds of the children of Dan at Laish. Consequently, mercy could be bestowed on him in all things down to the very least, and he could be among angels. So when the graven image was absent from his memory, I observed that he was able to worship the true Lord, otherwise than very many Christians.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2413

2413. There was pity in him [apud eum], which was observed when I read those things that were done by the sons of Dan, in Laish, so that pity could have been given him in all and singular things, and thus he could be amidst the angels. Therefore, when the graven image was absent from his memory, that he could thus worship the true Lord, differently from very many Christians.

Experientiae Spirituales 2413 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2413. Apud eum erat misericordia, quae observata, dum legerentur ea quae facta a filiis Danis in Laisch, sic ut ei misericordia in omnibus et singularibus dari potuit, et sic inter angelos esse, sic cum ex memoria ejus, sculptile abesset, [observatum] quod sic verum Dominum adorare potuit, secus ac Christiani perplures.
