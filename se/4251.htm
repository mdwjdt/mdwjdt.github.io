Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4251

4251. When I awoke, I thought about that wakefulness, and I desired to come into the state of that wakefulness, for then a person is ruled by the Lord more inwardly.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4251

4251. When awakened I reflected upon that state of wakefulness and desired to come into it; for it is thus that the interior man is governed of the Lord.

Experientiae Spirituales 4251 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4251. Cum evigilatus cogitavi de illa vigilia, optavi venire in illius vigiliae statum; nam sic interius homo a Domino regitur.
