《灵界经历》 第37节

037.个体的重生方式和总体，即教会、灵人界、天堂的重生方式是一样的，即：内在与外在不断争战，因而主的天使与控制这个人外在的灵人不断争战。这一切的发生因各人的性质及其各种状态而各异。

Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 37

37. 1Regeneration takes place in every individual case in the same way as it does collectively, namely in the Church, in the world of spirits, in heaven: there is a continual combat of inner elements with outward ones, thus of the Lord's angels with the spirits controlling the person's outer elements. This happens in different ways, depending on the nature of each individual, and their various states. See also Temptation. [Regeneration; Combat]

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 37

37. [CONCERNING REGENERATION]

That regeneration is the same in every particular as it is in the general, namely, in the Church, in the world of spirits, in heaven.
