Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3776

3776. That they reject the doctrinal matters of faith, and the Lord, became evident also on the street by means of a cane, with which they wanted to strike [my eye], symbolizing the fact that they utterly reject and hate the doctrinal tenets of faith concerning the Lord. 1748, 30 Oct.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3776

3776. That they reject the doctrinals of faith, and the Lord, appeared also in an open manner by means of a staff, with which they would strike the eye, which signifies that they wholly reject and hold in hatred the doctrinals of faith concerning the Lord. 1748, October 30.

Experientiae Spirituales 3776 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3776. Quod doctrinalia fidei, et Dominum rejiciant, constabat quoque in platea, per baculum, quo ferire oculum vellent, quod significat eos prorsus rejicere et odio habere doctrinalia fidei de Domino. 1748, 30 Oct.
