Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 765

765. About the year 1657

In a vision, I was shown 57, or 1657, the numbers being written before my eyes; but what they mean, I do not know exactly. 1748, 13 February. 1

Footnotes:

1. J.F.I. Tafel, first Latin editor of this text, wrote: "Why not 1757?" (the year of the Last Judgment).

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 765

765. CONCERNING THE YEAR 1657

There was shown me in vision the number 57, or 1657. The numbers were written before my eyes, but what they signify I do not clearly know. 1748, Feb. 13.
