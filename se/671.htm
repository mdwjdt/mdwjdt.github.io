671. About spiritual love

671. CONCERNING SPIRITUAL LOVE

Moreover, there is also a spiritual love, but in what way it is distinguished from celestial love, is not yet evident to me. 1748, Feb. 7.

671. De amore spirituali

Praeterea etiam datur amor spiritualis, sed qualiter discriminatur ab amore coelesti, nondum mihi constat. 1748, 7 Febr.
