Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3170

3170. He suffered the grievous punishment of dismemberment as to the whole head. Putting up resistance, he delayed for a long time in coming to his right mind, while others were looking on, but I did not see it. 1748, 15 Sept.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3170

3170. He suffered the grievous punishment of discerption as to the entire [universum] head. Resistance being induced, and repentance long delayed; others beheld [it], but I did not see it. - 1748, September 15.

Experientiae Spirituales 3170 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3170. is discerptionis poenam gravem subiit, quoad 1

universum caput, inducto renisu, et remorata diu resipiscentia, spectantibus aliis, ego vero non vidi. 1748, 15 Sept.
