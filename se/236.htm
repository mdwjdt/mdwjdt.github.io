《灵界经历》 第236节

236.我从内在被告知，弥赛亚神允许世上有很多这种和类似的事，因为一生中充满这类事物的正直和纯真的灵魂坚持要它们；他们只感知其中的纯真事物。（1747年11月1日）

Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 236

236. It was also told me from within that God the Messiah allows very many things of these and similar things in the world, for the reason that upright souls and innocents who had been brought up with such displays during their life, persist in them. For they only perceive harmless things therein. 1747, the 1st day of November (old calendar).

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 236

236. It was also said to me inwardly, that God Messiah permits many such things in the world, and others like them, for the reason that upright and innocent souls, who were imbued with such things during their life, attend, and that they only perceive innocent things. 1747, Nov. 1, o.s.

Experientiae Spirituales 236 (original Latin 1748-1764)

236. Dictum etiam intus mihi est, quod Deus Messias plurima permittat ejuscemodi in orbe terrarum, et similia, ob causam, quia probae animae ac innocentiae, quae similibus in vita imbutae sunt, insistunt, quia innocentia modo percipiunt. 1747, die 1 Nov. st. v.
