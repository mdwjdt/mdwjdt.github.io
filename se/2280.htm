Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2280

2280. At the time when I was in this condition, I could hear spirits speaking, but still, as if they were separate. For they said, not knowing whether I was there too, that I was absent, and that I was therefore taking leave of them. 1748, 11 June.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2280

2280. During the time that I was in such a state [tempore quo talis eram], I could hear spirits speaking, but yet they were as if separated, for they said that I was absent, being ignorant whether I was there together [with them], and that thus I departed from them. - 1748, June 11.

Experientiae Spirituales 2280 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2280. Tempore quo talis eram, spiritus loquentes audire potui, sed usque sicut separati essent, dicebant enim, quod abessem, nescientes num ibi simul essem, et quod sic discederem ab iis. 1748, 11 Junius.
