Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 752

752. Now the line drawn hence is the boundary, which, running to each city, and from one city to the other, forms a triangle within the space of which the robbers are.

752. From there, now, is the boundary from which a line drawn to each city, and from one city to the other, forms a triangular space within which are the robbers.
