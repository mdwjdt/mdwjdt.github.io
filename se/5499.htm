Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5499

5499. I saw them afterwards transferred further away in the same northern region, up to several hundred, and thus hidden away there in hell, where they lie half-dead and after ages are awakened and wander about like utterly mindless fools.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5499

5499. I saw them, afterwards, being transferred to a part farther removed in the same quarter, in the north, to the number of some hundreds, and thus hidden away there in hell, where they lie half-dead, and, ages after, are called forth and wander about like fools, totally destitute of understanding.

Experientiae Spirituales 5499 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5499. Vidi illos postea transferri remotius in eadem plaga septentrionali quoad aliquot centum, et sic ibi recondi in inferno, ubi jacent demimortui, et post saecula excitantur et vagantur sicut fatui absque ullo intellectu.
