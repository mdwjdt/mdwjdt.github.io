Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 812

812. The former ones [810], who are obstinate, or sticky, have been allotted a place before the face, a little to the left, near to the upper region, but not high up.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 812

812. The former spirits, who are contumacious or sticky, are allotted a place before the face, a little towards the left, near the upper part, but not on high.

Experientiae Spirituales 812 (original Latin 1748-1764)

812. Illi qui contumaces seu viscosi sunt, ante faciem sunt locum sortiti, paulo sinistrorsum, paene ad partem superiorem sed non alte.
