Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3844

3844. They flow into lower mediums who are present with people on earth, and these mediums know no better than that they are the person. But into them flow those who are overhead.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3844

3844. These flow into inferior subjects, which are with man, and the subjects know no otherwise than that they are themselves the man, but they who are above the head flow into them.

Experientiae Spirituales 3844 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3844. Influunt ii in subjecta inferiora, quae apud hominem, et subjecta non aliter sciunt, quam quod ii sint qui homo, sed in eos influunt ii qui supra caput sunt.
