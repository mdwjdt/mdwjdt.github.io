Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3293

3293. About a Light

Certain spirits, after the Lord was seen, were let down toward the regions in front to the right, and as they went forward they said they were seeing a Light, much purer than they had ever seen, and they were many, saying they had never seen greater light - and this was when it was here the time of evening. 1748, 23 Sept.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3293

3293. CONCERNING A LIGHT.

Certain [spirits] after the Lord was seen, were let down, towards the front [regions] on the right, and as they progressed, said that they saw a light, much purer than they had ever seen, and so said many; that they have never seen greater light; and this, when here, it was evening-time. - 1748, September 23.)

Experientiae Spirituales 3293 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3293. De Luce

Quidam, postquam visus Dominus, versus anteriora dextrorsum demissi, et cum progressi dicebant quod viderent Lucem, multo puriorem, quam usquam viderant, et hoc plures, dicendo quod majorem lucem nusquam viderint: et hoc cum hic esset tempus vesperae. 1748, 23 Sept.
