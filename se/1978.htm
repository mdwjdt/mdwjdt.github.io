Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1978

1978. What punishment the association, or mixing up, of mental images of holiness with profane acts carries with it, anyone can guess, for it is the combinations of images that form the mind. 1748, 16 May.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1978

1978. What kind of punishment this confusion and commixtion of holy and profane ideas draws after it everyone may divine, for conjunctions of ideas form the mind. - 1748, May 16.

Experientiae Spirituales 1978 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1978. Quam confusio idearum sanctitatis cum profanis, sic commixtio, poenam secum habeat, quisque potest augurari, nam idearum conjunctiones mentem formant. 1748, 16 Maj.
