《灵界经历》 第238节

238.空洞的思维无法上升高过第三层或最后一层天堂，因为如经上所说的基路伯在那里，它们进行守卫，将那些虚假和邪恶的东西转化为真理和良善，最终转化为构成至内层天堂的纯真。（1747年11月3日）

Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 238

238. Empty thoughts cannot rise up higher than to the third heaven, 1for there are Cherubim, as they are called [cf. Gen. 3:24], who keep guard, and who turn those things which are false and evil into truth and goodness, and at last into innocencies, of which the innermost heaven consists. 1747, the 3rd day of November (old calendar).

Footnotes:

1. I.e. the last, or third in descending order.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 238

238. Vain thoughts cannot rise higher than into "the third heaven": for the Cherubim, as they are called, are there, and they defend, and turn those things that are false and evil into truths and goods, and at length into the innocences of which the inmost heaven consists. 1947, Nov. 3, o.s.

238. Cogitationes vanae non possunt altius surgere quam in tertium 1
