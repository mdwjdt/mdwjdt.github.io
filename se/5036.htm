Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5036

5036. The good, on the other hand, are assembled in another city, which is like Gothenburg. In its middle are those who, in spite of all this, were honest in life; in the beginning they live for the most part in houses similar to those in which they were in life, but after this their houses are varied according to the changes of the state of their life.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5036

5036. The good, however, are associated in another city, which is like Gothenburg, in the midst of which are those who were uniformly honest in life. At first, they generally live in houses similar to those they had lived in during life; but they are changed afterwards, according to the changes of the state of their life.

Experientiae Spirituales 5036 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5036. At boni consociati sunt in alia urbe, quae similis est Gothoburgo, in cujus medio sunt qui usque sinceri fuerunt in vita, habitant plerumque in similibus domibus, in quibus in vita, principio, sed variantur postea, secundum mutationes status vitae eorum.
