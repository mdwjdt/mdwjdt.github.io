Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5771

5771. However it should be known that some speak more fully from mental images, some less, and this is according to their thoughts about the thing about which they are speaking.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5771

5771. It must be known, however, that some speak from ideas more fully, others less fully; and this according to their thoughts on the matter about which they are speaking.

Experientiae Spirituales 5771 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5771. Sed sciendum est, quod aliqui loquantur plenius ex ideis, aliqui minus plene, et hoc secundum eorum cognitiones de re de qua loquuntur.
