Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2152

2152. They were also told, and they even saw and can now see, by a spiritual mental image, that if they were given the liberty or freedom to act according to their desire, all things would perish, for their passion would grow beyond measure, so that they would rush toward the ruin of all.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2152

2152. It was also told them, what they also perceived in spiritual idea, and can now perceive, that should liberty or license to act according to their desires be granted them, all things would perish, for it would increase to an immense degree, so that they would rush into the destroying of all things.

Experientiae Spirituales 2152 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2152. Iis quoque dictum est, quod idea spirituali etiam perceperunt, et percipere nunc possunt, si iis daretur libertas seu licentia agendi secundum eorum cupiditatem, quod perirent omnia, nam in immensum cresceret, sic ut ruerent in ruinam omnium.
