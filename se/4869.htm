Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4869

4869. Spirits are able to put their speech into writing on paper, on which what is written appears in the common style, and also as it does in printed type. I have often seen such written documents and have been able to read but not understand them although the spirits with me said they could understand everything there because they were in their language.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4869

4869. Spirits can commit their speech to writings, wherein appear compositions in the ordinary style, and, also, just as are usual in printed publications. I have often seen such writings, and could read but not understand them, although a spirit with me said that he could understand all things there, because they are according to their speech.

Experientiae Spirituales 4869 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4869. Spiritus loquelam suam possunt mandare chartis, in quibus apparent stylo vulgari scripta, et quoque sicut solent typis vulgata; tales scripturas saepius vidi, et potui legere, sed non intelligere, tametsi spiritus apud me dixit se posse omnia ibi intelligere, quia sunt secundum loquelam eorum.
