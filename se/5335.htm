Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5335

5335. It was noticed that some who lived near to the wealthy city in the southern region had something like a millstone around their left arm, with the arm thrust into its center, which was a symbol that from the Word they had produced support for their atrocious dogmas. A millstone is such doctrinal teaching.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5335

5335. It was also observed, that some who dwelt nearer to the city of the rich, in the southern tract, had, as it were, a millstone around the left shoulder, the shoulder being inserted in the middle of it. This was a representative that they had confirmed their execrable dogmas from the Word. A millstone is such a doctrinal.

Experientiae Spirituales 5335 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5335. Observatum etiam est, quod quidam qui propius ad urbem divitem habitarent in tractu meridionali haberent sicut lapidem molarem circum brachium sinistrum, brachio inserto in medium ejus, quod erat repraesentativum quod illi ex Verbo confirmaverint nefanda sua dogmata, lapis molaris est doctrinale tale.
