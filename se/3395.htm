Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3395

3395. About the aura of persuasion

Again I was shown that the aura of persuasion spread about by those evil ones from the ancient Church, [was] measured by a sky blue band, [and I was shown] how it disappears. [See 3379.]

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3395

3395. CONCERNING THE SPHERE OF PERSUASION.

It was again shown me that the sphere of persuasions diffused by those who [are] of the evil [ones] of the Ancient Church, was measured as to its disappearance by a perpendicular bundle of blue color.

Experientiae Spirituales 3395 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3395. De persuasionis sphaera

Iterum ostensum mihi, quod sphaera persuasionum, quae diffusa ab iis, qui ex antiquae Ecclesiae malis, mensurata per fasciam perpendicularem coeruleam, quomodo 1

ea disparet. [Vide 3379.]
