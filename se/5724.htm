Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5724

5724. Those who are part of the dragon but who are from the Catholic religion are the beast of the sea and of the land, spoken of in Revelation [13:1, 11].

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5724

5724. These are of the dragon; but those who are from the Catholic religion are the beast 1of the sea and of the earth, which are mentioned in the Apocalypse.

Footnotes:

1. See SS 25.

5729a. 1THE MOGUL: ALSO THE LAST JUDGMENT.

The Mongols are at the southern side beyond the Christian region. They are haughty, and desire to be distinguished above all others. They are also hostile to Christians. I saw that they were likewise cast down into hells. There were many mountains there, on which they dwell; and they were cast down. The part sank. Those from some of them were cast down into the hells: those on others, to certain places in the desert. They are haughty on account of being rich. There is a diamond mine with them; also, gold with them. All those underwent such judgment who think only about Mohammed and little about God; as all there do who are in the love of self.

1. There are no nos. 5725-5729 in the original. -TR.
