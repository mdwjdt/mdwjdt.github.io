Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5512

5512. Also, before they were thrown out of the mountains I saw that first they were put in an order in this way, that is to say, arranged, and that after this they were thrown out to similar regions.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5512

5512. I saw, also, before they were cast out from the mountains, that they were, at first, thus arranged in order, or placed; and that they were afterwards cast out to corresponding positions.

Experientiae Spirituales 5512 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5512. Etiam vidi antequam ejicerentur ex montibus, quod primum ita ordinarentur seu disponerentur, et quod dein ad similes plagas ejicerentur.
