Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3400

3400. About spirits of a different earth

About the astonishing deeds of a certain kind of spirits who were unknown

There appeared to me a spirit a little toward the front up above in bed, who also appeared toward the back up above, for he was able to change locations, and it was shown then from many changes - in fact, he was changing the location of almost all in the realm.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3400

3400. CONCERNING SPIRITS OF ANOTHER EARTH. -CONCERNING THE STUPENDOUS DEEDS OF A CERTAIN KIND OF SPIRITS WHO WERE UNKNOWN.

There appeared to me a spirit, a little in front, above, in bed: who also appeared behind, above, for he could change his situation, and it was then shown from many things: yea, he changed the situation of nearly all in the sphere.

Experientiae Spirituales 3400 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3400. De spiritibus alius telluris

De stupendis factis generis cujusdam spirituum, qui incogniti

Apparuit mihi spiritus paulo antrorsum superius in lecto, qui etiam apparuit retrorsum superius, nam mutare situs potuit, et ostensum dein ex pluribus, imo mutabat situm omnium fere in sphaera.
