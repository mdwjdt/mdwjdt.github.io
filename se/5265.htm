Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5265

5265. There is a similar chasm on the northern side of the Mohammedan desert. Into it were thrown the worst of them as well as those from the Catholic religion who worshiped idols of their saints and thought nothing about the Lord. They were mingled with the gentiles and thrown into it.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5265

5265. There is also a similar chasm on the northern side of the Mohammedan desert. Thither were cast their worst ones; also, those of the Catholic religion who worshipped images of the saints and thought nothing about the Lord. These were mingled with the Gentiles and cast thither.

Experientiae Spirituales 5265 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5265. Est etiam similis hiatus a latere septentrionali deserti Mahumedani, illuc conjecti sunt pessimi: etiam qui e Catholica religione coluerunt idola sanctorum et nihil cogitarunt de Domino, illi commixti sunt cum gentilibus et illuc conjecti.
