Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1779

1779. One may also conclude from the above regarding inward convictions from the Lord, that one becomes convinced in one's belief and the secrets of faith by no means from one's own power, but from the Lord. 1748, 29 March.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1779

1779. Hence also it is given to conclude respecting interior persuasions from the Lord, that men are persuaded in faith, and concerning the thing of faith, from the Lord, and by no means from themselves. - 1748, March 29.

Experientiae Spirituales 1779 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1779. Exinde quoque concludere datur de persuasionibus interioribus, a Domino, quod persuadeatur in fide et de fidei arcanis, nequaquam ex semet, sed ex Domino. 1748, 29 Martius.
