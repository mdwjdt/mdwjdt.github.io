Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4965

4965. Below the mount of assembly and also above it watchtowers are seen as well. In these are those who look all around and also govern, as do those who are around on the sides.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4965

4965. Below the Mount of Assembly, and likewise above it, appear also watch-towers, in which are those who watch everywhere around, and also rule, like those who are around at the sides.

Experientiae Spirituales 4965 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4965. Infra montem conventus et quoque supra illum apparent etiam turres speculatoriae, in quibus sunt qui spectant circumquaque et quoque regunt sicut illi qui circum ad latera.
