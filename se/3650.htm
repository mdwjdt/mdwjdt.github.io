Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3650

3650. Furthermore, he declared his belief that he had been holy from birth, and because he became a pontiff he had had to be born holy. This idea he derived from their being called "holiness" [Ex. 28:36, 39:30].

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3650

3650. He said, farther, that he believed he was holy from his nativity, and that one made pontiff ought to be born thus holy; this idea he had contracted from their being called [each his] holiness.

Experientiae Spirituales 3650 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3650. Dixit porro, quod crederet se sanctum fuisse a nativitate, et quia pontifex factus, quod ita sanctus nasci debuit, quam ideam traxit ex eo, quod "Sanctitas" vocantur [Exod. XXVIII: 36, XXXIX: 30].
