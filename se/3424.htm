Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3424

3424. About lice

Lice especially of the household variety, which emit a foul smell, are those who secretly assail everything pertaining to faith, also secretly distorting those who are unable to know better, by various methods. On the way.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3424

3424. CONCERNING LICE.

(They who secretly assail the things which belong to faith, and secretly pervert in various modes those who cannot know better, are lice, especially the domestic sort, which have a foul smell. In the way.)

Experientiae Spirituales 3424 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3424. De pediculis

Pediculi imprimis domestici, qui foede olent, sunt ii qui clandestine aggrediuntur ea, quae sunt fidei, tum clandestine pervertunt eos, qui non meliora scire possunt, variis modis. In via.
