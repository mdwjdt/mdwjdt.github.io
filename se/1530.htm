Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1530

1530. When they die, they say that their bodies are thrown away, and they do not worry about being buried, because they say that what is being thrown away is filthy; and that it is their life, which was in the body, that becomes an angel.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1530

1530. They say that when they die their bodies are cast forth. They are not concerned with burial, for they say that what is cast away is something filthy; and what was within the body, and becomes an angel, is their life.

Experientiae Spirituales 1530 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1530. Quum moriuntur, dicunt, quod projiciantur corpora eorum, nec curant sepeliri, quia dicunt hoc spurcum esse, quod projicitur, et quod vita eorum sit, quae in corpore, et fit angelus.
