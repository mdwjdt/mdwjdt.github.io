Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5637

5637. The gang which was around [them], who were of the same nature but not exercising control because they were lower in rank, were let down behind the mountain on the southern side and hurled into quagmires depending on their evils.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5637

5637. Those of the surrounding crew who were of the same genius, but did not rule because they were meaner, were taken down behind the mountain at the southern side, and cast into gulfs according to their evils.

Experientiae Spirituales 5637 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5637. Turba quae circum erat, qui ejusdem genii erant, sed non imperabant, quia viliores, illi post montem a parte meridionali delati, et conjecti in voragines secundum eorum mala.
