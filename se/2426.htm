Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2426

2426. Still, by generations and families, individuals are more easily drawn and more prone to one kind or one species of evil because it has prevailed in the parents, as is well known to everyone. However, they can still be bent toward a different kind or species of evil.

2426. Still they are more prone and ready for one genus or one species of evil, because it has prevailed in parents. This may be sufficiently known to every one, but still [a man] can be bent to a genus or species of another evil.
