Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2265

2265. About the wisdom of spirits, especially of angels, and about the Inspiration of the Lord's Word

Besides the gifts with which souls after death are endowed, spoken of here and there [see 400, 2252], when the {w219} gives it, there is also the gift of being able find out what elements a mental image contains. They scan the scenes streaming into the thought thereby in very clear light what elements of falsity it has what of truth, also what elements of evil what of good.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2265

2265. CONCERNING THE WISDOM OF SPIRITS, ESPECIALLY OF ANGELS, AND CONCERNING THE INSPIRATION OF THE LORD'S WORD.

Besides the gifts with which souls are endowed after death, of which [I have treated] here and there, they are also able to know when the Lord grants, what is in a idea; for they perceive the varieties which inflow, and hence see as in clearer light, what [there is] of the false, and what of the true, as well as what evil and what good.

Experientiae Spirituales 2265 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2265. De spirituum, cumprimis angelorum sapientia, deque Inspiratione Verbi Domini

Praeter dotes, quibus donantur animae post mortem, de quibus passim [vide 400, 2252], etiam dum Dominus donat, scire possunt quid in idea sint 1

, nam percipiunt varietates, quae influunt, et inde sicut in luce clariore vident, quid falsi et quid veri, tum quid mali et quid boni.
