Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5336

5336. So Babylon has been purged and entirely destroyed on three sides, the south, the west and the north. This began at the end of the year 1756 and beginning of the year 1757; yesterday and today, these three regions, today is the 6th day of January, 1757.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5336

5336. Thus was Babylon devastated and utterly destroyed on three sides, the southern, the western, and the northern. This commenced at the end of the year 1756 and the beginning of the year 1757. Yesterday and today those three tracts [were fully destroyed]. Today is the 6th day of January, 1757.

Experientiae Spirituales 5336 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5336. Ita devastata et prorsus destructa est Babylonia a tribus lateribus meridionali, occidentali, et septentrionali, hoc inceptum fine anni 1756, et principio anni 1757, heri et hodie hi tres tractus, hodie die 6 Jan. 1757.
