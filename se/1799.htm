Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1799

1799. The second is the kind that pools together in the mammillary processes. This fluid collects from the interiors of the brain, from within its tissues, and is likewise steered toward the sieve-like layer.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1799

1799. Another kind is that which stagnates in the mammillary processes. This is a humour collected from the interiors of the brain, or from within its fibers, and is in like manner determined towards the laminam cribrosam.

Experientiae Spirituales 1799 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1799. Alterum genus est, quod in processibus mamillaribus stagnat, quod est humor collectus ab interioribus cerebri, seu ab intra fibrarum ejus, et similiter determinatur versus laminam cibrosam.
