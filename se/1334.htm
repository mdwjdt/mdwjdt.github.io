Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1334

1334. But there are differences. There are also some who desire clothing not so beautiful, but very durable. These look to life eternal, although a similar self-righteousness clings to them.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1334

1334. But there is this difference: There are also some who desire garments that are not so beautiful but more durable. These spirits look to eternal life although a like desire also adheres.
