Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4559

4559. I also felt a pressure and pain on the top of my head on my skull, directly on the upper part above the teeth. This also came from them, because hypocrites strike pain into the bones of the skull themselves.

4559. I also perceived a pressure and pain, on the head, in the skull, just above the part those teeth were below. This also was from thence; for hypocrites inflict pain on the very bones of the skull.
