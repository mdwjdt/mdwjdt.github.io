Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4776

4776. These with whom there was a conversation about the doctrine from heaven and about the Bible were a little to the right of the former, as has been said, and I perceived that they were in the entrance to Africa.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4776

4776. The ones with whom took place the conversation concerning doctrine from heaven, and concerning a Bible, were a little to the right of the former, as was said; and it was perceived that they were in the entrance to Africa.

Experientiae Spirituales 4776 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4776. Hi cum quibus sermo fuit de doctrina e coelo, et de Bibliis, erant ad dextrum paulo a prioribus, ut dictum est, et perceptum quod essent in introitu ad Africam.
