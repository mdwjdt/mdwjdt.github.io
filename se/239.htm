239.何为真正的信仰，它如何影响弥赛亚神的天堂。从这些事明显看出何为真正的信仰，以及它有什么样的影响，即：它影响弥赛亚神的天堂，也就是天使，甚至直达纯真天堂；如果信仰里面有纯真，它甚至能进入天堂本身，因而直达弥赛亚神那里。（1747年11月3日）

Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 239

239. What true belief is, how it touches the heavens of God the Messiah

From the above it can be evident what true belief is, and what effect it has, namely that it touches the heaven of God the Messiah, that is, the angels, and reaches even to the heaven of innocence, and if it is an innocent believing, even passes into that heaven itself, and thus to God the Messiah. 1747, the 3rd day of November (old calendar).

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 239

239. WHAT TRUE FAITH IS HOW IT AFFECTS THE HEAVENS OF GOD MESSIAH

From these things it can be manifest what true faith is, and what effect it has, namely, that it affects the heaven of God Messiah, that is, the angels, and reaches even to the heaven of innocence: and if in the faith there is innocence, it penetrates even into heaven itself, and thus to God Messiah. 1747, Nov. 3, o.s.
