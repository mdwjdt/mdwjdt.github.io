Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 902

902. Such inward seeing and conviction can never be obtained without belief in the Lord; for it is His, and therefore His gift, and not one whit thereof belongs to man, soul, spirit, or angel.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 902

902. Such perceptions and persuasions can never be obtained without faith in the Lord, for they are His, consequently His gift, nor is the least thereof from man, soul, spirit, or angel.

Experientiae Spirituales 902 (original Latin 1748-1764)

902. Perceptiones et persuasiones tales nusquam obtineri possunt absque fide in Dominum, nam est Ipsius, proinde Ipsius donum, nec hilum est hominis, animae, spiritus, angeli.
