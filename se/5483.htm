Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5483

5483. In the eastern quarter, toward the north, are those who are using lies and in this way cunningly leading others astray, and so who have no religion. Toward the south there are those who have no doctrine, vacillating and believing whatever they want. They are also somewhat domineering.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5483

5483. In the eastern quarter, towards the north, are those who are in lies, and by that method cunningly seduce - thus, who are not in any religion; towards the south there, those who are not in any doctrine, vacillating and believing whatever they have a mind to: to some extent also these are domineering.

Experientiae Spirituales 5483 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5483. In plaga orientali versus septentrionem sunt illi qui in mendaciis sunt, et eo modo astute seducunt; ita qui nulla religione sunt; versus meridiem ibi, qui nulla doctrina, vacillantes, et credentes quicquid volunt, aliquantum etiam imperiosi.
