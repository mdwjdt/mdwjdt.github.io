Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5145

5145. It was said to them that they had been cold in their divine worship, but still they had done good to the poor out of a kind of obedience. They said that they had done so, and that such things happen to them on account of this.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5145

5145. They were told that they were frigid in divine worship, but that still they did good to the poor, from a certain kind of obedience. They said that they did so; and that such things befall them on that account.

Experientiae Spirituales 5145 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5145. Dictum est illis quod in cultu divino fuerint frigidi, sed usque quod fecerint pauperibus bonum ex quadam specie obedientiae, dixerunt quod ita fecerint; et quod talia illis obveniant, ex eo.
