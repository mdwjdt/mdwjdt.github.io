《灵界经历》 第101节

101.魔鬼和恶灵会为某个邪恶的目的而在一句话或一个观念中挑起凡与他们自己一致的东西；这句话或这个观念要么是从肉身生活那里收集来的，要么源于人们的同伴，要么是从各种源头编造或获得的。

善灵和天使从主行动，转移他们的努力，把这些努力折向良善；或给出一个答复。

Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 101

101. [The index refers also to nos. 100 and 101 1/2.]

Demons and evil spirits arouse for some evil purpose whatever agrees with themselves in a word or in a mental image

/acquired and put together from various sources [Mental image (Idea); Evil; Good; Words, Expressions]

/originating within different groups [Nature]

/gathered during bodily life [Spirit]

101. 1/2. [The index refers also to nos. 100 and 101.]

Good spirits and angels, [acting] from the Lord, divert their efforts and bend them toward good, or else make a reply. [Spirit; Evil; Good]

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 101

101. Whatever is in agreement with themselves in a word, or in an idea gathered from the life of the body, or originating in companies of men, or made up and acquired from various sources, they stir up for an evil end. (Bonum, Idea, Malum, Natura, Spiritus, Vox.)

101 1/2. From God Messiah, good spirits and angels take these intentions away from them, and turn and bend them into what is good; or they give an answer to them. (Bonum, Malum, Spiritus.)
