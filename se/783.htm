Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 783

783. After this souls undergo preparation, so that their earthly part harmonizes with their spiritual part, then they are let into the inward heaven. 1

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 783

783. He is afterwards prepared so that his natural may accord with his spiritual; in this way he is admitted into the interior heaven.

783. Postea praeparatur, ut naturale ejus cum spirituali concordet, sic admittitur in coelum interius.
