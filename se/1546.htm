Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1546

1546. They eat vegetables, as well as a certain round fruit that sprouts directly from the earth, and the fruits of trees, also.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1546

1546. (They subsist upon different kinds of pulse, as also upon a certain round fruit which springs up immediately from the ground, not to mention the fruits of trees.)

Experientiae Spirituales 1546 (original Latin 1748-1764)

1546. Vescuntur leguminibus, tum fructu quodam rotundo ex terra immediate pullulante, praeter fructibus arborum.
