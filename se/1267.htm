Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 1267

1267. About the Mucus of the nostrils

There are those who relate to the sticky, phlegmy excretions, that is, those that are discharged from the brain through the meninges and bundled tissues into the nostrils, and at the same time into the little glands of the nostril, and which then thicken and cling.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 1267

1267. CONCERNING THE MUCUS OF THE NOSTRILS

There are spirits who relate to the phlegmy or viscous excretions, namely, those which are excreted from the brain through the meninges and the fasciculated fibers into the nostrils, as well also through the little glands of the nostrils, which are then thickened and made more sticky.
