Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4989

4989. Continuation about the worst [magicians] from Africa, here about those from India and the East, magical things

There was one of the worst magicians (she was said to be from India) who also had communication with evil spirits from the East, from eastern India. She was with me for a long time and in fact at the back and took a place for herself there so that she could not be seen. She had an aura of a certain kind of goodness which she also magically put on by making herself naked and in this way simulating innocence.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4989

4989. CONTINUATION CONCERNING THE WORST [MAGICIANS] FROM AFRICA. HERE [ARE DESCRIBED] MAGICAL THINGS FROM INDIA AND EASTERN COUNTRIES.

There was a sorceress among the worst ones, said to be from India, who also had communication with evil characters from the east - from Eastern India. She was with me a long time, and, in fact, at the back, and there took to herself such a position that she could not be seen. She had a sphere of a certain species of good. She also clothed herself there with in a magical manner, whereby she made herself naked, and so simulated innocence.

Experientiae Spirituales 4989 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4989. Continuatio de pessimis ex Africa, hic ex India et orientalibus, magica

Erat maga inter pessimas, dicebatur ex India, quae etiam communicationem habebat cum malis ex oriente, ex India orientali; haec fuerat diu apud me, et quidem a tergo, et sibi ibi sumsit locum ut non posset videri; habebat sphaeram speciei cujusdam boni, etiam illam magice induebat per quod se nudam faceret, et sic simularet innocentiam.
