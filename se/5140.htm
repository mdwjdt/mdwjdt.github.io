Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5140

5140. Something about hell

From a certain hell under the feet I heard them wailing that in fantasy they swallowed them and threw up, and that they know no otherwise than that this is real and consequently suffer various torments, besides vicious insults.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5140

5140. SOMETHING ABOUT HELL.

I heard, from a certain hell under the feet, some lamenting, that, by means of phantasies, they are swallowed up and cast out; also, that they do not know otherwise than that it is [really] so; and so they endure various torments, besides insults.

Experientiae Spirituales 5140 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5140. Aliquid de inferno

Audivi ex inferno quodam sub pedibus, quod lamentarentur, quod per phantasias deglutirent illos, et ejicerent, et quod non aliter sciant, quam quod ita sit, et sic sustinent varios cruciatus, praeter contumelias.
