2287. [Newly arrived] souls are surprised that they are now spirits

A certain spirit quite high overhead, toward the front, just now spoke with me when he heard that I was talking about the spirit, and he said, "What is a spirit?" He thought he was a person on earth, so he wanted to know what a spirit is.
