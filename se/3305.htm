Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 3305

3305. That they have a life similar to a persuasive one, I have said quite often [cf. 3285], and now again it was being said, because something of it had been injected into someone else.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 3305

3305. That they have a life similar to the persuasive [life] I have often said; and it was now also said, inasmuch as something thereof was infused into another.

Experientiae Spirituales 3305 (original Latin 1748-1764)

3305. Quod vitam persuasivae similem habeant, saepius dixi [cf. 3285], et nunc quoque dicebatur, quia aliquid ejus infusum alii.
