Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2718

2718. On Foresight and Providence

From eternity all things even to the very least have been foreseen by the Lord, but evils have been foreseen, and goods have been provided. 1748, 31 July.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2718

2718. CONCERNING PREVIDENCE AND PROVIDENCE.

From eternity are previded and provided by the Lord, each and all things, but evils are previded and provided. - 1748, July 31.

Experientiae Spirituales 2718 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2718. De Praevidentia et Providentia

Ab aeterno praevisa sunt a Domino omnia et singula, sed praevisa sunt mala, et provisa sunt bona. 1748, 31 Julius.
