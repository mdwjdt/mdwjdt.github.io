Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4960

4960. Around that mountain from one end to the other, people, that is to say spirits, are seen. They are looking downward and by this ruling all the things that are roundabout. These who are positioned in a row in this way, who number up to the hundreds, are called watchmen and each one of them has his own role.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4960

4960. There appear around that mountain, from one border to the other, men, or spirits, who look downwards, and by this means rule all things that are around. They who are thus placed in succession, and who number as many as hundreds, are called spies; and everyone of them has his function.

Experientiae Spirituales 4960 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4960. Apparent circum montem illum homines seu spiritus ab uno fine ad alterum, qui spectant deorsum, et per id regunt omnia quae circum sunt; qui ita in serie positi sunt, qui sunt usque ad centena, vocantur speculatores, et unusquisque eorum suam functionem habent.
