Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5283

5283. Similarly, those who live in that great surrounding tract are not seen above on the mountain but below, and they descend through caves to their villages and dwellings.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5283

5283. Likewise, those who dwell in that great surrounding tract, do not appear above the mountain region, but below it; and they descend through the shaft to their villas and dwellings.

Experientiae Spirituales 5283 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5283. Similiter illi qui in magno illo tractu circum habitant, illi nec apparent supra montanum, sed infra ac descendunt per antrum ad suas villas et domicilia.
