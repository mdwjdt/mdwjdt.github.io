Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5909

5909. About the holy supper and about Luther's opinion on it

An inquiry was made as to whether any of the Roman Catholics or of Christians in general had thought about the body and blood of the Lord according to doctrinal tenets when they went to the holy supper, and all confessed that they had not thought about it but only about the holiness they received. The papists too were questioned and said the same thing, so they had not entertained this material idea when they had gone to the holy supper.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5909

5909. THE HOLY SUPPER AND LUTHER IN RELATION TO IT.

Inquiry was made whether anyone from the Popish communion, or from a Christian people, when he partook of the Holy Supper, thought of the Lord's body and blood, according to the doctrinal; and all confessed that they did not, but only about the holiness which they are receiving. Papists also were questioned, and they said similarly, namely, that when they partake of the Sacrament of the Supper, they had not that material idea.

Experientiae Spirituales 5909 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5909. De sancta coena, et de Luthero de illa

Inquisitum est, num aliquis ex gente Pontificia et ex populo Christiano, dum obivit sanctam coenam, cogitaverit de corpore et sanguine Domini secundum doctrinale, et confessi sunt omnes quod non cogitaverint, sed modo de sancto quod accipiunt, etiam pontificii interrogati, et simile dixerunt, ita quod materialem illam ideam, cum obeunt sacramentum coenae, non habuerint.
