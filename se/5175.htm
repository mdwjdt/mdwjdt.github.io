Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5175

5175. They said further that changes also take place if others come to them. They are either evil or good, which they also look into. The reason is that communication is a matter of thoughts; and such thoughts arise from the mental state produced by the will.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5175

5175. They also stated that a change likewise occurs, if others come to them, whether they are evil or good - which, also, they investigate. The reason is, because there is a communication of the thoughts; and such things exist from the intellectual state which is derived from the voluntary.

Experientiae Spirituales 5175 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5175. Dicebant etiam quod mutatio existat quoque si alii veniunt ad illos, qui vel mali sunt vel boni, quod etiam inquirunt; causa est, quia communicatio est cogitationum; et ex statu intellectuali quod ex voluntario, talia existunt.
