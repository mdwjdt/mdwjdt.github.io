Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2635

2635. Indeed, neither can spiritual things be translucent except from heavenly ones, nor are heavenly things heavenly, except from the Lord. 1748, 17 July.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2635

2635. Yea, neither can spirituals be transparent, save from celestials, nor are celestials celestial, save from the Lord. - 1748, July 17.

Experientiae Spirituales 2635 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2635. Imo nec spiritualia translucere possunt, nisi a coolestibus, nec coelestia sunt coelestia, nisi a Domino. 1748, 17 Julius.
