Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2357

2357. About the location of spirits in relation to the body

You may see discussed previously the fact that spirits are allotted places, depending on each one's quality, in relation to the human body [2334-2338]. And the places also change, but no differently than as heterogeneous elements in the body are apt to occasionally go up into the head and infest it, or into other regions or parts of the body that are not where their real place is, but from which, in a healthy body, they are cast down.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2357

2357. CONCERNING THE SITUATION OF SPIRITS WITH RESPECT TO THE BODY.

See what was before said: that spirits obtain situations in relation to the human body according to the quality of each, and also that they change situations, but not otherwise than is usual, when the heterogeneous things of the body ascend to the head and infest it, or [go] to other regions or parts of the body where is not their [proper] genuine situation, from which, nevertheless they are cast out in the healthy body.

Experientiae Spirituales 2357 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2357. De situ spirituum respective ad corpus

Dictum videas prius [2334-2338], quod spiritus secundum qualitatem cujusvis sortiantur situs, respective ad corpus humanum, et quod mutent quoque situs, sed non aliter ac solent quandoque 1

heterogenea corporis ascendere in caput, idque infestare, seu in alias corporis regiones aut partes, ubi non eorum genuinus situs, e quo tamen dejiciuntur, in sano corpore.

Footnotes:

1. The Manuscript has solent, quandoque
