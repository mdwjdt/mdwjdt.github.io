Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4995

4995. About Nerez 1and wicked revenge and about those at the back

He was in a certain city on a rock and was accepted there among the others as the governor. But he conducted all the affairs for the sake of profit, and in each and every thing was looking for profit and in various ways compelled those who were wealthy to give him money.

Footnotes:

1. Joachim Nerez (1689ֱ748), Swedish ambassador and civil servant, ennobled in 1731, later served as President of the Royal College of Commerce.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4995

4995. CONCERNING NEREZ AND EXECRABLE REVENGE, AND CONCERNING THOSE AT THE BACK.

He was in a certain city, which was upon a rock and he was accepted there by the others as governor; but he did everything for the sake of gain, and, in the whole and in every particular, had regard to profit; and, in various ways, he compelled those who were rich to give him money.

Experientiae Spirituales 4995 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4995. De Nerez, et nefanda vindicta, et de illis a tergo

Ille fuit in urbe quadam super petra, et receptus ibi inter alios ut gubernator, sed agebat omnia propter lucrum, et in omnibus et singulis spectabat lucrum, et qui divites erant adigebat vario modo ad dandum sibi pecunias.
