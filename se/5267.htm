Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5267

5267. Then I saw the whole northern valley up to the mountains there completely eradicated and all there thrown down and partially mixed in with the gentiles and others, and then something smoky was seen too.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5267

5267. Next, I saw the whole northern valley, as far as the mountains there, completely upturned, and all who were there scattered, and partly mingled with the Gentiles and others; and then there also appeared there as it were a smokiness.

5267. Dein vidi totam vallem septentrionalem usque ad montes ibi evulsam funditus, et omnes qui ibi fuerunt disjecti, et partim cum gentilibus aliisque commixti, et tunc apparuit etiam ibi sicut fumosum.
