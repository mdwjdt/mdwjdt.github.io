Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 706

706. One group of spirits who were agitating to be let in, received the reply that it was not yet time, but at another time, if they came prepared. Watching this incident were also some souls whom I had known. 1748, 10 February.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 706

706. To some spirits who were solicitous about being admitted, reply was made that it was not yet time, but at another time if they came prepared. There were also souls who had been known to me who viewed these things. 1748, Feb. 10.

Experientiae Spirituales 706 (original Latin 1748-1764)

706. Spiritibus quibusdam, qui sollicitabant, ut intromitterentur, responsum 1

dabatur, quod nondum tempus esset, sed alio tempore, si praeparati venirent, fuerant etiam animae mihi notae, quae haec spectabant. 1748, 10 Febr.
