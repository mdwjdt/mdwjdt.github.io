Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 5194

5194. There was a certain one of the ancients in an angelic society who within had wisdom. He left, and then the rest as to their understanding were in the shade [cf. 5189].

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 5194

5194. There was a certain one of the ancients, in an angelic society, who had wisdom in himself. He withdrew; and, then, the rest were in the shade, in respect to intelligence. 1

Footnotes:

1. See above, no. 5189.

Experientiae Spirituales 5194 (original Latin 1748-1764)

5194. Erat quidam ex antiquis in societate angelica, qui sapientiam habebat in se, ille discessit, et tunc reliqui in umbra erant quoad intelligentiam [cf. 5189].
