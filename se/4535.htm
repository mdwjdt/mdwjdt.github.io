Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4535

4535. Affirmative truths are able also to be confirmed by many fallacies, but this is of no concern, for they are easily dispelled. Hence many varieties in forms of worship still make one, if all have charity.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4535

4535. True affirmatives may be confirmed even by many fallacies, but this is of little account, for they are easily dispelled. Hence it is that various forms of worship are of scarcely any importance provided there be charity in all.

Experientiae Spirituales 4535 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4535. Affirmativa vera possunt confirmari etiam multis fallaciis, sed hoc nihil facit, nam eae facile discutiuntur. Inde cultus et varietates plures, usque unum faciunt, si omnibus charitas.
