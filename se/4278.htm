Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4278

4278. About the sirens

Certain sirens are maltreated in such a way that they are transferred into sad and gloomy societies, where they encounter nothing but sorrowful circumstances. In this way their deceitful inclination is broken, and it was said that they then lose something, and become as if insane. 1749, 17 May. 1

Footnotes:

1. Astrological symbol of Mercury, meaning Wednesday.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4278

4278. CONCERNING SIRENS.

Certain sirens are punished by being transmitted into sad and sorrowful societies, where they meet with nothing but what is mournful. Their deceitful minds are thus broken down, and it was said that they thus lose, in a measure, their faculties, and become, as it were, insane. - 1749, May 17.

Experientiae Spirituales 4278 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4278. De sirenibus

Quaedam sirenes ita multantur, quod transmittantur in societates tristes et lugubres, ut illis nihil nisi lugubria, sic frangitur earum dolosus animus, et dictum quod sic perdant aliquid, et fiant quasi insanae. 1749, 17 Maj. 3 19
