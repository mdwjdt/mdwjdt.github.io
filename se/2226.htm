Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2226

2226. The Lord's mercy is universal toward all and each, and yet it is greater toward the angels, because they are the orphans and the widows, having no other father, or husband, than the Lord; for they do not trust in themselves. 1748, 6 June.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2226

2226. The mercy of the Lord is universal towards all and each, but yet is greater towards the angels, because they are orphans and widows - they have no other father nor husband than the Lord, for they do not trust in themselves. - 1748, June 6.

Experientiae Spirituales 2226 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2226. Misericordia Domini est universalis erga omnes et singulos, sed usque est major erga angelos, quia sunt pupilli et viduae, non alium patrem, aut maritum, quam Dominum habent, non enim sibi fidunt. 1748, 6 Junius.
