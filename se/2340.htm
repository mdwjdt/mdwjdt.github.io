Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 2340

2340. Yet the inner meaning of the Lord's Word is different, namely, that each person has his or her own judgment when they die. For shortly afterwards the soul or spirit is separated from the body, and comes into the other life, and undergoes judgment according to the person's faith and works. Judgment is the subject everywhere where hell and heaven are discussed. 1748, 17 June.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 2340

2340. But still the interior sense of the Lord's Word is different, to wit, that everyone has his judgment when he dies, for immediately then is the soul or spirit separated from the body, and comes into another life, and suffers judgment according the judgment to faith and works. It is everywhere treated concerning where it speaks of hell and of heaven. - 1748, June 17.

Experientiae Spirituales 2340 (original Latin 1748-1764)

2340. Sed usque sensus interior Verbi Domini alius est; nempe quod unicuivis est ejus judicium, dum moritur, nam mox tunc anima seu spiritus separatur a corpore, et in alteram vitam venit, fertque judicium secundum fidem, et opera: de judicio ubivis actum est, ubi de inferno, et de coelo. 1748, 17 Junius.
