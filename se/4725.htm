Spiritual Experiences (Odhner and Nemitz translation 1998) 4725

4725. The Lord can certainly appear also to those who do not have love, and so to those to who have a falsely convincing faith, but this is an imaginary appearance, not a real one.

Spiritual Experiences (Buss translation 1902) 4725

4725. The Lord can, indeed, appear, even to those that are not in love, thus to those who are in persuasive faith; but it is an imaginative, not a real appearance.

Experientiae Spirituales 4725 (original Latin 1748-1764)

4725. Apparere quidem potest Dominus, etiam illis qui non in amore sunt, ita qui in fide persuasiva, sed est apparentia imaginativa, non realis.
