----中文待译----

Apocalypse Explained (Tansley translation 1923) 379

379. I heard the voice of the fourth animal saying. That this signifies out of the inmost heaven from the Lord, is plain also from what has been said and shown above (n. 353, 362, 370).

Apocalypse Explained (Whitehead translation 1912) 379

379. I heard the voice of the fourth animal saying, signifies out of the inmost heaven from the Lord; this, too, is evident from what has been said and shown above (n. 35 3, 362, 370).

Apocalypsis Explicata 379 (original Latin 1759)

379. "Audivi vocem quarti animalis dicentam." - Quod significet e caelo intimo a Domino, constat etiam ex illis quae supra (n. 353, 362, 370) dicta et ostensa sunt.
