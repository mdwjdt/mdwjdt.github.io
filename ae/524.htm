Apocalypse Explained (Tansley translation 1923) 524

524. And the fourth angel sounded, signifies influx out of heaven, and thence a fourth change, as above (n. 502, and what follows).

Apocalypse Explained (Whitehead translation 1912) 524

524. Verse 12. And the fourth angel sounded, signifies influx out of heaven, and the fourth consequent change (See above, n. 502), and what follows).

Apocalypsis Explicata 524 (original Latin 1759)

524. [Vers. 12.] "Et quartus angelus clanxit", significat influxum e caelo et quartam inde mutationem, ut supra (n. 502. et postea).
