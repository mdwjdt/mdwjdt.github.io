Apocalypse Explained (Tansley translation 1923) 371

371. Come and see. That this signifies attention and perception, is manifest from what was explained above, where similar words occur (n. 354).

Apocalypse Explained (Whitehead translation 1912) 371

371. Come and see, signifies attention and perception, as is evident from what was explained above where there are like words (n. 354).

Apocalypsis Explicata 371 (original Latin 1759)

371. "Veni et aspice." - Quod significet attentionem et perceptionem, constat etiam ex illis quae supra (n. 354) explicata sunt, ubi similia verba.
