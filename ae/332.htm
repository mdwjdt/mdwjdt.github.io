Apocalypse Explained (Tansley translation 1923) 332

332. (31, where similar words occur.

Apocalypse Explained (Whitehead translation 1912) 332

332. Verse 10. And hast made us unto our God kings and priests, signifies that from the Lord they are in the truths and goods of the church and of heaven, as can be seen from what was shown above n. 31, where there are like words.

Apocalypsis Explicata 332 (original Latin 1759)

332. [Vers. 10.] "Et fecisti nos Deo nostro reges et sacerdotes." - Quod significet quod illi a Domino sint in veris et bonis ecclesiae et caeli, constare potest ex illis quae supra (n. 31) ostensa sunt, ubi similia verba.
