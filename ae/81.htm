Apocalypse Explained (Tansley translation 1923) 81

81. I am the first and the last. That this signifies who rules all things from primaries by means of ultimates, and thus all things of heaven, is evident from the explanation given above (n. 41).

Apocalypse Explained (Whitehead translation 1912) 81

81. I am the First and the Last, signifies who governs all things from firsts by means of ultimates, thus all things of heaven. This is evident from the explanation given above (n. 41).

Apocalypsis Explicata 81 (original Latin 1759)

81. "Ego sum Primus et Ultimus." - Quod significet qui omnia regit ex primis per ultima, et sic omnia caeli, constat ex illis quae supra (n. 41) explicata sunt.
