《圣爱与圣智》 第20节

20、当从功用及其对应关系的角度来看待受造宇宙时，也能得出同样的结论。但在理解这一点之前，必须先举例说明。

Divine Love and Wisdom #20 (Dole (2003))

20. We can draw the same inference from the created universe if we turn our attention to its functions and the things that answer to them. However, this will not be comprehensible until some examples have been offered.

Divine Love and Wisdom #20 (Rogers (1999))

20. The same conclusion may be drawn from the created universe when viewed in terms of the uses it serves and their correspondences. But before this can be understood, it must be preceded by instances to illustrate it.

Divine Love and Wisdom #20 (Harley and Harley (1969))

20. It can be similarly inferred from the created universe when this is regarded from uses and their correspondences. But before this can be understood, some things will precede as illustrations.

Divine Love and Wisdom #20 (Ager (1890))

20. From the created universe a like conclusion may be drawn when it is regarded from uses and their correspondences. But before this can be understood some preliminary illustrations must be given.

De Divino Amore et de Divina Sapientia #20 (original Latin,1763)

20. Simile potest induci ex Universo creato, dum hoc spectatur ex usibus et eorum correspondentiis: sed antequam hoc potest intelligi, praecedent quae illustrabunt.
