属天的奥秘 第7885节

7885.“七日”表神圣之物。这从“七”的含义清楚可知，“七”是一个包含神圣之物的数字；“七日”是指神圣之物。至于“七”包含神圣之物，可参看前文（参看395，433，716，881，5265，5268节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7885

7885. Seven days. That this signifies what is holy, is evident from the signification of "seven," as involving what is holy; and of "seven days," as being what is holy (that "seven" involves what is holy, see n. 395, 433, 716, 881, 5265, 5268).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7885

7885. 'Seven days' means what is holy. This is clear from the meaning of 'seven' as a number that implies holiness, and of 'seven days' as that which is holy. For 'seven' as a number implying holiness, see 395, 433, 716, 881, 5265, 5268.

Latin(1748-1756) 7885

7885. `Septem diebus': quod significet sanctum, constat ex significatione `septem' quod involvat sanctum, et `septem dierum' quod {1}sint sanctum; quod `septem' involvat sanctum, videatur n. 395, 433, 716, 881, 5265, 5268. @1 sit$
