属天的奥秘 第4878节

4878.“与她同寝”表结合。这从“同寝”的含义清楚可知，“同寝”是指结合，也如前所述（4820，4868，4870节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 4878

4878. And came to her. That this signifies conjunction, is evident from the signification of "coming" or "entering to anyone," as being conjunction, of which also above (n. 4820, 4868, 4870).

Elliott(1983-1999) 4878

4878. 'And came [in] to her' means the joining together. This is clear from the meaning of 'coming (or going in) to someone' as a joining together, also dealt with above, in 4820, 4868, 4870.

Latin(1748-1756) 4878

4878. `Et venit ad illam': quod significet conjunctionem, constat (c)ex significatione `venire seu intrare ad aliquam' quod sit conjunctio, de qua etiam supra n. 4820, 4868, 4870.
