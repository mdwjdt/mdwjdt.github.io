属天的奥秘 第7559节

7559.“凡在田间的”表凡属教会之物。这从“田”的含义清楚可知，“田”是指教会，如刚才所述（7557节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7559

7559. Which shall be found in the field. That this signifies which is of the church, is evident from the signification of "field," as being the church (of which just above, n. 7557).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7559

7559. 'That is found in the field' means which belongs to the Church. This is clear from the meaning of 'the field' as the Church, dealt with just above in 7557.

Latin(1748-1756) 7559

7559. `Quae invenitur in agro': quod significet quod Ecclesiae, constat ex significatione `agri' quod sit Ecclesia, de qua mox supra n. 7557.
