属天的奥秘 第9217节

9217.“将来他向我呼求”表向主祈求。这从“向耶和华呼求”的含义清楚可知，“向耶和华呼求”是指向主祈求，如前所述（9202节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9217

9217. And it shall be, when he shall cry unto Me. That this signifies supplication to the Lord, is evident from the signification of "crying unto Jehovah," as being supplication to the Lord (as above, n. 9202).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9217

9217. 'When he cries out to Me' means pleading to the Lord. This is clear from the meaning of 'crying out to Jehovah' as pleading to the Lord, as above in 9202.

Latin(1748-1756) 9217

9217. `Quando clamaverit ad Me': quod significet supplicationem ad Dominum, constat ex significatione `clamare ad {1}Jehovam' quod sit supplicatio ad Dominum, ut supra n. 9202. @1 Me$
