属天的奥秘 第4714节

4714.“就带回话来给我”表获知。这从“带回话”的含义清楚可知，“带回话”是指报告情形如何，因而表示获知。

Potts(1905-1910) 4714

4714. And bring me word again. That this signifies knowledge, is evident from the signification of "bringing word again," as being to report how matters were, thus knowledge.

Elliott(1983-1999) 4714

4714. 'And bring back word to me' means knowledge. This is clear from the meaning of 'bringing back word' as describing what the situation was, and so means knowledge.

Latin(1748-1756) 4714

4714. `Et reduc mihi verbum': quod significet cognitionem, constat ex significatione `reducere verbum' quod sit referre quomodo se res haberet, ita cognitio.
