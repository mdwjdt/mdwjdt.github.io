属天的奥秘 第9764节

9764.“二十肘的”表直到完全。这从“二十”的含义清楚可知，“二十”是指完全或完整之物（参看9641节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9764

9764. Of twenty cubits. That this signifies to the full is evident from the signification of "twenty," as being what is full (see n. 9641).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9764

9764. 'Of twenty cubits' means to the full. This is clear from the meaning of 'twenty' as what is full or complete, dealt with in 9641.

Latin(1748-1756) 9764

9764. `Viginti cubiti': quod significet {1} ad plenum, constat ex significatione `viginti' quod sint plenum, de qua n. (x)9641. @1 sint IT$
