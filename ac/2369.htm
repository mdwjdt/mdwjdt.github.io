属天的奥秘 第2369节

2369．“他们说”表愤怒的回答。这从前后文清楚可知，因而无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 2369

2369. And they said. That this signifies a reply from anger, is evident from what precedes and what follows, and thus without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2369

2369. That 'they said' means the reply made in anger becomes clear from what goes before and from what follows, and so without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 2369

2369. `Et dixerunt': quod significet responsum ex ira, constare potest ab illis quae praecedunt et quae sequuntur, ita absque explicatione.
