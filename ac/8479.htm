属天的奥秘 第8479节

8479.“他们不听从摩西”表缺乏信，并因此缺乏顺服。这从“听从”的含义清楚可知，“听从”是指觉知、有信并顺服（参看5017，7216，8361节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8479

8479. And they heard not unto Moses. That this signifies no faith and thence no obedience, is evident from the signification of "to hear," as being to perceive, to have faith, and to obey (see n. 5017, 7216, 8361).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8479

8479. 'And they did not hear Moses' means lack of faith and consequently lack of obedience. This is clear from the meaning of 'hearing' as perceiving, possessing faith, and obeying, dealt with in 5017, 7216, 8361.

Latin(1748-1756) 8479

8479. `Et non audiverunt ad Moschen': quod significet non fidem, et inde non oboedientiam, constat ex significatione `audire' quod sit percipere, fidem habere, et oboedire, de qua n. 5017, 7216, 8361.
