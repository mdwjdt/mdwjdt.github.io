属天的奥秘 第6457节

6457.“在迦南地”表教会所在之地。这从“迦南地”的含义清楚可知，“迦南地”是指教会（参看3686，3705，4447，5136节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6457

6457. In the land of Canaan. That this signifies where the church is, is evident from the signification of the "land of Canaan," as being the church (see n. 3686, 3705, 4447, 5136).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6457

6457. 'In the land of Canaan' means where the Church is. This is clear from the meaning of 'the land of Canaan' as the Church, dealt with in 3705, 3686, 4447, 5136.

Latin(1748-1756) 6457

6457. `In terra Canaan': quod significet ubi Ecclesia, constat ex significatione `terrae Canaanis' quod sit Ecclesia, de qua n. 3705, 3686, 4447, 5136.
