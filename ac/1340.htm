[NCE]1340. This symbolism too needs no proof here. The fact that Shelah was a nation by that name and that it symbolizes the effect of secular knowledge was stated earlier, at verse 24 of the last chapter [1235, 1237].

Potts(1905-1910) 1340

1340. That these things are signified calls for no confirmation. That "Shelah" was a nation so called, whereby is signified that which pertains to memory-knowledge, has been stated before, at verse 24 of the preceding chapter.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1340

1340. There is no need to confirm that this is what these words mean as it has been stated already at verse 24 of the previous chaptera that 'Shelah' was a nation so named and that it means that which is the offspring of knowledge.
