属天的奥秘 第1418节

1418.“我必赐福给你”表属天之物的繁殖和属灵之物的增多。这从圣言中“赐福”的含义清楚可知，对此，我稍后会予以论述。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1418. And bless you symbolizes the way heavenly things reproduce themselves and spiritual things multiply, as can be seen from the symbolism in the Word of blessing, discussed just below [搂搂1420, 1422].

Potts(1905-1910) 1418

1418. And I will bless thee. That this signifies the fructification of celestial things and the multiplication of spiritual things, is evident from the signification in the Word of "to bless," concerning which presently.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1418

1418. 'I will bless you' means fruitfulness in celestial and increase in spiritual things. This becomes clear from the meaning of 'blessing' in the Word, to be dealt with in what follows shortly.

Latin(1748-1756) 1418

1418. 'Et benedicam tibi': quod significet caelestium fructificationem et spiritualium multiplicationem, constare potest a significatione 'benedicere' in Verbo, de qua in mox sequentibus.
