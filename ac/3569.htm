属天的奥秘 第3569节

3569.“我的灵魂给你祝福”表结合。这从“祝福”的含义清楚可知，“祝福”是指结合（3504，3514，3530，3565节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 3569

3569. That my soul may bless thee. That this signifies conjunction, is evident from the signification of "being blessed," as being conjunction (n. 3504, 3514, 3530, 3565).

Elliott(1983-1999) 3569

3569. 'So that my soul may bless you' means conjunction. This is clear from the meaning of 'being blessed' as conjunction, dealt with in 3504, 3514, 3530, 3565

Latin(1748-1756) 3569

3569. `Ideo ut benedicat tibi anima mea': quod significet conjunctionem, constat ex significatione `benedici' quod sit conjunctio, de qua n. 3504, 3514, (x)3530, 3565.
