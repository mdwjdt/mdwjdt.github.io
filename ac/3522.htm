属天的奥秘 第3522节

3522.“使他给你祝福”表由此的结合。这从“祝福”的含义清楚可知，“祝福”是指结合（参看3504，3514节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 3522

3522. That he may bless thee. That this signifies conjunction thereby, is evident from the signification of "blessing," as being conjunction (see n. 3504, 3514).

Elliott(1983-1999) 3522

3522. 'So that he may bless you' means conjunction thereby. This is clear from the meaning of 'blessing' as conjunction, dealt with above in 3504, 3514.

Latin(1748-1756) 3522

3522. `Propterea ut benedicat tibi': quod significet conjunctionem sic, constat a significatione benedicere quod sit conjunctio, de qua supra n. 3504, 3514.
