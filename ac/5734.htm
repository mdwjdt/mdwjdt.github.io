5734.“尽他们所能带的”表到足量的程度。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 5734

5734. As much as they can carry. That this signifies to sufficiency, may be seen without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5734

5734. 'As much as they can carry' means to the point of sufficiency. This becomes clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 5734

5734. `Quantum possunt ferre': quod significet ad sufficientiam constare potest absque explicatione.
