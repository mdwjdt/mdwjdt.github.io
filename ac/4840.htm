属天的奥秘 第4840节

4840.“耶和华也就叫他死了”表教会的代表又没了。这从前面所述（4833节）清楚可知，那里有同样的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 4840

4840. And He caused him also to die. That this signifies that there was also no representative of the church, is evident from what was shown above (n. 4833), where similar words occur.

Elliott(1983-1999) 4840

4840. 'And He caused him to die also' means that again there was no representative of the Church. This is clear from what has been shown above in 4833, where similar words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 4840

4840. `Et mori fecit etiam eum': quod significet quod etiam Ecclesiae repraesentativum nullum, constat ex illis quae supra n. 4833 ostensa sunt, ubi similia verba.
