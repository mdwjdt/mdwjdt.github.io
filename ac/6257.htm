属天的奥秘 第6257节

6257.“年纪老迈”表因为他处于其代表的最后阶段。这从“年纪老迈”的含义清楚可知，“年纪老迈”是指代表的一种新状态，或代表的新样（参看3254节），因而是指前一个代表或状态的最后阶段。

Potts(1905-1910) 6257

6257. With old age. That this signifies because at the end of the representation, is evident from the signification of "old age," as being newness of representation (see n. 3254), thus the end of the former one.

Elliott(1983-1999) 6257

6257. 'Because of old age' means because he was in the final phase of his representation. This is clear from the meaning of 'old age' as a new state of representation, dealt with in 3254, thus the final phase of a former one.

Latin(1748-1756) 6257

6257. `Prae senectute': quod significet quia in fine repraesentationis, constat ex significatione `senectutis' quod sit novum repraesentationis, de qua n. 3254, ita finis prioris.
