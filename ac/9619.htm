9619.“一幅幔子要这样”表个体真理要这样。也如前所述（9602节），那里有同样的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 9619

9619. For one curtain. That this signifies thus in each of the truths, is evident from the signification of "curtain," of which also above (n. 9602), where the same words occur.

Elliott(1983-1999) 9619

9619. '[This is what] one curtain [shall be]' means so shall it be for the individual truths, also dealt with above, in 9602, where the same words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 9619

9619. `Aulaeum unum': quod significet ita singulis veris, de qua etiam {1} supra n. 9502, ubi similia verba. @1 ut quoque$
