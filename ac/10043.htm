属天的奥秘 第10043节

10043.“亚伦和他儿子要按手”表能力的转移。这从“按手”的含义清楚可知，“按手”是指能力的转移（参看10023节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10043

10043. And Aaron and his sons shall lay their hands. That this signifies the communication of power, is evident from the signification of "laying on hands," as being the communication of power (see above, n. 10023).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10043

10043. 'And Aaron and his sons shall lay their hands' means the transmission of power ... This is clear from the meaning of 'laying hands on' as the transmission of power, dealt with above in 10023.

Latin(1748-1756) 10043

10043. `Et imponent Aharon et filii ejus manus suas': quod significet potentiae communicationem, constat ex significatione `imponere manus' quod sit communicatio potentiae, de qua supra n. 10,023.
