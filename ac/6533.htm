属天的奥秘 第6533节

6533.“又有战车和他一同上去”表教义事物。这从“战车”的含义清楚可知，“战车”是指教义事物（参看5321，5945节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6533

6533. And there went up with him both chariots. That this signifies doctrinal things, is evident from the signification of "chariots," as being doctrinal things (see n. 5321, 5945).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6533

6533. 'And there went up with him both chariots' means matters of doctrine. This is clear from the meaning of 'chariots' as matters of doctrine, dealt with in 5321, 5945.

Latin(1748-1756) 6533

6533. `Et ascendit cum eo etiam currus': quod significet doctrinalia, constat ex significatione `currus' quod sint doctrinalia, de qua n. 5321, 5945.
