Potts(1905-1910) 4511

4511. They took captive and made a prey of. That this signifies that they stripped them and perverted them, is evident from the series of things in the internal sense.

4511. 'They took captive and plundered' means that they seized them and perverted them. This is clear from the train of thought in the internal sense.
