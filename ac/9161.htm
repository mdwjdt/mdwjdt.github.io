属天的奥秘 第9161节

9161.“谁就要加倍赔偿给他的同伴”表完全修正。这从“赔偿”和“加倍”的含义清楚可知：“赔偿”是指修正（9097节）；“加倍”是指完全（9103节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9161

9161. Shall repay double to his companion. That this signifies amendment to the full, is evident from the signification of "repaying" as being amendment (n. 9097); and from the signification of "double," as being to the full (n. 9103).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9161

9161. 'Shall repay double to his companion' means making amends to the complete amount. This is clear from the meaning of 'repaying' as making amends, dealt with in [9087,] 9097; and from the meaning of 'double' as to the complete amount, dealt with in 9103.

Latin(1748-1756) 9161

9161. `Rependet duplum socio suo': quod significet emendationem ad plenum, constat ex significatione `rependere' quod sit emendatio, de qua n. 9097, et ex significatione `dupli' quod sit ad plenum, de qua n. 9103.
