属天的奥秘 第9650节

9650.“和银座四十个”表以及在外在部分中通过真理所获得的完全支撑。如前所述（9643节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9650

9650. And their forty bases of silver. This signifies there also a full support by means of truth (as above, n. 9643).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9650

9650. 'And their forty bases [made] from silver' means complete support received through truth in the external parts as well, as above in 9643.

Latin(1748-1756) 9650

9650. `Et quadraginta bases eorum argento' significat etiam ibi plenariam sustentationem per verum, ut supra n. 9643.
