属天的奥秘 第2059节

2059.“他背了我的约”表他无法被联结。这从“约”的含义清楚可知，“约”是指结合，如前面所解释的。因此，“背约”就是使自己分离到无法被联结的地步。

Potts(1905-1910) 2059

2059. He hath made vain My covenant. That this signifies that he cannot be conjoined, is evident from the signification of a "covenant," as being conjunction, explained before. Thus to "make the covenant vain," is to disjoin himself to such an extent that he cannot be conjoined.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2059

2059. 'He has broken My covenant' means that he cannot be conjoined. This is clear from the meaning of 'a covenant' as conjunction, dealt with already, and so 'breaking a covenant' is cutting oneself off to such an extent that conjunction is not possible.

Latin(1748-1756) 2059

2059. Quod `foedus Meum irritum fecit' significet quod conjungi non possit, constat ex significatione `foederis' quod sit conjunctio, de qua prius, ita `foedus irritum facere' est se disjungere, adeo ut conjungi non possit.
