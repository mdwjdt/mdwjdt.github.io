属天的奥秘 第5024节

5024.“就照这些话向他讲述”表说谎。这从接下来的经文明显可知，因为她告诉她丈夫的事都是虚假。

Potts(1905-1910) 5024

5024. And she spoke unto him according to these words. That this signifies false speaking, is evident from what follows; for the things which she told her husband are falsities.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5024

5024. 'And she spoke to him in these words' means speaking falsely. This is clear from what follows, for the things she told her husband were false.

Latin(1748-1756) 5024

5024. `Et locuta ad illum secundum verba haec' quod significet falsiloquium, constat ex illis quae sequuntur, sunt enim falsa quae ad maritum suum locuta est.
