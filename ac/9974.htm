Potts(1905-1910) 9974

9974. CHAPTER THE TWENTY-NINTH.

THE DOCTRINE OF CHARITY AND FAITH*

9974. Those who believe that they merit heaven by the goods which they do, do goods from themselves, and not from the Lord. * Here concerning Merit and Reward. (REVISER.)

Elliott(1983-1999) 9974

9974.29 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------

TEACHINGS ABOUT CHARITY AND FAITH

Those who think that by the good deeds they perform they merit heaven perform good deeds which commence in self, not in the Lord.

Latin(1748-1756) 9974

9974. EXODI CAPUT VICESIMUM NONUM DOCTRINA CHARITATIS ET FIDEI

Qui credunt quod per bona quae faciunt mereantur caelum, bona faciunt ex se et non ex Domino.
