属天的奥秘 第10390节

10390.由于洗礼是这些事的一个标志和一个提醒，所以一个人在还是个小孩子，甚至是一个婴儿时可能就受洗了，若那时没有受洗，可能到了成年时也会受洗。

Potts(1905-1910) 10390

10390. As Baptism is for a sign and a memorial of these things, therefore a man may be baptized when an infant, and if not then, when an adult.

Elliott(1983-1999) 10390

10390. Since baptism serves as a sign and reminder of these things a person may be baptized when he is a young child, or if not then, when he is adult.

Latin(1748-1756) 10390

10390. Quia Baptismus est in signum et in memoriale illorum, ideo homo baptizari potest infans, et si non tunc, potest adultus.
