7413.“不肯听他们”表不服从。这从前面所述（参看7224，7275，7301，7339节）清楚可知，那里有相同的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 7413

7413. And he heard them not. That this signifies no obedience, is evident from what was said above (see n. 7224, 7275, 7301, 7339), where are the like words.

Elliott(1983-1999) 7413

7413. 'And did not hear them' means disobedience, as above in 7224, 7275, 7301, 7379, where similar words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 7413

7413. `Et non audivit ad illos': quod significet non oboedientiam, ut supra n. 7224, (x)7275, 7301, 7339, ubi similia vera.
