属天的奥秘 第10281节

10281.“这要给我作圣膏油”表神性人身方面的主的一个代表。这从前面的说明（10267节）清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 10281

10281. This shall be to Me an oil of anointing of holiness. That this signifies a representative of the Lord as to the Divine Human, is evident from what was shown above (n. 10267).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10281

10281. 'This shall be a holy anointing oil to Me' means a representative sign of the Lord as regards His Divine Human. This is clear from what has been shown above in 10267.

Latin(1748-1756) 10281

10281. `Oleum unctionis sanctitatis erit hoc Mihi': quod significet repraesentativam Domini quoad Divinum Humanum, constat ex illis quae supra n. (x)10,267 ostensa sunt.
