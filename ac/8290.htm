属天的奥秘 第8290节

8290.“我要追袭”表侵扰。这从“追袭”的含义清楚可知，当由那些陷入邪恶的人指着那些处于良善的人来说时，“追袭”是指侵扰并试图征服。

Potts(1905-1910) 8290

8290. I will pursue. That this signifies infestation, is evident from the signification of "pursuing," when it is said by those who are in evil concerning those who are in good, as being to infest, and to endeavor to subjugate.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8290

8290. 'I will pursue' means molestation. This is clear from the meaning of 'pursuing', when said by those ruled by evil regarding those governed by good, as molesting and trying to place in subjection.

Latin(1748-1756) 8290

8290. `Persequar': quod significet infestationem, constat ex significatione `persequi,' cum dicitur ab illis qui in malo de illis qui sunt in bono, quod sit infestare et conari subjugare.
