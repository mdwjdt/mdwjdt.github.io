属天的奥秘 第8322节

8322.“等候这百姓过去”表那些属于教会，处于真理和良善的人将以这种方式得救。这从刚才的解释清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 8322

8322. Until shall pass over this people. That this signifies that thus shall be saved those of the church who are in truth and good, is evident from what has been unfolded just above.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8322

8322. 'Till this people pass over' means that in this way those belonging to the Church who are governed by truth and good will be saved. This is clear from the explanation immediately above.

Latin(1748-1756) 8322

8322. `Usque dum transibit populus hic': quod significet quod sic salvabuntur qui in vero et bono ab Ecclesia, constat ex illis quae nunc supra explicata sunt.
