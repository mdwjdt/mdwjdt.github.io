属天的奥秘 第9558节

9558.“一个枝上”表源于良善的真理的能力。这从“枝”的含义清楚可知，“枝”是指源于良善的真理和由此而来的能力，如前所述（9555节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9558

9558. In one reed. That this signifies the power of truth from good, is evident from the signification of a "reed," as being truth from good and the consequent power (of which above, n. 9555).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9558

9558. 'On one branch' means the power of truth derived from good. This is clear from the meaning of 'branch' as truth derived from good, and consequently power, dealt with above in 9555.

Latin(1748-1756) 9558

9558. `In calamo uno': quod significet potentiam veri ex bono, constat ex significatione `calami' quod sit verum ex bono, et inde potentia, de qua supra n. 9555.
