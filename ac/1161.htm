New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1161. The symbolism of Ham as faith detached from charity can be seen from what was said and shown about Ham in the last chapter [1062-1063, 1076-1078].

Potts(1905-1910) 1161

1161. That by "Ham" is signified faith separated from charity, is evident from what was said and shown concerning Ham in the preceding chapter.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1161

1161. That 'Ham' means faith separated from charity is clear from what has been stated and shown about Ham in the previous chapter.

Latin(1748-1756) 1161

1161. Quod per 'Ham' significetur fides separata a charitate, constat ab illis quae in capite praecedente de Hamo dicta et ostensa sunt.
