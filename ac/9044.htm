9044.“却未造成伤害”表因此没有在那里，就是在属世层中造成任何伤害。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

9044. And harm is not done. That this signifies thus no injury there, namely, in the natural, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 9044

9044. 'And no harm is done' means that thus no injury is caused there - in the natural. This is clear without explanation.

9044. `Et non fit damnum': quod significet quod sic non laesio ibi, nempe in naturali, constat absque explicatione.
