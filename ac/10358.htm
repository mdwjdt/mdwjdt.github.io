属天的奥秘 第10358节

10358.“世世代代”表在教会的每一个事物里面。这从“世代”的含义清楚可知，当论及以色列人时，“世代”是指教会的每一个事物（10282节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10358

10358. To your generations. That this signifies in each and all things of the church, is evident from the signification of "generations," when said of the sons of Israel, as being each and all things of the church (n. 10282).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10358

10358. '[Throughout] your generations' means within every single thing of the Church. This is clear from the meaning of 'generations', when this refers to the children of Israel, as every single thing of the Church, dealt with in 10282.

Latin(1748-1756) 10358

10358. `Generationibus vestris': quod significet in omnibus et singulis Ecclesiae, constat ex significatione `generationum' cum de filiis Israelis, quod sint omnia et singula Ecclesiae, de qua n. 10,282.
