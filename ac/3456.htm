属天的奥秘 第3456节

3456.“以撒就为他们设摆筵席”表住在一起。这从“筵席”的含义清楚可知，“筵席”是指住在一起（2341节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 3456

3456. And he made them a feast. That this signifies a dwelling together, is evident from the signification of a "feast," as being a dwelling together (n. 2341).

Elliott(1983-1999) 3456

3456. 'He made a feast for them' means dwelling together. This is clear from the meaning of 'a feast' as dwelling together, dealt with in 2341.

Latin(1748-1756) 3456

3456. `Fecit illis convivium': quod significet cohabitationem, constat a significatione `convivii' quod sit cohabitatio, de qua n. 2341.
