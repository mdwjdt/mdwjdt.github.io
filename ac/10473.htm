属天的奥秘 第10473节

10473.“他们对我说”表劝告。这从“说”的含义清楚可知，当论及那些兴趣在于与内在之物分离的外在事物的人时，“说”是指劝告（参看10398节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10473

10473. And they said unto me. That this signifies exhortation, is evident from the signification of "saying," when said of those who are in things external separate from what is internal, as being exhortation (see above, n. 10398).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10473

10473. 'And they said to me' means exhortation. This is clear from the meaning of 'saying', when it has reference to those whose interest lies in external things separated from what is internal, as exhortation, as above in 10398.

Latin(1748-1756) 10473

10473. `Et dixerunt mihi': quod significet exhortationem, constat ex significatione `dicere' cum de illis qui in {1}externis separatis ab interno sunt, quod sit exhortatio, ut{2} supra n. 10,398. @1 externo separato$ @2 i quoque$
