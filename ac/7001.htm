属天的奥秘 第7001节

7001.“他一见你”表觉知。这从“见”的含义清楚可知，“见”是指理解和觉知（参看2150，2807，3764，3863，4567，4723节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7001

7001. And he will see thee. That this signifies perception, is evident from the signification of "seeing," as being to understand and perceive (see n. 2150, 2807, 3764, 3863, 4567, 4723).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7001

7001. 'And he will see you' means perception. This is clear from the meaning of 'seeing' as understanding and perceiving, dealt with in 2150, 2807, 3764, 3863, 4567, 4723.

Latin(1748-1756) 7001

7001. `Et videbit te': quod significet perceptionem, constat ex significatione `videre' quod sit intelligere et percipere, de qua n. 2150, 2807, 3764, 3863, 4567, 4723.
