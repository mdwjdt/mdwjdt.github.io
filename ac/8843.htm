属天的奥秘 第8843节

8843.“恐怕祂在他们身上制造破口”表与真理和良善的分离（如前所述8833节），在此是指既与真理，也与良善的分离，因为它既论及“百姓”，也论及祭司。

Potts(1905-1910) 8843

8843. Lest perchance He make a breach in them. That this signifies separation from truth and good, is evident (as above, n. 8833), here, from truth also, because it is said of the people also.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8843

8843. 'So that He does not perhaps make a breach in them' means separation from truth and good, as above in 8833, at this point a separation from truth as well as from good since it speaks of 'the people' as well as the priests.

Latin(1748-1756) 8843

8843. `Ne forte rupturam faciat in illis' significat separationem a vero et bono, ut supra n. 8833, hic quoque a vero, quia dicitur etiam de populo.
