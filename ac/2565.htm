属天的奥秘 第2565节

2565.“亚比米勒把”表信之教义。这从“亚比米勒”的含义清楚可知，“亚比米勒”是指信之教义（参看2504，2509，2510节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 2565

2565. Abimelech took. That this signifies the doctrine of faith, is evident from the signification of "Abimelech," as being the doctrine of faith (see n. 2504, 2509, 2510).

Elliott(1983-1999) 2565

2565. 'Abimelech took' means that the doctrine of faith.... This is clear from the meaning of 'Abimelech' as the doctrine of faith, dealt with in 2504, 2509, 2510.

Latin(1748-1756) 2565

2565. Quod `accepit Abimelech' significet quod doctrina fide constat a significatione `Abimelechi' quod sit doctrina fidei, de qua n. (o)2504, 2509, 2510.
