属天的奥秘 第748节

748.“它们进入方舟”表它们受到保护。这一点在前面第7节已予以说明，那里论及挪亚和他的儿子、儿媳。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]748. I already said at verse 7 above [741], where it concerned Noah and his sons and the wives, that they entered the ark means that they were protected.

Potts(1905-1910) 748

748. That their "going into the ark" signifies that they were protected, was stated before at the seventh verse, where it is said concerning Noah and his sons and their wives.

Elliott(1983-1999) 748

748. 'They went into the ark' means that these pairs were protected. This has been stated already at verse 7 above where Noah, his sons, and their wives are the subject.

Latin(1748-1756) 748

748. Quod 'intrarunt in arcam' significent quod tutata sint, prius est dictum, vers. praec. 7, ubi de Noaho, et filiis ejus, et uxoribus.
