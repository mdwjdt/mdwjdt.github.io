属天的奥秘 第10448节

10448.“于是耶和华后悔，不把祂所说的祸降与祂的百姓”表对他们的怜悯。如前所述（10441节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10448

10448. And Jehovah repented of the evil which He said He would do to His people. That this signifies mercy for them, may be seen above (n. 10441).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10448

10448. 'And Jehovah repented of the evil which He said He would do to His people' means having mercy on them, as above in 10441.

Latin(1748-1756) 10448

10448. `Et paenitebat Jehovam super malo quod dixit facere populo Suo': quod significet misericordiam pro illis, ut supra n. 10,441.
