属天的奥秘 第8064节

8064.“当那日，你要告诉你的儿子说”表对真理的内层觉知，这种觉知属于良知。这从前面的解释（参看7935节）清楚可知，那里有同样的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 8064

8064. And thou shalt tell thy son in that day, saying. That this signifies the interior perception which is of conscience, is evident from what was unfolded above (see n. 7935), where are like words.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8064

8064. 'And you shall tell your son on that day, saying' means the inward perception of truth that belongs to conscience. This is clear from the explanation in 7935, where similar words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 8064

8064. `Et indicabis filio tuo in die isto, dicendo': quod significet interiorem perceptionem veri, quae conscientiae, constat ex illis quae n. 7935 explicata sunt, ubi similia.
