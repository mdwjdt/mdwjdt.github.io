属天的奥秘 第6790节

6790.“你们去请他”表它必须被结合。这从“请”的含义清楚可知，“请”是指被结合（参看6047节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6790

6790. Call him. That this signifies that it should be conjoined, is evident from the signification of "calling," as being to be conjoined (see n. 6047).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6790

6790. 'Call him' means that it was to be joined. This is clear from the meaning of 'calling' as being joined together, dealt with in 6047.

Latin(1748-1756) 6790

6790. `Vocate illum': quod significet quod conjungendum, constat ex significatione `vocare' quod sit conjungi, de qua n. 6047.
