属天的奥秘 第6129节

6129.“那一年”表这种状态的时期。这从“一年”的含义清楚可知，“一年”是指从开始到结束的一整个时期（参看2906节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6129

6129. In this year. That this signifies the period of this state, is evident from the signification of a "year," as being an entire period from beginning to end (see n. 2906).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6129

6129. 'That year' means the period covered by that state. This is clear from the meaning of 'year' as a whole period from start to finish, dealt within 2906.

Latin(1748-1756) 6129

6129. `In anno hoc': quod significet illius status periodum, constat ex significatione `anni' quod sit integra periodus a principio ad finem, de qua n. 2906.
