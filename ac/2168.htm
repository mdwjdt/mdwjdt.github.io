属天的奥秘 第2168节

2168.“你们既到仆人这里来”表他们为此目的而来。这也是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 2168

2168. For therefore have ye passed over unto your servant. That this signifies that they came for this purpose, is also evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2168

2168. 'For this is why you have passed over to your servant' means that this had been the purpose of their coming. This too is clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 2168

2168. `Nam quamobrem transiistis ad servum vestrum': quod significet quod ideo venerint, etiam absque explicatione constat.
