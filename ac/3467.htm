属天的奥秘 第3467节

3467.“直到今日”表状态的永久性。这从“直到今日”的含义清楚可知，“直到今日”是指状态的永久性（2838节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 3467

3467. Unto this day. That this signifies the perpetuity of the state, is evident from the signification of "unto this day," as being perpetuity of state (n. 2838).

Elliott(1983-1999) 3467

3467. 'Even to this day' means the perpetuity of the state. This is clear from the meaning of 'to this day' as perpetuity of the state, dealt with in 2838.

Latin(1748-1756) 3467

3467. `Usque ad diem hunc': quod significet status perpetuum, constat a significatione `ad diem hunc' {1}quod sit status perpetuum, de qua n. 2838. @1 i seu hodie$
