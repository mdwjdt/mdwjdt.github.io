属天的奥秘 第2358节

2358.“说”表诚恳地乞求。这从下文清楚可知，因而无需进一步解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 2358

2358. And he said. That this signifies exhortation, is evident from what now follows, thus without further explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2358

2358. 'And he said' means an earnest appeal. This is clear from what follows next, thus without further explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 2358

2358. `Et dixit': quod significet exhortationem, constat ex illis quae nunc sequuntur, ita absque ulteriore explicatione.
