属天的奥秘 第7612节

7612.“法老见”表一个发现。这从“看见”的含义清楚可知，“看见”是指一个发现（参看2150，3764，4723，5400节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7612

7612. And Pharaoh saw. That this signifies a noticing, is evident from the signification of "seeing," as being a noticing (see n. 2150, 3764, 4723, 5400).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7612

7612. 'And Pharaoh saw' means a discernment. This is clear from the meaning of 'seeing' as a discernment, dealt with in 2150, 3764, 4723, 5400.

Latin(1748-1756) 7612

7612. `Et vidit Pharao': quod significet apperceptionem, constat ex significatione `videre' quod sit apperceptio, de qua n. (x)2150, 3764, 4723, 5400.
