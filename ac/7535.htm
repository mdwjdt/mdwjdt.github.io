属天的奥秘 第7535节

7535.“正如耶和华对摩西所说的”表正如所预言的。如前所述（7302，7340，7414，7432节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7535

7535. As Jehovah had spoken unto Moses. That this signifies according to the prediction, see above, n. 7302, 7340, 7414, 7432.

Elliott(1983-1999) 7535

7535. 'As Jehovah had spoken [to Moses]' means as accorded with what had been foretold, as previously in 7301, 7340, 7414, 7432.

Latin(1748-1756) 7535

7535. `Quemadmodum locutus Jehovah [ad Moschen]': quod significet secundum praedictionem, ut prius n. 7302, 7340, 7414, 7432.
