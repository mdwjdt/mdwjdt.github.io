属天的奥秘 第7767节

7767.“然后他必把你们从这里打发走”表那时要离开他们。这从“打发走”是指离开，如前面频繁所述。

Potts(1905-1910) 7767

7767. Afterward he will let you go from hence. That this signifies that then they will be left, is evident from the signification of "to let go," as being to leave (as frequently above).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7767

7767. 'Afterwards he will send you away from here' means that then they will be left alone. This is clear from the meaning of 'sending away' as leaving, as often before.

Latin(1748-1756) 7767

7767. `Post ita dimittet vos abhinc': quod significet quod tunc relinquentur, constat a significatione `dimittere' quod sit relinquere, ut saepe prius.
