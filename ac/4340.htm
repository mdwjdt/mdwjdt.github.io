属天的奥秘 第4340节

4340.“见以扫来了”表神性属世良善。这从“以扫”的代表清楚可知，“以扫”是指属世层中的神性良善（参看3576节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 4340

4340. And behold Esau came. That this signifies Divine good natural, is evident from the representation of Esau, as being Divine good in the natural (see n. 3576).

Elliott(1983-1999) 4340

4340. 'Esau was coming' means Divine Natural Good. This is clear from the representation of 'Esau' as Divine Good within the natural, dealt with in 3576.

Latin(1748-1756) 4340

4340. Quod `Esau venit' significet quod Divinum Bonum naturale, constat ex repraesentatione `Esavi' quod sit Divinum Bonum in naturali, de qua n. 3576 {1}. @1 i 3599$
