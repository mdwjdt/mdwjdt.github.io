Potts(1905-1910) 6166

6166. Concerning the ground of Egypt, that Pharaoh should have the fifth. This signifies remains, as before (n. 6156).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6166

6166. 'Regarding the ground of Egypt, that a fifth went to Pharaoh' means remnants, as previously in 6156.

Latin(1748-1756) 6166

6166. `Super humum Aegypti Pharaoni ad quintam': quod significet reliquias, ut prius, n. 6156.
