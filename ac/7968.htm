属天的奥秘 第7968节

7968.“扛在肩头上”表用他们所拥有的一切能力来支持。这从“肩头”的含义清楚可知，“肩头”是指一切能力（参看1085，4931-4937节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7968

7968. Upon their shoulder. That this signifies according to all ability, is evident from the signification of the "shoulder," as being all power (see n. 1085, 4931-4937).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7968

7968. 'On their shoulder' means supported with all the power they had. This is clear from the meaning of 'shoulder' as all power, dealt with in 1085, 4931-4937.

Latin(1748-1756) 7968

7968. `Super humero illorum': quod significet secundum omnem potentiam, constat ex significatione `humeri' quod sit omnis potentia, de qua n. 1085, 4931-4937.
