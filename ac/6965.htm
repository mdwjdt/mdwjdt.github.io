属天的奥秘 第6965节

6965.“把手放回怀里”表将真理变成他们自己的。这从“把手放回怀里”的含义清楚可知，“把手放回怀里”是指将真理变成人自己的（参看6960节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6965

6965. Bring back thy hand unto thy bosom. That this signifies the appropriation of truth, is evident from the signification of "bringing the hand into the bosom," as being the appropriation of truth (see n. 6960).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6965

6965. 'Put your hand back to your bosom' means making truth their own. This is clear from the meaning of 'putting a hand into one's bosom' as making truth one's own, dealt with in 6960.

Latin(1748-1756) 6965

6965. `Reduc manum tuam ad sinum tuum': quod significet appropriationem veri, constat ex significatione `adducere manum in sinum' quod sit appropriatio veri, de qua {1} n. 6960. @1 i supra$
