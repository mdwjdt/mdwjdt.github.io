属天的奥秘 第5762节

5762.“就这样行吧”表从轻处罚。这从接下来的话清楚可知，那里给出了不怎么严厉的处罚。

Potts(1905-1910) 5762

5762. So be it. That this signifies a milder sentence, is evident from the words that follow, in which this milder sentence is given.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5762

5762. 'So let it be' means a less severe sentence. This is clear from what immediately follows, where the less severe sentence is declared.

Latin(1748-1756) 5762

5762. `Ita id': quod significet mitiorem sententiam, constat ex nunc sequentibus ubi mitior illa sententia dictatur.
