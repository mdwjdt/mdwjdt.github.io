属天的奥秘 第8905节

8905.出埃及记20:13.不可偷盗表不可将人的属灵良善或财产从他那里夺走，不可将属于主的事物归于自己。

Potts(1905-1910) 8905

8905. Verse 13. Thou shalt not steal, signifies that no one's spiritual goods must be taken away from him, and that those things which belong to the Lord are not to be attributed to self.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8905

8905. Verse 15 You shall not steal means that no one's spiritual goods or possessions must be taken away from him, and that things which are the Lord's must not be attributed to self.

Latin(1748-1756) 8905

8905. {1} Non furaberis, significat quod non aliqui ejus bona spiritualia auferenda sint, et quod sibi non tribuenda illa quae Domini sunt. @1 See p. 252, note 4. The earlier summary in A reads: Non furaberis, significat quod sibi non vindicabitur verum et bonum, nec justitia quia sunt Domini.$
