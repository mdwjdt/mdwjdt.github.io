属天的奥秘 第1842节

1842.“你要确实知道”表这是肯定的。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1842. You must know positively means that it was certain, as is self-evident.

Potts(1905-1910) 1842

1842. Knowing thou shalt know. That this signifies that it is certain, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1842

1842. That 'know for sure' means that it is a certainty is clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 1842

1842. Quod 'cognoscendo cognosces' significet quod certum constat absque explicatione.
