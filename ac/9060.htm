属天的奥秘 第9060节

9060.“把眼打坏了”表以至于消灭它。这从“打坏”的含义清楚可知，当论述的主题是“眼”所表示的信之真理时，“打坏”是指消灭。

Potts(1905-1910) 9060

9060. And shall destroy it. That this signifies so as to extinguish it, is evident from the signification of "destroying," when the subject treated of is the truth of faith, which is signified by "the eye," as being to extinguish.

Elliott(1983-1999) 9060

9060. 'And destroys it' means so that it wipes it out. This is clear from the meaning of 'destroying', when used in reference to the truth of faith, meant by 'the eye', as wiping out.

Latin(1748-1756) 9060

9060. `Et corruperit eum': quod significet ut exstinxerit, constat ex significatione `corrumpere' cum de vero fidei, (d)quod significatur per `oculum,' quod sit exstinguere.
