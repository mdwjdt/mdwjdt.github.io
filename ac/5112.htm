属天的奥秘 第5112节

5112.“在梦里”表预言。这从“梦”的含义清楚可知，“梦”是指预见，和由此而来的预言，如前所述（5091，5092，5104节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5112

5112. In my dream. That this signifies prediction, is evident from the signification of a "dream," as being foresight and prediction therefrom (of which above, n. 5091, 5092, 5104).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5112

5112. 'in my dream' means a foretelling. This is clear from the meaning of 'a dream' as foresight, and from this a foretelling, dealt with above in 5091, 5092, 5104.

Latin(1748-1756) 5112

5112. `In somnio meo': quod significet praedictionem constat ex significatione `somnii' quod sit praevidentia, et ex illa praedictio, de qua supra n. 5091, 5092, 5104.
