属天的奥秘 第4886节

4886.“由他的朋友亚杜兰人经手”表通过虚假。这从“亚杜兰人希拉”的代表清楚可知，“亚杜兰人希拉”，就是“犹大的朋友”是指虚假（4817，4854节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 4886

4886. By the hand of his companion the Adullamite. That this signifies by falsity, is evident from the representation of Hirah the Adullamite, who was the "companion" of Judah, as being falsity (n. 4817, 4854).

Elliott(1983-1999) 4886

4886. 'By the hand of his companion the Adullamite' means by means of falsity. This is clear from the representation of 'Hirah the Adullamite' - who was Judah's companion - as falsity, dealt with in 4817, 4854.

Latin(1748-1756) 4886

4886. `In manu socii sui Adullamitae': quod significet per falsum, constat ex repraesentatione `Hirae Adullamitae' qui socius Jehudae quod sit falsum, de qua n. 4817, 4854.
