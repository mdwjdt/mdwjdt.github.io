Potts(1905-1910) 2978

2978. After this. That this signifies that it was so, is evident from the series, for here is the conclusion, namely, that they were regenerated, and thus a new spiritual church was set up again.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2978

2978. 'After this' means that it was so. This is clear from the train of thought, for this is the conclusion, that is to say, they had now been regenerated and so a new spiritual Church had been established.

Latin(1748-1756) 2978

2978. `Post ita': quod significet quod sic, constat a serie, nam hic est conclusio, quod nempe regenerati, et quod sic nova Ecclesia spiritualis instaurata.
