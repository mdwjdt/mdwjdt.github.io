属天的奥秘 第9508节

9508.“宽一肘半”表就真理而言的完整或完全之物。这一点也可见于前文（9488节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9508

9508. And a cubit and a half the breadth thereof, signifies full in respect to truth, as may also be seen above (n. 9488).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9508

9508. 'And a cubit and a half its breadth' means what is complete so far as truth is concerned, also as above, in 9488.

Latin(1748-1756) 9508

9508. `Et cubitus et dimidius latitudo ejus': quod significet plenum quoad verum, ut quoque supra n. 9488.
