属天的奥秘 第1225节

1225.前面（9:18往后）已阐述和说明，“闪”表内在教会。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1225. The symbolism of Shem as the inner church was stated and illustrated in the last chapter, at verse 18 and later verses [1062, 1083, 1091, 1096:2, 1098, 1102:1, 3].

Potts(1905-1910) 1225

1225. That by "Shem" is signified an internal church, has been stated and shown in the preceding chapter, at the 18th and following verses.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1225

1225. That 'Shem' means the internal Church has been stated and shown in verse 18 onwards of the previous chapter.

Latin(1748-1756) 1225

1225. Quod per 'Shemum' significetur Ecclesia interna, dictum et ostensum est capite praecedente vers. 18 et seq.
