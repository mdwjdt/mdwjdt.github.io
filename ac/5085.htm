属天的奥秘 第5085节

5085.“放在监里”表在虚假当中。这从“监里”的含义清楚可知，“监里”是指对虚假的消磨，因而是指虚假本身（4958，5037，5038节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5085

5085. Unto the prison house. That this signifies among falsities, is evident from the signification of a "prison house," as being the vastation of falsity, and hence falsity (n. 4958, 5037, 5038).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5085

5085. 'At the prison-house' means among falsities. This is clear from the meaning of 'the prison-house' as the vastation of falsity, and consequently as falsity itself, dealt with in 4958, 5037, 5038.

Latin(1748-1756) 5085

5085. `Ad domum carceris': quod significet inter falsa, constat ex significatione `domus carceris' quod sit vastatio falsi, et inde falsum, de qua n. 4958, 5037, 5038.
