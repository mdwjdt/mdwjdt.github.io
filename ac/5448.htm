属天的奥秘 第5448节

5448.“我要这样试试你们”表要看看事情是不是这样。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 5448

5448. Hereby ye shall be proved. That this signifies that it will be seen whether it is so, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5448

5448. 'In this manner you will be tested' means it will be seen whether this is so. This is clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 5448

5448. `In hoc probabimini': quod significet num ita sit, videbitur, constat absque explicatione.
