属天的奥秘 第797节

797.综上所述，清楚可知“淹没大山的水”所表示的含义，即灭绝一切仁之善的虚假信念。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]797. From all this it can now be seen what the water that covered the mountains symbolizes: the fact that persuasive lies wiped out all the good effects of charity.

Potts(1905-1910) 797

797. From all this it is now evident what is signified by the "waters with which the mountains were covered" namely, persuasions of falsity, which extinguished all the good of charity.

Elliott(1983-1999) 797

797. From all these considerations it is now clear what is meant by 'the waters with which the mountains were covered', namely that persuasions of falsity did away with all good that stemmed from charity.

Latin(1748-1756) 797

797. Ex omnibus his nunc constat quid per 'aquas quibus operti sunt montes' significetur, nempe quod persuasiones falsi exstinxerint omne bonum charitatis.
