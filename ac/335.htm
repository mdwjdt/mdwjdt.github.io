属天的奥秘 第335节

335.主题摘要：“该隐”所象征的信将仁灭绝后，主赐下一个新的信，仁藉此被植入，该信被称为“塞特”（25节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]335. At the end of the story, after the obliteration of charity by a detached faith (Cain), the Lord granted a new kind of faith — one through which charity would be introduced. That faith is Seth (verse 25).

Potts(1905-1910) 335

335. A summary of the subject is given: that after faith, signified by "Cain" had extinguished charity, a new faith was given by the Lord, whereby charity was implanted. This faith is called "Seth" (verse 25).

Elliott(1983-1999) 335

335. A Summary of these matters is then presented: after separated faith, meant by Cain, had annihilated charity, new faith was provided by the Lord, by means of which charity was implanted. This faith is Seth, verse 25.

Latin(1748-1756) 335

335. Summa rerum traditur; quod postquam fides separata exstinxerit charitatem, quod est 'Cain,' nova fides a Domino donata, per quam implantabatur charitas; quae fides est 'Sheth,' vers. 25.
