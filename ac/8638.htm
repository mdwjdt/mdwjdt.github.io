属天的奥秘 第8638节

8638.这些和更多的事都是要重生之人所必须知道的信之事务；因为知道它们的人就会思考它们，然后意愿它们，最后行出它们，从而拥有新生命。

Potts(1905-1910) 8638

8638. These things and more are of faith, which must be known by the man who is to be regenerated; for he who knows them can think them, then will them, and lastly do them, and thus have new life.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8638

8638. These and many more are the matters of faith which a person who is to be regenerated ought to know. For one who knows them is able to think about them, then intend them, and go on at length to do them, and so possess the new life.

Latin(1748-1756) 8638

8638. Haec et plura sunt fidei, quae homo qui regenerandus est scire debet, nam qui scit illa cogitare potest illa, dein velle illa, et demum facere illa, et sic habere vitam novam.
