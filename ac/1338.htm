属天的奥秘 第1338节

1338.“并且生儿育女”表教义。这从前面论述的“儿子”的含义清楚可知（264，489-491，533节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1338. The symbolism of fathering sons and daughters as doctrinal matters can be seen from the symbolism of offspring above at 264, 489, 490, 491, 533.

Potts(1905-1910) 1338

1338. And begat sons and daughters. That this signifies doctrinal things, is evident from the signification of "sons," as before given (n. 264, 489-491, 533).

Elliott(1983-1999) 1338

1338. That 'begetting sons and daughters' means matters of doctrine is clear from the meaning of 'sons', dealt with already in 264, 489-491, 533.

Latin(1748-1756) 1338

1338. Quod 'gignere filios et filias' significet doctrinalia, constat ex significatione 'filiorum,' prius n. 264, 489-491, 533.
