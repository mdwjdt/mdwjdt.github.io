属天的奥秘 第1087节

1087.“遮盖父亲的赤身”表他们就这样原谅这些事。这一点同样从上下文联系和“赤身”表堕落的含义清楚看出来。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1087. The fact that they covered their father's nakedness means that in doing so they excused those wrongs can again be seen from context. It is also established by the symbolism of nakedness as perversions.

Potts(1905-1910) 1087

1087. And covered the nakedness of their father. That this signifies that they excused them, is evident both from the connection, and from the signification of "nakedness" that is, perversions.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1087

1087. They covered their father's nakedness' means that in this way they excused those things. This is equally clear from the train of thought, and also from the meaning of 'nakedness' as perversities.

Latin(1748-1756) 1087

1087. Quod 'texerunt nuditatem patris sui' significet quod sic ea excusaverint, constat pariter ex serie, tum a significatione 'nuditatis' quod sint perversa.
