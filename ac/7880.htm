属天的奥秘 第7880节

7880.“我击打埃及地的时候”表当那些陷入与仁分离之信的人受到诅咒时。这从前面所述（参看7871节）清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 7880

7880. When I smite the land of Egypt. That this signifies when they are damned who are in faith separate from charity, is evident from what was said above (see n. 7871).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7880

7880. 'When I strike the land of Egypt' means when those governed by faith separated from charity are damned. This is clear from what has been stated above in 7871.

Latin(1748-1756) 7880

7880. `In percutere Me terram Aegypti': quod significet cum damnantur illi qui in fide separata a charitate, constat ex illis supra n.7871.
