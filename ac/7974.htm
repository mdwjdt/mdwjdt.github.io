属天的奥秘 第7974节

7974.“除了小孩子”表纯真的良善。这从“小孩子”的含义清楚可知，“小孩子”是指纯真的良善（参看430，1616，2126，2305，3183，3494，4797，5608节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7974

7974. Besides babe. That this signifies the good of innocence, is evident from the signification of an "infant," as being the good of innocence (see n. 430, 1616, 2126, 2305, 3183, 3494, 4797, 5608).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7974

7974. 'besides young children' means the good of innocence. This is clear from the meaning of 'young child' as the good of innocence, dealt with in 430, 1616, 2126, 2305, 3183, 3494, 4797.

Latin(1748-1756) 7974

7974. `Praeter infantem': quod significet bonum innocentiae, constat ex significatione `infantis' quod sit bonum innocentiae, de qua n. 430, 1616, 2126, 2305, 3183, 3494, 4797, 5608.
