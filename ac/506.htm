属天的奥秘 第506节

506.创世记5:12 该南活到七十岁，生了玛勒列。
“该南”表第四个教会，“玛勒列”是第五个。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]506. Genesis 5:12. And Kenan lived seventy years and fathered Mahalalel.
Kenan symbolizes the fourth church and Mahalalel the fifth.

Potts(1905-1910) 506

506. Verse 12. And Kenan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalalel. By "Kenan" is signified a fourth church, and by "Mahalalel" a fifth.

Elliott(1983-1999) 506

506. Verse it And Kenan lived seventy years; and he beget Mahalalel. 'Kenan' means a fourth Church, and 'Mahalelel' a fifth.

Latin(1748-1756) 506

506. Vers. 12. Et vixit Kenan septuaginta annis, et genuit Mahalalel. Per 'Kenan' significatur quarta Ecclesia: et per 'Mahalalel' quinta.
