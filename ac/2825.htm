属天的奥秘 第2825节

2825.“一点不可害他”表释放。这是显而易见的，无需解释。当他被吩咐不可对他下手时，就意味着这个行为必被中止，因此他会得到释放。

Potts(1905-1910) 2825

2825. And do not anything unto him. That this signifies liberation, is evident without explication; for when it is said that he should do nothing to him, it means that the act should be interrupted, and thus that he will be liberated.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2825

2825. 'And do not do anything to him' means release. This becomes clear without explanation, for when he is told to do nothing to him it means that the action must be discontinued, thus that he will be released.

Latin(1748-1756) 2825

2825. `Et ne facias illi quicquam': quod significet liberationem, constare potest absque explicatione; cum enim dicitur quod nihil ei faciet, est quod actus intermittetur, ita quod liberabitur.
