属天的奥秘 第1281节

1281.然后论述的是名为希伯的第二代古教会，以及它的衍生物和状态，它最终陷入偶像崇拜（10-26节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1281. Verses 10-26 deal with the second ancient church, named for Eber. They also discuss what developed out of that church and what conditions were like in it, since it ended in idolatry.

Potts(1905-1910) 1281

1281. The second Ancient Church is then treated of which was named from Eber-and its derivation and state, which finally ended in idolatry (verses 10-26).

Elliott(1983-1999) 1281

1281. The subject in verses 10-26 is the second Ancient Church called Eber, its derivation and its state, which at length sank into idolatry.

Latin(1748-1756) 1281

1281. Agitur de Ecclesia Antiqua altera quae ab Ebero nominata; ejusque derivatione et statu quae desiit tandem in idololatriam, vers. 20-26.
