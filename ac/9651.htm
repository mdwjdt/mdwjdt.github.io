9651.“一板底下有两座”表通过与良善的结合。也如前所述（9645节）。

9651. Two bases under one plank. This signifies through conjunction with good (as also above, n. 9645).

9651. 'Two bases under one board' means through a joining to good, as also above, in 9645.

9651. `Duas bases sub assere uno' significat per conjunctionem cum bono, ut quoque supra n. 9645.
