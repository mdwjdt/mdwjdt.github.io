8874.“因为我是耶和华你的神”表来自主的神性在每一个事物中。这从前面的解释清楚可知（8864，8865节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8874

8874. For I Jehovah thy God. That this signifies the Divine from the Lord in each and all things, is evident from what was unfolded above (n. 8864, 8865).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8874

8874. 'For I am Jehovah your God' means that the Divine from the Lord is in every single thing. This is clear from the explanations above in 8864, 8865.

Latin(1748-1756) 8874

8874. `Quia Ego Jehovah Deus tuus': quod [significet quod] Divinum a Domino in omnibus et singulis, constat ex illis quae supra n. 8864, 8865, explicata sunt.
