Potts(1905-1910) 2314

2314. Further: in the internal sense, by the "inhabitants of Sodom" is described the state of those within the same church who are against the good of charity, and how in course of time evil and falsity increase with them until they have nothing but evil and falsity.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2314

2314. Besides this, 'the inhabitants of Sodom' describes, in the internal sense, the state of those people inside the same Church who are averse to the good of charity, and describes how evil and falsity among them increases with the passage of time until nothing but evil and falsity are theirs.

Latin(1748-1756) 2314

2314. Porro in sensu interno per `habitatores Sodomae' describitur status eorum intra eandem Ecclesiam qui contra bonum charitatis sunt; et quomodo malum et falsum apud eos progressu temporis accrescit, usque dum iis nihil nisi malum et falsum.
