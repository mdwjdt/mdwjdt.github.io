属天的奥秘 第8178节

8178.“耶和华对摩西说”表告诫。这从“耶和华说”的含义清楚可知，当论述的主题是从试探中被提升和释放时，“耶和华说”是指告诫（参看7033，7090节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8178

8178. And Jehovah said unto Moses. That this signifies exhortation, is evident from the signification of "Jehovah said," when the subject treated of is elevation and liberation from temptation, as being exhortation (see n. 7033, 7090).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8178

8178. 'And Jehovah said to Moses' means an admonition. This is clear from the meaning of 'Jehovah said', when the subject is being raised and delivered from temptation, as an admonition, as in 7033, 7090.

Latin(1748-1756) 8178

8178. `Et dixit Jehovah ad Moschen': quod significet exhortationem, constat ex significatione `dixit Jehovah,' cum agitur de elevatione et liberatione a {1}tentatione, quod sit exhortatio, ut {2} n. 7033, 7090. @1 tentationibus$ @2 i quoque$
