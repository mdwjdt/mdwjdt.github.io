属天的奥秘 第6995节

6995.“摩西说，凭我，我主”表明确的断言。这从以下事实清楚可知，“凭我”是一个常用短语，用来表示明确断言某事就是这样，如前所述（6981节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6995

6995. And he said, In me, my Lord. That this signifies asseveration, is evident from the fact that "in me" is a form of asseveration (as above, n. 6981).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6995

6995. 'And he said, By me, Lord' means a categorical assertion. This is clear from the fact that 'by me' is a stock phrase used to assert categorically that something is so, as above in 6981.

Latin(1748-1756) 6995

6995. `Et dixit, In me, Domine': quod significet asseverationem, constat ex eo quod `in me' sit formula asseverationis quod ita sit ut supra n. 6981.
