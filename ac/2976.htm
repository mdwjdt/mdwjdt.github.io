属天的奥秘 第2976节

2976.“所有出入城门的”表在一切教义方面。这从前面所述（2943节）清楚可知，那里有相同的话。


上一节　 下一节

Potts(1905-1910) 2976

2976. Of all that went in at the gate of his city. That this signifies as to all doctrinal things, is evident from what was said above (n. 2943), where the same words occur.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2976

2976. 'In [the presence] of all entering the gate of his city' means as regards all matters of doctrine. This is clear from what has been stated above in 2943, where the same words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 2976

2976. `In omnium intrantium portam urbis ejus': quod significet quoad omnia doctrinalia, constat ab illis quae supra n. 2943 dicta sunt, ubi eadem verba.
