属天的奥秘 第5909节

5909.“请你下到我这里来，不要耽延”表一种必然的结合。这从“下来”或“下到我这里来”和“不要耽延”的含义清楚可知：“下来”或“下到我这里来”是指结合；“不要耽延”是指必然的事。

Potts(1905-1910) 5909

5909. Come down unto me, tarry not. That this signifies sure conjunction, is evident from the signification of "coming down," or "coming unto me," as being conjunction; and from the signification of "tarry not," as being what is sure.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5909

5909. 'Come down to me, do not delay' means a sure joining together. This is clear from the meaning of 'coming down to me' as a joining together; and from the meaning of 'do not delay' as that which is sure.

Latin(1748-1756) 5909

5909. `Descende ad me, ne subsistas': quod significet conjunctionem certam, constat ex significatione `descendere' seu venire ad me quod sit conjunctio, et a significatione `ne subsistas' quod sit certum.
