属天的奥秘 第6962节

6962.“及至抽出来”表由此所看到的东西。这从“从怀里抽出手并看见”的含义清楚可知，“从怀里抽出手并看见”是指发现它是何性质，因而是指由此所看见的东西。

Potts(1905-1910) 6962

6962. And he brought it out. That this signifies that it was thence, is evident from the signification of "bringing the hand out of the bosom and seeing" as being to observe what was its quality, thus that it was thence.

Elliott(1983-1999) 6962

6962. 'And took it out' means what was seen from this. This is clear from the meaning of 'taking out the hand from one's bosom and seeing' as a recognition of what it is like, thus what was seen from this.

Latin(1748-1756) 6962

6962. `Et eduxit illam': quod significet quod inde, constat ex significatione `educere manum e sinu et videre' {1} quod sit animadvertere qualis est, ita quod inde. @1 i qualis est$
