属天的奥秘 第4920节

4920.“一个孩子伸出一只手来”表能力。这从“手”的含义清楚可知，“手”是指能力（878，3387节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 4920

4920. And one put out a hand. That this signifies power, is evident from the signification of a "hand," as being power (n. 878, 3387).

Elliott(1983-1999) 4920

4920. 'That one put out a hand' means power. This is clear from the meaning of 'a hand' as power, dealt with in 878, 3387.

Latin(1748-1756) 4920

4920. `Et dedit manum': quod significet potentiam, constat ex significatione `manus' quod sit potentia, de qua n. 878, 3387.
