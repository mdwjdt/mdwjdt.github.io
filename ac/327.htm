属天的奥秘 第327节

327.属分离之信者变得邪恶，他们的状态以“该隐被大大地激怒，脸沉了下来”来形容（5-6节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]327. Cain's burning anger and fallen face portrayed a change for the worse in the condition of those who latched on to a separated faith (verses 5, 6).

Potts(1905-1910) 327

327. That the state of those who were of separated faith became evil, is described by Cain's "anger being kindled, and his countenance falling" (verses 5, 6).

Elliott(1983-1999) 327

327. The state of those who belonged to separated faith altered to a state that was evil. This is described as Cain's anger blazing and his facea falling, verses 5, 6.

Notes

a lit. faces

Latin(1748-1756) 327

327. Quod mutatus in malum status eorum qui ex fide separata fuerunt, describitur per 'iram accensam, et cadere facies Caino, vers. 5, 6.
