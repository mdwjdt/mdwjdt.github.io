属天的奥秘 第8791节

8791.“因为第三天”表因为在最后，就是他们准备好接受的时候。这从刚才的阐述清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 8791

8791. Because in the third day. That this signifies because in the end when they are prepared to receive, is evident from what has just now been shown.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8791

8791. 'For on the third day' means because at the end, when they have been prepared to receive ... This is clear from what has been stated immediately above.

Latin(1748-1756) 8791

8791. `Quia in die tertio': quod significet quoniam in fine cum praeparati ad recipiendum, constat ex illis nunc supra.
