属天的奥秘 第6529节

6529.“只有他们的小孩子”表纯真。这从“小孩子”的含义清楚可知，“小孩子”是指纯真（参看430，3183，5608节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6529

6529. Only their babes. That this signifies innocence, is evident from the signification of "babes," as being innocence (see n. 430, 3183, 5608).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6529

6529. 'Only their young children' means innocence. This is clear from the meaning of 'young children' as innocence, dealt with in 430, 3183, 5608.

Latin(1748-1756) 6529

6529. `Tantum infantes suos': quod significet innocentiam, constat ex significatione `infantum' quod sint innocentia, de qua n. 430, 3183, 5608.
