属天的奥秘 第1804节

1804．“才成为你的后嗣”表这些人将成为后嗣。这从前面所论述“后嗣”的含义清楚可知。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1804. Will be your heir means that they would become the heirs, as is established by the symbolism of an heir, already discussed.

Potts(1905-1910) 1804

1804. He shall be thine heir. That this signifies that they shall become heirs, is evident from the signification of an "heir," already treated of.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1804

1804. That 'he will be your heir' means that these will become the heirs is clear from the meaning of 'an heir', dealt with already.

Latin(1748-1756) 1804

1804. Quod 'ille hereditabit' significet quod illi heredes fient, constat ex significatione heredis, de qua actum.
