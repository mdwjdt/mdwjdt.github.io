属天的奥秘 第835节

835.第三状态是当他开始通过仁爱行事与思维的时候，也就是重生之人的第一状态（15-19节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]835. The third state, when they start acting and thinking in terms of charity; this is the first state the reborn person goes through (verses 15-19).

Potts(1905-1910) 835

835. His third state, when he begins to act and think from charity, which is the first state of the regenerate (verses 15 to 19).

Elliott(1983-1999) 835

835. The third state is when he starts to act and think from charity, which is the first state of a regenerate person, verses 15-19.

Latin(1748-1756) 835

835. De Tertio statu, cum ex charitate incipit agere et cogitare, qui est status primus regenerati, vers. 15-19.
