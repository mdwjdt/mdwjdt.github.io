属天的奥秘 第2582节

2582.“和他的妻子”表在真理方面。这从“妻子”的含义清楚可知，“妻子”是指真理（参看2581节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 2582

2582. And his wife. That this signifies in respect to truth, is evident from the signification of a "wife," as being truth (see just above, n. 2581).

Elliott(1983-1999) 2582

2582. 'And his wife' means as regards truth. This is clear from the meaning of 'a wife' as truth, dealt with immediately above in 2581.

Latin(1748-1756) 2582

2582. `Et uxorem ejus': quod significet quoad verum, constat a significatione `uxoris' quod sit verum, de qua mox supra, n. 2581.
