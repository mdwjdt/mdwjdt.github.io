属天的奥秘 第9775节

9775.“座要用铜作”表一切都通过良善所获得的支撑。如前所述（9770节）。之所以表示一切，是因为本节论述的是形成院子的一切。

Potts(1905-1910) 9775

9775. And their bases of brass, signifies the supports of all things by means of good (as above, n. 9770). That it denotes of all things, is because all the things of the court are treated of in this verse.

Elliott(1983-1999) 9775

9775. 'And their bases from bronze' means the support they all receive through good, as above in 9770. The reason why all are meant is that this verse refers to all things forming the court.

Latin(1748-1756) 9775

9775. `Et bases illorum aere' significat sustentationes omnium per bonum, ut supra n. 9770; quod sint {1} omnium, est quia in hoc versu agitur de omnibus atrii. @1 i sustentationes$
