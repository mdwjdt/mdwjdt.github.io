Potts(1905-1910) 5358

5358. And the seven years of abundance of produce were ended. That this signifies after the states of the multiplication of truth, is evident from what was unfolded above (n. 5276, 5292, 5339), where similar words occur.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5358

5358. 'And the seven years of abundance of corn ended' means after those states during which truth was multiplied. This is clear from the matters explained above in 5276, 5292, 5339, where similar words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 5358

5358. `Et absoluti septem anni abundantiae annonae': quod significet post status multiplicationis veri, constat ex illis quae supra n. 5276, 5292, 5339 explicata sunt, ubi similia verba.
