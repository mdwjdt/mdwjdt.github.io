4743.“他们拿住约瑟，把他丢在坑里”表在虚假当中。这从前面所述（4728，4736节）清楚可知，那里有同样的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 4743

4743. And they took him, and cast him into the pit. That this signifies among falsities, is evident from what has been said above (n. 4728, 4736), where similar words occur.

Elliott(1983-1999) 4743

4743. 'And they took him and threw him into the pit' means among falsities. This is clear from what has been stated above in 4728, 4736, where similar words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 4743

4743. `Et sumpserunt eum, et miserunt eum in foveam': quod significet inter falsa, constat ex illis quae supra n. 4728, 4736 dicta sunt, ubi similia.
