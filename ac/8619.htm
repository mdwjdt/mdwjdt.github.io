属天的奥秘 第8619节

8619.“耶和华对摩西说”表指示。这从“说”的含义清楚可知，“说”包含接下来的事；由于摩西在此被指示必须要做的事，故“说”表示指示。

Potts(1905-1910) 8619

8619. And Jehovah said unto Moses. That this signifies instruction, is evident from the signification of "saying," as involving the things that follow; and because Moses is here instructed what must be done, it is instruction that is signified.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8619

8619. 'And Jehovah said to Moses' means instruction. This is clear from the meaning of 'saying', which includes what comes after it; and since this consists of instructions telling Moses what he had to do, instruction is what is meant by 'saying'.

Latin(1748-1756) 8619

8619. `Et dixit Jehovah ad Moschen': quod significet instructionem, constat ex significatione `dicere,' quod involvat sequentia, et quia ibi instruitur Moscheh {1}quod faciendum, est instructio quae significatur. @1 quid faciendum cum Amaleko$
