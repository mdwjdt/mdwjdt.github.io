属天的奥秘 第7961节

7961.“也依你们所说的”表照着他们的意愿。如刚才所述（7959节）。

7961. As ye spoke, signifies according to the will (as just above, n. 7959).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7961

7961. 'As you have spoken' means in accordance with their will, as just above in 7959.

Latin(1748-1756) 7961

7961. `Quemadmodum locuti estis' significat secundum voluntatem, ut mox supra n. (x)7959.
