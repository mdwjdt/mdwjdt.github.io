属天的奥秘 第10700节

10700.“摩西与他们说完了话”表关于将以外在形式来代表的首要真理的教导发出之后。这从刚才的说明（10699节）清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 10700

10700. And Moses left off from speaking with them. That this signifies after information concerning the primary truths which were to be represented in external things is evident from what has been said just above (n. 10699).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10700

10700. 'And Moses finished talking to them' means after the instructions regarding the primary truths which were to be represented in outward forms had been delivered. This is clear from what has been stated immediately above in 10699.

Latin(1748-1756) 10700

10700. `Et absolvit Moscheh a loquendo cum illis': quod significet post informationem de primariis veris quae in externis repraesentanda, constat ex illis quae nunc supra n. 10699 dicta sunt.
