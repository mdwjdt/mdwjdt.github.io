属天的奥秘 第4860节

4860.“又遮住身体”表因而得不到承认。这从“遮住”的含义清楚可知，“遮住”是指隐藏，因而是指得不到承认，参看刚才所述（4859节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 4860

4860. And wrapped herself. That this signifies thus not acknowledged, is evident from the signification of "wrapping," as being to conceal, thus not to be acknowledged-see what was said just above (n. 4859).

Elliott(1983-1999) 4860

4860. 'And concealed herself' means and so was not acknowledged. This is clear from the meaning of 'concealing' as hiding away, and so as not being acknowledged. See what is said immediately above in 4859.

Latin(1748-1756) 4860

4860. `Et operuit se': quod significet sic non agnitum, constat ex significatione `operire' quod sit abscondere, ita non agnosci, videantur quae mox supra n. 4859.
