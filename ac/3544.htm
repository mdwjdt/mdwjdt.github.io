属天的奥秘 第3544节

3544.“和饼”表来自这个源头的良善。这从“饼”的含义清楚可知，“饼”是指良善（参看276，680，1798，2165，2177，3464，3478节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 3544

3544. And the bread. That this signifies the good thence derived, is evident from the signification of "bread," as being good (see n. 276, 680, 1798, 2165, 2177, 3464, 3478).

Elliott(1983-1999) 3544

3544. 'And bread' means good from that source. This is clear from the meaning of 'bread' as good, dealt with in 276, 680, 1798, 2165, 2177, 3464, 3478.

Latin(1748-1756) 3544

3544. `Et panem': quod significet bonum inde, constat (c)ex significatione `panis' quod sit bonum, de qua n. 276, 680, 1798, 2165, 2177, 3464, 3478.
