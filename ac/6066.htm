Potts(1905-1910) 6066

6066. And all that they have, signifies whatever is thence derived (as above, n. 6046).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6066

6066. 'And all that they have' means whatever is dependent on them, as above in 6046.

Latin(1748-1756) 6066

6066. `Et omnia quae illis': quod significet quicquid inde est, supra n. 6046.
