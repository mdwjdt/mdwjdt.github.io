属天的奥秘 第6520节

6520.“照着他叫你起的誓”表因为他把它放在心上。这从“起的誓”的含义清楚可知，“起的誓”是指放在心上（参看6514节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6520

6520. According as he made thee swear. That this signifies because this is at heart, is evident from the signification of "making to swear," as being to have at heart (see n. 6514).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6520

6520. 'As he made you swear on oath' means because he had it at heart. This is clear from the meaning of 'making to swear on oath' as having at heart, dealt with above in 6514.

Latin(1748-1756) 6520

6520. `Quemadmodum adjuravit te': quod significet quia illa cordi, constat ex significatione `adjurare' quod sit cordi esse, de qua supra n. 6514.
