属天的奥秘 第9566节

9566.“因为有六个枝子从灯台出来”表就属灵天堂中的一切事物而言，源于良善的真理的能力。如前所述（9561节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9566

9566. For the six reeds going out of the lampstand, signifies the power of truth from good in respect to all things in the spiritual heaven (as above, n. 9561).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9566

9566. 'For the six branches going out of the lampstand' means the power of truth derived from good so far as all things in the spiritual heaven are concerned, as above in 9561.

Latin(1748-1756) 9566

9566. `Sex calamis exeuntibus ex candelabro' significat potentiam veri ex bono quoad omnia in caelo spirituali, ut supra n. 9561.
