3555. How is it that thou hast hastened to find it, my son? That this signifies production so speedy, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 3555

3555. 'Why have you found it so quickly, my son?' means so rapid a production. This is clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 3555

3555. `Quid hoc festinasti invenire, fili mi?': quod significet productionem tam festinam, constat absque explicatione.
