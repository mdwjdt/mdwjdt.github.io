属天的奥秘 第9774节

9774.“帷子要用捻的细麻作”表出于理解力。这从“捻的细麻”的含义清楚可知，“捻的细麻”是指理解力（参看9596，9744节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9774

9774. Of fine twined linen. That this signifies from the understanding, is evident from the signification of "fine twined linen," as being what belongs to the understanding (see n. 9596, 9744).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9774

9774. '[With hangings made] from fine twined linen' means coming out of the understanding. This is clear from the meaning of 'fine twined linen' as the understanding, dealt with in 9596, 9744.

Latin(1748-1756) 9774

9774. `Byssino contexto': quod significet ex intellectuali, constat ex significatione `byssini contexti' quod sit intellectuale, de qua n. 9596, 9744.
