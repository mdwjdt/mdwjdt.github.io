属天的奥秘 第7526节

7526.“他们取了炉灰”表恶欲的虚假。这从“炉灰”的含义清楚可知，“炉灰”是指恶欲的虚假，如前所述（7519节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7526

7526. And they took ashes of the furnace. That this signifies the falsities of cupidities, is evident from the signification of "ashes of the furnace," as being the falsities of cupidities (of which above, n. 7519).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7526

7526. 'And they took the ashes of the furnace' means the falsities that are a product of evil desires. This is clear from the meaning of 'the ashes of the furnace' as falsities that are a product of evil desires, dealt with above in 7519.

Latin(1748-1756) 7526

7526. `Et acceperunt favillam fornacis': quod significet falsa cupiditatum, constat ex significatione `favillae fornacis' quod sint falsa cupiditatum, de qua supra n. 7519.
