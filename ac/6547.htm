属天的奥秘 第6547节

6547.“将他埋葬”表在那里复活得生命。这从“埋葬”的含义清楚可，“埋葬”是指复活得生命（参看5551，6516节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6547

6547. And buried him. That this signifies resuscitation there, is evident from the signification of "being buried," as being resuscitation (see n. 5551, 6516).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6547

6547. 'And buried him' means restoration to life there. This is clear from the meaning of 'burying' as restoration to life, dealt with in 5551, 6516.

Latin(1748-1756) 6547

6547. `Et sepeliverunt illum': quod significet resuscitationem ibi, constat ex significatione `sepeliri' quod sit resuscitatio, de qua n. 5551, 6516.
