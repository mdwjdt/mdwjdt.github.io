属天的奥秘 第8319节

8319.“因你膀臂的巨大”表由于全能。这从“膀臂”的含义清楚可知，“膀臂”是指能力（参看878，4931-4937节），当论及神性时，是指全能（7205节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8319

8319. In the greatness of Thine arm. That this signifies by virtue of omnipotence, is evident from the signification of "arm," as being power (see n. 878, 4931-4937), and when said of the Divine, as being omnipotence.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8319

8319. 'By the greatness of Your arm' means as a result of almighty power. This is clear from the meaning of 'arm' as power, dealt with in 878, 4931-4937, and when used in connection with the Divine, as almighty power, 7205.

Latin(1748-1756) 8319

8319. `In magnitudine brachii (x)Tui': quod significet ex omnipotentia, constat ex significatione `brachii' quod sit potentia, de qua n. 878, 4931-4937 {1}; et cum dicitur de Divino, quod sit omnipotentia, n. 7205. @1 i, 4932, 4934, 4935$
