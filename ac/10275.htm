属天的奥秘 第10275节

10275.“洗濯盆和盆座”表与从邪恶和虚假中洁净，以及被主重生有关的一切。这从“洗濯盆”和“盆座”的含义清楚可知：在其中进行洗濯的“洗濯盆”是指从邪恶和虚假中洁净，以及重生的手段（参看10235节）；“盆座”是指提供支撑并作为仆人进行事奉的感官层（10236节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10275

10275. And the laver, and its base. That this signifies all things that belong to purification from evils and falsities, and to regeneration, by the Lord, is evident from the signification of "the laver," in which washing was performed, as being a means of purification from evils and falsities, and also of regeneration (see n. 10235); and from the signification of its "base," as being the sensuous that supports and ministers (n. 10236).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10275

10275. 'And the laver and its pedestal' means everything connected with purification from evils and falsities, and with regeneration by the Lord. This is clear from the meaning of 'the laver', in which washing takes place, as the means of purification from evils and falsities, and also of regeneration, dealt with in 10235; and from the meaning of 'its pedestal' as the level of the senses, providing support and acting as a servant, dealt with in 10236.

Latin(1748-1756) 10275

10275. `Et labrum et basin ejus': quod significet omnia quae sunt purificationis a malis et falsis, ac regenerationis a Domino, constat ex significatione `labri in quo lavatio' quod sit {1}purificatorium a malis et falsis, et quoque {2}regeneratorium, de qua n. 10,235, et ex significatione `baseos ejus' quod sit sensuale sustentans et ministrans, de qua n. 10,236. @1 purificatio$ @2 regeneratio$
