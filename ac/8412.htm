属天的奥秘 第8412节

8412.“到这旷野”表一个试探的状态。这从“旷野”的含义清楚可知，“旷野”是指经历试探时的一个状态（参看8098节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8412

8412. Unto this wilderness. That this signifies a state of temptations, is evident from the signification of a "wilderness," as being a state of undergoing temptations (see n. 8098).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8412

8412. 'To this wilderness' means a state involving temptations. This is clear from the meaning of 'the wilderness' as a state when temptations have to be undergone, dealt with in 8098.

Latin(1748-1756) 8412

8412. `Ad desertum hoc': quod significet statum tentationum, constat ex significatione `deserti' quod sit status {1}subeundi tentationes, de qua n. 8098. @1 tentationum$
