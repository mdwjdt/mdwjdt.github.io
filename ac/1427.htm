属天的奥秘 第1427节

1427.“亚伯兰就照着耶和华吩咐他的去了”表向着神性事物发展。这从刚才所述明显可知。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1427. The symbolism of and Abram went, as Jehovah had spoken to him, as progress toward divine characteristics can be seen from the remarks just made.

Potts(1905-1910) 1427

1427. And Abram went as Jehovah spoke unto him. That this signifies progression toward Divine things, is evident from what has just been said.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1427

1427. 'And Abram went, as Jehovah had told him' means an advance towards Divine things. This is clear from what has just been stated.

Latin(1748-1756) 1427

1427. 'Et ivit Abram, quemadmodum ad illum locutus Jehovah': quod significet progressionem ad Divina, ex illis quae nunc dicta sunt, constat.
