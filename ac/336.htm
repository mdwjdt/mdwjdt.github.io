属天的奥秘 第336节

336.通过此信被植入的仁叫做“以挪士”或另一个“人”，这是该教会的名（26节）。


上一节　 下一节

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]336. Charity introduced by means of faith is called Enosh, or "another person,"{*1} which is the name of that church (verse 26).

Footnotes:
{*1} The Hebrew name represented by "Enosh," אֱנוֹשׁ ('ĕnôš), is a word meaning "person." [LHC]

Potts(1905-1910) 336

336. The charity implanted by faith is called "Enosh" or another "man" [homo], which is the name of that church (verse 26).

Elliott(1983-1999) 336

336. Charity that has been implanted by means of faith is called Enosh, that is, a different man [from the one before]. This is the name of that Church, verse 26.

Latin(1748-1756) 336

336. Charitas implantata per fidem, vocatur 'Enosh,' seu homo alius, quod nomen est istius Ecclesiae, vers. 26.
