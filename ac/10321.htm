属天的奥秘 第10321节

10321.圣言因是来自神的一种启示，故是神性，无论总体还是具体；因为从神而来的，不可能是别的东西。


上一节　 下一节

Potts(1905-1910) 10321

10321. As the Word is a revelation from the Divine, it is Divine in general and in particular, for that which is from the Divine cannot be otherwise.

Elliott(1983-1999) 10321

10321. Since the Word is a revelation coming from God it is Divine in each individual part; for what comes from God cannot be anything other.

Latin(1748-1756) 10321

10321. Verbum quia est revelatio a Divino, est Divinum in omnibus et singulis, nam quod a Divino est non potest aliter esse.
