属天的奥秘 第9045节

9045.“他要受罚款”表修正。这从“受罚款”的含义清楚可知，“受罚款”是指修正，因为罚款是为了修正而征收的。

Potts(1905-1910) 9045

9045. With fining he shall be fined. That this signifies amendment, is evident from the signification of "to be fined," as being amendment, for a fine is for the sake of amendment.

Elliott(1983-1999) 9045

9045. '[The one inflicting the blow] shall surely be fined' means amendment. This is clear from the meaning of 'fining' as amending; for a fine is imposed for the sake of amendment.

Latin(1748-1756) 9045

9045. `Mulctando mulctabit': quod significet emendationem, constat ex significatione `mulctare' quod sit {1}emendatio, nam `mulcta' est propter emendationem. @1 emendare$
