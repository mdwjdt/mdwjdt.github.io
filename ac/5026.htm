属天的奥秘 第5026节

5026.“要调戏我”表它起来。这从“调戏”的含义清楚可知，“调戏”是指起来，如前所述（5014节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5026

5026. To mock me. That this signifies that it rose up, is evident from the signification of "mocking," as being to rise up (as above, n. 5014).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5026

5026. 'To make sport of me' means that it rose up. This is clear from the meaning of 'making sport of' as rising up, as above in 5014.

Latin(1748-1756) 5026

5026. `Ad illudendum mihi': quod significet quod insurgeret, constat ex significatione `illudere' quod sit insurgere, ut supra n. 5014.
