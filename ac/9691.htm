属天的奥秘 第9691节

9691.“柱子上的钩子要用金的”表通过良善将它们联结起来的方式。如前所述（9676节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9691

9691. And their hooks shall be of gold. That this signifies the methods of conjunction by means of good, may be seen above (n. 9676).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9691

9691. 'And their hooks [shall be made] from gold' means the modes by which they are linked together through good, as above in 9676.

Latin(1748-1756) 9691

9691. `Et unci earum auro': quod significet conjunctionis modos per bonum, ut supra n. 9676.
