属天的奥秘 第10347节

10347.“和他儿子的衣服”表低级属灵事物的一个代表。这从“亚伦的儿子和他们的衣服”的含义清楚可知，“亚伦的儿子和他们的衣服”是低级属灵事物的一个代表（参看10068节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10347

10347. And the garments of his sons. That this signifies a representative of lower spiritual things, is evident from the representation of the sons of Aaron and their garments, as being a representative of lower spiritual things (see n. 10068).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10347

10347. 'And the garments of his sons' means that which is representative of lower spiritual things. This is clear from the representation of 'Aaron's sons and their garments' as that which is representative of lower spiritual things, dealt with in 10068.

Latin(1748-1756) 10347

10347. `Et vestes filiorum ejus': quod significet repraesentativum spiritualium inferiorum, constat ex repraesentatione `filiorum Aharonis ac vestium illorum' quod sint repraesentativum spiritualium inferiorum, de qua n. 10,068.
