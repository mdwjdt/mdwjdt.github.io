属天的奥秘 第2419节

2419.“要往山上逃跑”表向着爱与仁所流入的良善。这从“山”的含义清楚可知，“山”是指爱与仁（参看795，1430节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 2419

2419. Escape to the mountain. That this signifies to the good of love and of charity, is evident from the signification of a "mountain," as being love and charity (see n. 795, 1430).

Elliott(1983-1999) 2419

2419. 'Escape into the mountain' means towards the good that flows from love and charity. This is clear from the meaning of 'a mountain' as love and charity, dealt with in 795, 1430.

Latin(1748-1756) 2419

2419. `In montem eripe te': quod significet ad bonum amoris et charitatis, constat a significatione `montis' quod sit amor et charitas. de qua n. 795, 1430.
