属天的奥秘 第6594节

6594.“正一百一十岁”表那时的状态。这从“一百一十岁”的含义清楚可知，“一百一十岁”是指来自内在的记忆知识生命的状态和性质（参看6582节）。“岁（即年）”是指状态（参看487，488，493节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6594

6594. A son of a hundred and ten years. That this signifies the state then, is evident from the signification of a "hundred and ten years," as being the state and quality of the life of memory-knowledges from the internal (see n. 6582). (That "years" denote states, see n. 487, 488, 493.)

Elliott(1983-1999) 6594

6594. 'A hundred and ten years old' means the state at that time. This is clear from the meaning of 'a hundred and ten years' as the state and essential nature of the life of factual knowledge from the internal, dealt with in 6582 - 'years' being states, see 487, 488, 497.

Latin(1748-1756) 6594

6594. `Filius centum et decem annorum': quod significet statum tunc, constat ex significatione `centum et decem annorum' quod sint status et quale vitae scientificorum ab interno, de qua n. 6582; quod `anni' (x)sint status, videatur n. 487, 488, 493.
