属天的奥秘 第4485节

4485.“成为一样的人民”表在教义上。这从“人民”的含义清楚可知，“人民”是指教义（参看4468节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 4485

4485. To be for one people. That this signifies in respect to the doctrine, is evident from the signification of "people," as being doctrine (see also above, n. 4468).

Elliott(1983-1999) 4485

4485. 'To be one people' means in doctrine. This is clear from the meaning of 'people' as doctrine, also dealt with above, in 4468.

Latin(1748-1756) 4485

4485. `Ad esse in populum unum': quod significet quoad doctrinam, constat ex significatione `populi' quod sit doctrina, de qua etiam supra n. 4468.
