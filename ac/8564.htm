Potts(1905-1910) 8564

8564. And they said, Give ye us water and we will drink. That this signifies an ardent longing for truth, is evident from what was shown just above (n. 8562) on the signification of "water" and of "drinking."

Elliott(1983-1999) 8564

8564. 'And said, Give us water and let us drink' means a burning desire for truth. This is clear from what has been shown just above in 8562 about the meaning of 'water' and 'drinking'.

Latin(1748-1756) 8564

8564. `Et dixerunt, Date nobis aquam et bibamus': quod significet veri ardens desiderium, constat ex illis quae mox supra n. 8562 de significatione `aquae' et `bibere' ostensa sunt.
