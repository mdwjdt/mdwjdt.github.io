属天的奥秘 第10426节

10426.“是领你们从埃及地上来的”表引领他们的。如前所述（10409节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10426

10426. Which made thee to come up out of the land of Egypt. That this signifies which have led them, may be seen above (n. 10409).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10426

10426. 'Who caused you to come up out of the land of Egypt' means which led them, as above in 10409.

Latin(1748-1756) 10426

10426. `Qui ascendere fecerunt te e terra Aegypti': quod significet quae (x)duxerunt, ut supra n. 10,409.
