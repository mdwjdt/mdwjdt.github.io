属天的奥秘 第1021节

1021.“挪亚和同他一起的儿子”表古教会，这在前面已说明，并清楚可见于本章随后部分，故无需在此赘述。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1021. The symbolism of Noah and his sons with him as the ancient church has already been demonstrated and will also be evident later in the chapter [531, 764-765, 768, 773, 788, 915, 1025:3, 1062-1063], so there is no need to spend time corroborating it here.

Potts(1905-1910) 1021

1021. That by "Noah and his sons with him" is signified the Ancient Church, has been shown before, and will be evident in what follows in this chapter, so that there is no need to confirm it now.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1021

1021. That 'Noah and his sons with him' means the Ancient Church has been shown already and will be clear in subsequent parts of this chapter. Consequently there is no need to pause and confirm the point here.

Latin(1748-1756) 1021

1021. Quod per 'Noahum et filios ejus cum eo' significetur Ecclesia Antiqua, prius ostensum est, et in sequentibus hujus capitis constabit, quare illis confirmandis immorari non opus est.
