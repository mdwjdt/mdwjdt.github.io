属天的奥秘 第7300节

7300.“法老心里刚硬”表固执。这从“心里刚硬”的含义清楚可知，“心里刚硬”是指固执，如前所述（7272节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7300

7300. And Pharaoh's heart was made firm. That this signifies obstinacy, is evident from the signification of the "heart being made firm," as being obstinacy (as above, n. 7272).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7300

7300. 'And Pharaoh's heart was unyielding' means obstinacy. This is clear from the meaning of 'the heart being unyielding' as obstinacy, as above in 7272.

Latin(1748-1756) 7300

7300. `Et obfirmatum cor Pharaonis': quod significet obstinationem, constat ex significatione `obfirmari cor' quod sit obstinatio, ut supra n. 7272.
