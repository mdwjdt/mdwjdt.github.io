属天的奥秘 第466节

466.第十个，即大洪水后三个教会的母教会，是“挪亚”，它将被称为古教会（28-29节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]466. The tenth, and the progenitor of three churches that followed the Flood, is Noah. This church must be called the ancient church (verses 28, 29).

Potts(1905-1910) 466

466. A tenth, the parent of three churches after the flood, was named "Noah." This church is to be called the Ancient Church (verses 28, 29).

Elliott(1983-1999) 466

466. The tenth, the parent Church of the three coming after the Flood, is Noah, which is to be called the Ancient Church, verses 28, 29.

Latin(1748-1756) 466

466. Decima, parens trium Ecclesiarum post diluvium, est Noah; quae Ecclesia vocanda est Ecclesia Antiqua, vers. 28, 29.
