Potts(1905-1910) 9885

9885. At the extremities of the breastplate. That this signifies in the extremes is evident from what was said above (see n. 9883).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9885

9885. 'At the ends of the breastplate' means in the most external parts. This is clear from what has been stated above in 9882.

Latin(1748-1756) 9885

9885. `Ad extremitates pectoralis': quod significet in extremis, constat ex illis quae supra n. 9882 dicta sunt.
