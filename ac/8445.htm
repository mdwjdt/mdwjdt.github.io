属天的奥秘 第8445节

8445.“我已经听见以色列人的怨言”表由试探所产生的报怨将停止。如前所述（8428，8433节），那里有同样的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 8445

8445. I have heard the murmurings of the sons of Israel, signifies that the complaints arising from the temptation will cease (as above, n. 8428, 8433, where are like words).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8445

8445. 'I have heard the grumblings of the children of Israel' means that there will be an end to the complaints which the temptation gives rise to, as above in 8428, 8433, where similar words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 8445

8445. `Audivi murmurationes filiorum Israelis': quod significet quod cessaturae querimoniae quae ex tentatione, ut supra n. 8428, 8433, ubi similia verba.
