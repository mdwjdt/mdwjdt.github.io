属天的奥秘 第2103节

2103.“正当那日”表被论及的状态。这从“日”的含义清楚可知，“日”的内义是指状态（参看23，487，488，493，893节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 2103

2103. In the selfsame day. That this signifies the state that has been spoken of, is evident from the signification of "day," as being in the internal sense state (see n. 23, 487, 488, 493, 893).

Elliott(1983-1999) 2103

2103. 'On that same day' means that state which has been spoken of. This is clear from the meaning of 'day' in the internal sense as state, dealt with in 23, 487, 488, 493, 893.

Latin(1748-1756) 2103

2103. `In eodem die hoc': quod significet statum illum de quo dictum, constat a significatione `diei' quod in sensu interno sit status, de qua n. 23, 487, 488, 493, 893.
