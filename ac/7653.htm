属天的奥秘 第7653节

7653.“这事为我们的网罗要到几时呢”表这样他们将被自己的邪恶所掳。这从“这事为网罗”的含义清楚可知，“这事为网罗”是指被自己的邪恶所掳，因而被引入惩罚所带来的邪恶中。

Potts(1905-1910) 7653

7653. How long shall this man be a snare to us? That this signifies that thus they would be caught by their own evil, is evident from the signification of "this man being a snare," as being to be caught by their own evil, and thus brought into the evil of the penalty.

Elliott(1983-1999) 7653

7653. 'How long will this thing be a snare to us?' means that by behaving in that way they would be held captive by their own evil. This is clear from the meaning of 'this thing being a snare' as being held captive by their own evil, and so being led into the misery that punishment brings.

Latin(1748-1756) 7653

7653. `Quousque erit hoc nobis in laqueum?': quod significet quod sic caperentur a malo suo, constat a significatione `hoc esse in laqueum' quod sit capi a malo suo, et sic induci in malum poenae.
