属天的奥秘 第9066节

9066.“以致死亡”表到了毁灭它的程度。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 9066

9066. And he die. That this signifies even so as to destroy them, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 9066

9066. 'And [the person] dies' means to the extent that it destroys it. This is clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 9066

9066. `Et moriatur': quod significet usque ut destruxerit, constat absque explicatione.
