5884.“他们就近前来”表结果，即内层交流得以实现。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 5884

5884. And they came near. That this signifies the effect, namely, that a more interior communication was effected, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5884

5884. 'And they drew near' means the effect, that is to say, the interior communication was accomplished. This is clear without explanation.

5884. `Et accesserunt': quod significet effectum, nempe quod communicatio interior facta, constat absque explicatione.
