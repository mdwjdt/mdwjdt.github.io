属天的奥秘 第525节

525.创世记5:26-27 玛土撒拉生拉麦之后，又活了七百八十二年；他生了众子和众女。玛土撒拉共活了九百六十九岁就死了。
这些话的含义和其它的一样。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]525. Genesis 5:26-27. And after Methuselah fathered Lamech he lived seven hundred eighty-two years. And he fathered sons and daughters. And all Methuselah's days were nine hundred sixty-nine years, and he died.
The meanings here are the same as for the rest.

Potts(1905-1910) 525

525. Verses 26, 27. And Methuselah lived after he begat Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters. And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred and sixty and nine years, and he died. These words have a like signification.

Elliott(1983-1999) 525

525. Verses 26, 27 And Methuselah lived after he beget Lamech seven hundred and eighty-two years; and he beget sons and daughters. And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred and sixty-nine years; and he died.

These words are similar in meaning to all the rest.

Latin(1748-1756) 525

525. Vers. 26, 27. Et vixit Methushelah, postquam genuit illum Lamechum, duobus et octoginta annis ei septingentis annis; et genuit filios et filias. Et fuerunt omnes dies Methushelah, novem et sexaginta anni et nongenti anni; et mortuus est. Haec similiter se habent sicut cum ceteris.
