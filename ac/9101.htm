属天的奥秘 第9101节

9101.“是卖了”表或如果他疏远它。这从“卖”的含义清楚可知，“卖”是指疏远（参看4098，4752，4758，5886，6143节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9101

9101. Or sell it. That this signifies or if he shall alienate it, is evident from the signification of "selling," as being to alienate (see n. 4098, 4752, 4758, 5886, 6143).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9101

9101. 'Or sells it' means, or if he alienates it. This is clear from the meaning of 'selling' as alienating, dealt with in 4098, 4752, 4758, 5886, 6143.

Latin(1748-1756) 9101

9101. `Vel vendiderit illud': quod significet si vel id abalienaverit, constat ex significatione `vendere' quod sit abalienare, de qua n. 4098, (x)4752, 4758, 5886, 6143.
