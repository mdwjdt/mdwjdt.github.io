属天的奥秘 第7965节

7965.“因为他们说，我们都要死了”表因为地狱正面对着他们。这从“死”的含义清楚可知，“死”是指地狱。“死”在灵义上是指地狱（参看5407，6119节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7965

7965. For they said, We be all dead. That this signifies thus hell for them, is evident from the signification of "to die," as being hell. (That "death" in the spiritual sense denotes hell, see n. 5407, 6119.)

Elliott(1983-1999) 7965

7965. 'For they said, We are all dying' means for the reason that hell was facing them. This is clear from the meaning of 'dying' as hell, 'death' in the spiritual sense being hell, see 5407, 6119.

Latin(1748-1756) 7965

7965. `Quia dixerunt, Omnes nos morimur': quod significet sic infernum illis, constat ex significatione `mori' quod sit infernum; quod `mors' in spirituali sensu sit infernum, videatur n. 5407, 6119.
