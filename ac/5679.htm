属天的奥秘 第5679节

5679.“他还活着吗”表这良善有生命。这从“活着”的含义清楚可知，“活着”是指属灵生命（参看5407节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5679

5679. Is he yet alive? That this signifies that it has life, is evident from the signification of "living," as being spiritual life (see n. 5407).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5679

5679. 'is he still alive?' means that this has life. This is clear from the meaning of 'being alive' as spiritual life, dealt with in 5407.

Latin(1748-1756) 5679

5679. `An adhuc is vivit': quod significet quod ei vita, constat ex significatione `vivere' quod sit vita spiritualis, de qua n. 5407.
