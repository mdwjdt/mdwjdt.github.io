属天的奥秘 第9746节

9746.“一角”表真理住在光中所在的地方。这从“向南的南边”的含义清楚可知，“向南的南边”，即此处的“一角”，是指真理住在光中所在的地方，如前所述（9742节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9746

9746. At the one corner. That this signifies where truth is in light, is evident from the signification of "the corner of the south southward," which is here "the one corner," as being where truth is in light (of which above, n. 9742).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9746

9746. 'To one corner' means where truth dwells in light. This is clear from the meaning of the corner of the south, towards the south, to which 'one corner' refers here, as the place where truth dwells in light, dealt with above in 9742.

Latin(1748-1756) 9746

9746. `Ad angulum unum': quod significet ubi verum in luce constat ex significatione `anguli meridiei ad austrum,' qui hic es angulus unus, quod sit ubi verum in luce, de qua supra n. 9742.
