属天的奥秘 第6883节

6883.“神又对摩西说，你要对以色列人这样说”表第三次教导。这从刚才的解释（6881节）清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 6883

6883. And God said further unto Moses, Thus shalt thou say unto the sons of Israel. That this signifies the third instruction is evident from what has been unfolded just above (n. 6881).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6883

6883. 'And God said further still to Moses, Thus you are to say to the children of Israel' means the instruction that is provided third. This is clear from the explanation just above in 6881.

Latin(1748-1756) 6883

6883. `Et dixit adhuc Deus ad Moschen, Ita dicas ad filios Israelis': quod significet instructionem tertiam, constat ex illis quae mox supra n. 6881 explicata sunt.
