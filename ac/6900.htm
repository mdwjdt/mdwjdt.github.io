属天的奥秘 第6900节

6900.“他们必听你的声音”表顺从。这从“听”的含义清楚可知，“听”是指顺从（参看2542，3869，4652-4660节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6900

6900. And they shall hear thy voice. That this signifies obedience, is evident from the signification of "hearing," as being obedience (see n. 2542, 3869, 4652-4660).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6900

6900. 'And they will hear your voice' means obedience. This is clear from the meaning of 'hearing' as obedience, dealt with in 2542, 3869, 4652-4660.

Latin(1748-1756) 6900

6900. `Et audiverint vocem tuam': quod significet oboedientiam, constat ex significatione `audire' quod sit oboedientia, de qua n. 2542, 3869, 4652-4660.
