属天的奥秘 第6258节

6258.“不能看见”表没有注意到。这从“看见”的含义清楚可知，“看见”是指觉知（参看6249节），因而是指注意。

Potts(1905-1910) 6258

6258. He could not see. That this signifies no advertence, is evident from the signification of "seeing," as being perception (see n. 6249), thus also advertence.

Elliott(1983-1999) 6258

6258. 'He could not see' means not noticing. This is clear from the meaning of seeing' as discerning, dealt with above in 6249, thus also noticing.

Latin(1748-1756) 6258

6258. `Non potuit videre': quod significet non animadvertentiam, constat ex significatione `videre' quod sit apperceptio, de qua supra n. 6249, ita quoque animadvertentia.
