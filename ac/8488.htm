属天的奥秘 第8488节

8488.“到第六天”表在每个状态结束的时候。这从“第六天”的含义清楚可知，“第六天”是指每个状态的结束（参看8421节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8488

8488. And it was on the sixth day. That this signifies at the end of every state, is evident from the signification of "the sixth day," as being the end of every state (see above, n. 8421).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8488

8488. 'And so it was, that on the sixth day' means at the end of each state. This is clear from the meaning of 'the sixth day' as fee end of each state, dealt with above in 8421.

Latin(1748-1756) 8488

8488. `Et fuit in die sexto': quod significet ad finem cujusvis status, constat ex significatione `diei sexti' quod sit finis cujusvis status, de qua supra n. 8421.
