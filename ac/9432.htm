属天的奥秘 第9432节

9432.“第七天祂呼召摩西”表当真理与良善结合时，主的降临。这从“第七天”的含义清楚可知，“第七天”是指第二种状态，就是当真理与良善结合之时，也就是当此人处于良善之时（对此，可参看刚才所述9431节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9432

9432. And on the seventh day He called unto Moses. That this signifies when truth has been conjoined with good, is evident from the signification of "the seventh day," as being the second state, when truth has been conjoined with good, that is, when the man is in good (concerning which see what was said just above, n. 9431).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9432

9432. 'And He called to Moses on the seventh day' means [the Lord's coming] when truth has been joined to good. This is clear from the meaning of 'the seventh day' as the second state, when truth has been joined to good, or when the person is governed by good, regarding which see what has been stated immediately above in 9431.

Latin(1748-1756) 9432

9432. `Et vocavit ad Moschen in die septimo': quod significet [adventum Domini] cum verum conjunctum bono, constat ex significatione `diei septimi' quod sit alter status, quando verum conjunctum est bono, seu quando homo in bono est, de qua videantur quae nunc supra n. 9431 dicta sunt.
