属天的奥秘 第2785节

2785.“就起身”表提升。这从“起身”的含义清楚可知，每当这个词出现在圣言中时，它都表示某种提升。

Potts(1905-1910) 2785

2785. And he rose up. That this signifies elevation, is evident from the signification of "rising up," as meaning where it occurs in the Word some elevation.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2785

2785. 'Rose up' means a raising up. This is clear from the meaning of 'rising up' - whenever this occurs in the Word - as a phrase that implies some kind of raising up.
