属天的奥秘 第10801节

10801.王权本身并不在这个人身上，只是附属于此人。认为王权在自己本人身上的君主，或认为尊重权威是由于他本人的当权者是愚蠢的。

Potts(1905-1910) 10801

10801. The royalty itself is not in the person, but is adjoined to the person. The king who believes that the royalty is in his own person, and the overseer who believes that the dignity of the overseership is in his own person, is not wise.

Elliott(1983-1999) 10801

10801. Monarchy itself is not invested in the person but it is an adjunct to the person. The monarch who thinks that monarchy resides within his own person, or anyone in authority who thinks that respect for authority is owed to his own person, is stupid.

Latin(1748-1756) 10801

10801. Ipsum regium non est in persona sed adjunctum est personae; rex qui credit quod regium sit in sua persona, et praefectus qui credit quod praefecturae dignitas sit in sua persona, non sapit.
