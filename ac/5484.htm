5484.“在他们眼前”表觉知。这从“眼”的含义清楚可知，“眼”是指理解和觉知（参看2701，4083，4403-4421，4523-4534节）

Potts(1905-1910) 5484

5484. Before their eyes. That this signifies to the perception, is evident from the signification of "eyes," as being the understanding and perception (see n. 2701, 4083, 4403-4421, 4523-4534).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5484

5484. 'before their eyes' means a discernment. This is clear from the meaning of 'the eyes' as understanding and discernment, dealt with in 2701, 4083, 4403-4421, 4523-4534.

Latin(1748-1756) 5484

5484. `Ad oculos illorum': quod significet ad apperceptionem, constat ex significatione `oculorum' quod sit intellectus et apperceptio, de qua n. 2701, 4083, {1} 4403-4421, 4523-4534. @1 i 4339$
