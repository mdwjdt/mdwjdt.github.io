属天的奥秘 第79节

79.属天之人就如这样一座园子，该园子属于主，由主赐予人享受，非人自有（15节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]79. People of heavenly character are this kind of garden. They are free to enjoy everything in the garden, but because it is the Lord's, they are not given personal possession of any of it (verse 15).

Potts(1905-1910) 79

79. The celestial man is such a garden. But as the garden is the Lord's, it is permitted this man to enjoy all these things, and yet not to possess them as his own (verse 15).

Elliott(1983-1999) 79

79. The celestial man is a garden such as this. But because it is the Lord's, though he is free to enjoy all things in it he is not free to take possession of them as his own, verse 15.

Latin(1748-1756) 79

79. Homo caelestis talis hortus est; sed quia est Domini, omnibus illis frui ei conceditur sed non ut sua possidere, vers. 15.
