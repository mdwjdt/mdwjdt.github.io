属天的奥秘 第8525节

8525.“要将一满俄梅珥吗哪”表最大的能力。这从“俄梅珥”的含义清楚可知，“俄梅珥”是指足够的量和能力（参看8468，8473节）；“俄梅珥”因表示足够的量，或一个人所拥有的容量，故也表示他的最大能力。

Potts(1905-1910) 8525

8525. Fill an omer with it. That this signifies the highest degree of power, is evident from the signification of "an omer," as being sufficient, and power (see n. 8468, 8473); and as "an omer" denotes sufficient, or as much as one person has, so also it denotes his highest degree of power.

Elliott(1983-1999) 8525

8525. 'Fill an omer with it' means the greatest capacity. This is clear from the meaning of 'an omer' as the sufficient amount, and capacity, dealt with in 8468, 8473; and since 'an omer' is the sufficient amount, or the capacity one person has, it also means his greatest capacity.

Latin(1748-1756) 8525

8525. `Imple omerem ex illo': quod significet summum potentiae gradum, constat ex significatione `omeris' quod sit quantum satis et potentia, de qua n. 8468, 8473, et quia omer est quantum satis, seu quantum potentiae uni, ita quoque ei est summum potentiae gradus.
