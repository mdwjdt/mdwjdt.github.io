属天的奥秘 第7283节

7283.“摩西、亚伦这样行，耶和华怎样吩咐他们，他们就照样行了”表所说的事也都行了。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 7283

7283. And Moses and Aaron did so; as Jehovah commanded them, so did they. That this signifies that what things were said were also done, can be seen without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 7283

7283. 'And Moses and Aaron did so; as Jehovah had commanded, so they did' means that the things which had been said were also done. This becomes clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 7283

7283. `Et fecit Moscheh et Aharon, quemadmodum praecepit Jehovah, ita fecerunt': quod significet quod quae dicta etiam facta sint, constare potest absque explicatione.
