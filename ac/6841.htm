属天的奥秘 第6841节

6841.“从荆棘中间”表出于真正的记忆知识或记忆知识的真理。这从“荆棘”的含义清楚可知，“荆棘”是指真正的记忆知识，或记忆知识的真理（参看6832，6833节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6841

6841. Out of the midst of the bramble. That this signifies from the truths of memory-knowledge, is evident from the signification of a "bramble," as being the truth of memory-knowledge (see n. 6832, 6833).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6841

6841. 'From the middle of the bramble bush' means out of true factual knowledge. This is clear from the meaning of 'the bramble bush' as true factual knowledge, dealt with in 6832, 6833.

Latin(1748-1756) 6841

6841. `E medio rubi': quod significet e veris scientificis, constat ex significatione `rubi' quod sit verum scientificum, de qua n. 6832, 6833.
