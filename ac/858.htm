属天的奥秘 第858节

858.“十月”表存在于余留中的真理。这一点从表示余留的“十”（576节）的含义，以及刚才有关居于内在人的余留的阐述清楚可知。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]858. The symbolism of the tenth month as the truth belonging to the remnant is established by the symbolism of ten as a remnant (576) and by what was said just above about the remnant in the inner self.

Potts(1905-1910) 858

858. That the "tenth month" signifies the truths which are of remains, is evident from the signification of "ten" as being remains (n. 576); and from what was said above concerning the remains in the internal man.

Elliott(1983-1999) 858

858. 'The tenth month' means the truths that are present within remnants. This is clear from the meaning of 'ten' as remnants, 576, and also from what has been stated just above about remnants residing with the internal man.

Latin(1748-1756) 858

858. Quod 'mensis decimus' significet vera quae sunt reliquiarum, constat a significatione 'decem,' quod sint reliquiae, n. 576, tum ex illis quae mox supra de reliquiis apud internum hominem dicta sunt.
