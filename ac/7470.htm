Potts(1905-1910) 7470

7470. And Jehovah did according to the word of Moses. That this signifies that it was so done according to the Word of the Lord, is evident from what was unfolded above (n. 7406).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7470

7470. 'And Jehovah did according to the word of Moses' means that it was done according to the Lord's word. This is clear from the explanation above in 7406.
