属天的奥秘 第9821节

9821.“可以给我供祭司的职分”表主的一个代表。这从前面的说明清楚可知（9809节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9821

9821. To his ministering in the priest's office to Me. That this signifies a representative of the Lord, is evident from what was shown above (n. 9809).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9821

9821. 'So that he may serve Me in the priestly office' means that which is representative of the Lord. This is clear from what has been shown above in 9809.

Latin(1748-1756) 9821

9821. `Ad sacerdotio fungendum eum Mihi': quod significet repraesentativum Domini, constat ex illis quae supra n. 9809 ostensa sunt.
