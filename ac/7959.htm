属天的奥秘 第7959节

7959.“依你们所说的”表照着他们的意愿。这从“说”的含义清楚可知，“说”是指意愿（参看2626节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7959

7959. According to your speaking. That this signifies according to the will, is evident from the signification of "to say," as being the will (see n. 2626).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7959

7959. 'In accordance with what you have spoken' means in accordance with their will. This is Clear from the meaning of 'speaking' as the will, dealt with in 2626.

Latin(1748-1756) 7959

7959. `Secundum loqui vos': quod significet secundum voluntatem, constat ex significatione `loqui' quod sit voluntas, de qua n. 2626.
