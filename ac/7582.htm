属天的奥秘 第7582节

7582.“从人到牲畜”表它的内层和外层良善。这从“人和牲畜”的含义清楚可知，“人和牲畜”是指内层和外层良善（参看7424，7523节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7582

7582. From man and even to beast. That this signifies its interior and exterior good, is evident from the signification of "man and beast," as being interior and exterior good (see n. 7424, 7523).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7582

7582. 'From man and even to beast' means its good, interior and exterior. This is clear from the meaning of 'man and beast' as interior and exterior good, dealt with in 7424, 7523.

Latin(1748-1756) 7582

7582. `Ab homine et usque ad bestiam': quod significet bonum ejus interius et exterius, constat ex significatione `hominis et bestiae' quod sit bonum interius et exterius, de qua n. 7424, 7523.
