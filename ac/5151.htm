属天的奥秘 第5151节

5151.“你的梦是这样解”表它（即梦）里面所包含的东西。这从“解”的含义清楚可知，“解”是指某物里面所包含的东西，也就是在里面的东西，如前所述（5093，5105，5107节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5151

5151. This is the interpretation thereof. That this signifies what [the dream] had in it, is evident from the signification of "interpretation," as being what it has in it, or what is therein (of which above, n. 5093, 5105, 5107).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5151

5151. 'This is the interpretation of it' means what it held within it. This is clear from the meaning of 'the interpretation' as what something holds within it, that is, what lies within, dealt with above in 5093, 5105, 5107.

Latin(1748-1756) 5151

5151. `Haec interpretatio ejus': quod significet quid in se haberet, constat ex significatione `interpretationis' quod sit quid in se habeat, seu quid insit, de qua supra n. 5093, 5105, 5107.
