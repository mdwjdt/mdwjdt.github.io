9621.“要把五幅幔子连在一起，又把六幅幔子连在一起”表真理与良善并良善与真理的不断交流。如前所述（9604节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9621

9621. And thou shalt join together five curtains by themselves, and six curtains by themselves. That this signifies the constant communication of truth with good, and of good with truth, may be seen above (n. 9604).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9621

9621. 'And you shall join five curtains together by themselves, and six curtains by themselves' means unceasing communication of truth with good and of good with truth, as above in 9604.

Latin(1748-1756) 9621

9621. `Et conjunges quinque aulaea solum, et sex aulaea solum' quod significet communicationem veri cum bono (c)et boni cum vera constantem, ut {1}supra n. 9604. @1 quoque$
