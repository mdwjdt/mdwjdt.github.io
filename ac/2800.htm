属天的奥秘 第2800节

2800．“于是二人同行”表（神性本身与神圣理性）尽可能地融合。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 2800

2800. And they went both of them together. That this signifies unition as far as possible, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2800

2800. 'And the two of them went on together' means the closest possible union then [of the Divine itself and the Divine Rational]. This becomes clear without explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 2800

2800. `Et iverunt ambo illi simul': quod significet unitionem quantum possibile, constare potest absque explicatione.
