属天的奥秘 第5143节

5143.“我在梦中”表预言。这从“梦”的含义清楚可知，“梦”是指关于结果的预言（参看5092，5104，5112节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5143

5143. I also was in my dream. That this signifies prediction, is evident from the signification of a "dream," as being prediction concerning the event (see n. 5092, 5104, 5112).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5143

5143. 'I also was in my dream' means a foretelling. This is clear from the meaning of 'a dream' as a foretelling regarding the outcome, dealt with in 5092, 5104, 5112.

Latin(1748-1756) 5143

5143. `Etiam ego in somnio meo': quod significet praedictionem constat ex significatione `somnii' quod sit praedictio de eventu, de qua n. 5092, 5104, 5112.
