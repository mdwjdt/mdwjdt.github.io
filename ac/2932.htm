属天的奥秘 第2932节

2932.“就请听从我”表他们要顺从。这从“听”的含义清楚可知，“听”是指听从或顺从（参看2542节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 2932

2932. Hear me. That this signifies that they should comply, is evident from the signification of "hearing," as being to obey, or comply (see n. 2542).

Elliott(1983-1999) 2932

2932. 'Hear me' means that they were to comply. This is clear from the meaning of 'hearing' as obeying or complying, dealt with in 2542.

Latin(1748-1756) 2932

2932. `Audite me': quod significet ut obtemperarent, constat a significatione `audire' quod sit oboedire seu obtemperare, de qua n. 2542.
