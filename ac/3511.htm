属天的奥秘 第3511节

3511.“你把野味给我带来”表从良善所获得的真理。这从“野味”的含义清楚可知，“野味”是指从良善所获得的真理（3501节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 3511

3511. Bring me a hunting. That this signifies the truth of good, is evident from the signification of a "hunting," as being the truth of good (n. 3501).

Elliott(1983-1999) 3511

3511. 'Bring me venison' means truth acquired from good. This is clear from the meaning of 'venison' as truth acquired from good, dealt with above in 3501.

Latin(1748-1756) 3511

3511. `Adduc mihi venationem': quod significet boni verum, constat ex significatione `venationis' quod sit boni verum, de qua supra n. 3501.
