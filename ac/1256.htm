属天的奥秘 第1256节

1256.“立族”表这个教会总体上的敬拜形式。这从前面有关民族的论述，以及下文清楚可知。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1256. The symbolism of in their nations as the general ways that church worshiped can be seen from statements above and below concerning nations.{*1}
Footnotes:
{*1} For statements "above," see 1140-1143 and throughout the earlier parts of this chapter, where the names of the various nations appear. For statements "below," see 1258-1259yyy1. [LHC]

Potts(1905-1910) 1256

1256. In their nations. That these nations signify the various worships of that church in general, is evident from what has been said before about nations, and from what follows.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1256

1256. 'In their nations' means the Church's forms of worship in general. This is clear from what has been stated already about nations, and from what follows.

Latin(1748-1756) 1256

1256. 'In gentibus eorum': quod significent cultus Ecclesiae communi, constat ab illis quae prius de gentibus dicta, et ab illis quae sequuntur.
