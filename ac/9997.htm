属天的奥秘 第9997节

9997.“用筐子带来”表由此产生的它们全部的同在。这从“带来”和“筐子”的含义清楚可知：“带来”是指结合和同在（参看9378节）；“筐子”是指包含它们全部或一切事物的感官层，如刚才所述（9996节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9997

9997. And bring them near in the basket. That this signifies thus the presence of all, is evident from the signification of "bringing near," as being conjunction and presence (see n. 9378); and from the signification of a "basket," as being the sensuous in which are all things (of which just above, n. 9996).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9997

9997. 'And bring them in the basket' means the resulting presence of them all. This is clear from the meaning of 'bringing them' as togetherness and presence, dealt with in 9378; and from the meaning of 'the basket' as the level of the senses which contains them all, dealt with immediately above in 9996.

Latin(1748-1756) 9997

9997. `Et appropinquare facies illa in corbe': quod significet sic praesentiam {1} omnium, constat ex significatione `appropinquare facere' quod sit conjunctio (d)et praesentia, de qua n. 9378, et ex significatione `corbis' quod sit sensuale in quo omnia, de qua mox supra n. 9996. @1 d praesentiam i conjunctionem$
