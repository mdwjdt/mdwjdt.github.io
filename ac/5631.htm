属天的奥秘 第5631节

5631.“和便雅悯”表以及内层真理。这从“便雅悯”的代表清楚可知，“便雅悯”是指内层真理，如前所述（5600节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5631

5631. And Benjamin. That this signifies, and also interior truth, is evident from the representation of Benjamin, as being interior truth (of which above, n. 5600).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5631

5631. 'And Benjamin' means so that interior truth may be granted also. This is clear from the representation of 'Benjamin' as interior truth, dealt with above in 5600.

Latin(1748-1756) 5631

5631. `Et Benjaminem': quod significet utque verum interius, constat ex repraesentatione `Benjaminis' quod sit verum interius, de qua supra n. 5600.
