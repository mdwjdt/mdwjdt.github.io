属天的奥秘 第8359节

8359.“又说”表指示。这从“说”的含义清楚可知，当由耶和华来“说”试探方面的秩序真理时，“说”是指指示（也可参看6879，6881，6883，6891，7186，7267，7304，7380，7517，8127节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8359

8359. And He said. That this signifies instruction, is evident from the signification of "saying," when by Jehovah concerning the truth of order in respect to temptations, as being instruction (see also n. 6879, 6881, 6883, 6891, 7186, 7267, 7304, 7380, 7517, 8127).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8359

8359. 'And He said' means instruction. This is clear from the meaning of 'saying', when done by Jehovah, referring to the truth of order in respect of temptations, as instruction, as also in 6879, 6881, 6883, 6891, 7186, 7267, 7304, 7380, 7517, 8127.

Latin(1748-1756) 8359

8359. `Et dixit': quod significet instructionem, constat (c)ex significatione `dicere,' cum a Jehovah de vero ordinis quoad (x)tentationes, quod sit instructio, ut quoque n. 6879, 6881, (x)6883, 6801, 7186, (x)7267, 7304, 7380, 7517, 8127.
