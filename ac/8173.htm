属天的奥秘 第8173节

8173.“今天向你们所要施行”表将持续永恒之物。这从“今天”的含义清楚可知，“今天”是指永恒之物（参看2838，3998，4304，6165，6984节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8173

8173. Which He will do for you today. That this signifies which is to eternity, is evident from the signification of "today," as being what is eternal (see n. 2838, 3998, 4304, 6165, 6984).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8173

8173. 'Which He will accomplish today' means which will continue into eternity. This is clear from the meaning of 'today' as that which is eternal, dealt with in 2838, 3998, 4304, 6165, 6984.

Latin(1748-1756) 8173

8173. x). `Quam faciet hodie': {1}quod significet quae in aeternum, constat ex significatione `hodie' quod sit aeternum, de qua n. 2838, 3998, 4304, 6165, 6984. @1 significat$
