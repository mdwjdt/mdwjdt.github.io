4546.“我们要起来，上伯特利去”表神性属世层。这从前面所述（4539节）清楚可知，那里有同样的话。

Potts(1905-1910) 4546

4546. And let us arise, and go up to Bethel. That this signifies the Divine natural, is evident from what was said above (n. 4539), where the same words occur.

Elliott(1983-1999) 4546

4546. 'And let us rise up and go up to Bethel' means the Divine Natural. This is clear from what has been stated above in 4539, where the same words occur.

Latin(1748-1756) 4546

4546. `Et surgamus et ascendamus Bethelem': quod significet Naturale Divinum, constat ab illis quae supra n. 4539 dicta sunt, ubi eadem verba.
