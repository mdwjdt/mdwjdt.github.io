属天的奥秘 第8534节

8534.“耶和华怎么吩咐摩西”表照着这个命令。这从“吩咐”的含义清楚可知，当由耶和华来吩咐时，“吩咐”是指一个命令（参看8466，8524节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8534

8534. As Jehovah commanded Moses. That this signifies according to the mandate, is evident from the signification of "commanding," when by Jehovah, as being a mandate (see above, n. 8466, 8524).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8534

8534. 'As Jehovah commanded Moses' means in accordance with what was commanded. This is clear from the meaning of 'commanding', when done by Jehovah, as a command, as above in 8466, 8524.

Latin(1748-1756) 8534

8534. `Quemadmodum praecepit Jehovah ad Moschen': quod significet secundum mandatum, constat ex significatione `praecipere' cum a Jehovah, quod sit mandatum, ut supra n. 8466, 8524.
