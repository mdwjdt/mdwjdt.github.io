属天的奥秘 第758节

758.创世记7:12．四十昼夜降大雨在地上。
这表示试探持续的时间，“雨”是试探，“四十昼夜”表它的持续。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]758. Genesis 7:12. And there was a downpour on the earth for forty days and forty nights means that this struggle continued. The downpour is the struggle, forty days and nights is its duration.

Potts(1905-1910) 758

758. Verse 12. And the rain was upon the earth forty days and forty nights. This signifies that the temptation continued. "Rain" is temptation; "forty days and forty nights" denotes its duration.

Elliott(1983-1999) 758

758. Verse it And there was rain over the earth for forty days and forty nights means the time this temptation lasted, 'rain' being temptation, 'forty days and nights' its duration.

Latin(1748-1756) 758

758. Vers. 12. Et fuit imber super terra quadraginta diebus et quadraginta noctibus, significat quod haec tentatio duraverit; 'imber' est tentatio, 'quadraginta dies et noctes' est duratio.
