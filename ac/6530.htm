属天的奥秘 第6530节

6530.“羊群”表仁爱。这从“羊群”的含义清楚可知，“羊群”是指仁爱的内层良善（参看5913，6048节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6530

6530. And their flocks. That this signifies charity, is evident from the signification of "flocks," as being the interior good of charity (see n. 5913, 6048).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6530

6530. 'And their flocks' means charity. This is clear from the meaning of 'flocks' as the interior good of charity, dealt with in 5913, 6048.

Latin(1748-1756) 6530

6530. `Et greges suos': quod significet charitatem, constat ex significatione `gregum' quod sint bonum charitatis interius, de qua n. 5913, 6048.
