属天的奥秘 第8597节

8597.“出去和亚玛力争战”表对抗内层邪恶所生的虚假。这从“亚玛力”的代表清楚可知，“亚玛力”是指由内层邪恶所产生的虚假（参看8593节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 8597

8597. And go out, fight against Amalek. That this signifies against the falsities from interior evil, is evident from the representation of Amalek, as being falsities from interior evil (see above, n. 8593).

Elliott(1983-1999) 8597

8597. 'And go out, fight against Amalek' means against falsities arising from interior evil. This is clear from the representation of 'Amalek' as falsities arising from interior evil, dealt with above in 8593.

Latin(1748-1756) 8597

8597. `Et exi, pugna in Amalekum': quod (x)significet contra falsa ex interiore malo, constat ex repraesentatione `Amaleki' quod sint falsa ex interiore malo, de qua supra n. 8593.
