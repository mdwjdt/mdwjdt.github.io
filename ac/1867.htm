属天的奥秘 第1867节

1867.创世记15:19-21. 就是基尼人、基尼洗人、甲摩尼人、赫人、比利洗人、利乏音人、亚摩利人、迦南人、革迦撒人、耶布斯人之地。
“基尼人、基尼洗人、甲摩尼人”表被逐出主国度的虚假。“赫人、比利洗人、利乏音人”表谬念。“亚摩利人、迦南人”表邪恶。“革迦撒人、耶布斯人”表源于邪恶的虚假。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1867. Genesis 15:19-20, 21. The Kenite, and the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite; and the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Rephaim; and the Amorite, and the Canaanite, and the Girgashite, and the Jebusite.
The Kenite, and the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite, symbolize falsities that need to be banished from the Lord's kingdom. The Hittite, the Perizzite, and the Rephaim, symbolize persuasive lies. The Amorite and the Canaanite symbolize evil. The Girgashite and the Jebusite symbolize falsity rising out of evil.

Potts(1905-1910) 1867

1867. Verses 19, 20, 21. The Kenite, and the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite, and the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Rephaim, and the Amorite, and the Canaanite, and the Girgashite, and the Jebusite. "The Kenite, and the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite," signify falsities which are to be expelled from the Lord's kingdom; "the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Rephaim," signify persuasions of falsity; "the Amorite and the Canaanite," signify evils; "the Girgashite and the Jebusite," signify falsities from evils.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1867

1867. Verses 19-21 The Kenite, and the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite, and the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Rephaim, and the Amorite, and the Canaanite, and the Girgashite, and the Jebusite.

The Kenite, and the Kenizzite, and the Kadmonite' means the falsities that were to be cast out from the Lord's kingdom. 'The Hittite, the Perizzite, and the Rephaim' means false persuasions. 'The Amorite and the Canaanite' means evils. 'The Girgashite and the Jebusite' means falsities derived from evils.

Latin(1748-1756) 1867

1867. Vers. 19-21. Kenitam, et Kenizzitam, et Kadmonita Et Hittitam, et Perizzitam, et Rephaim. Et Emoritam, et Canaanita et Girgashitam, et Jebusitam. 'Kenita, et Kenizzita, et Kadmonita' significant falsa quae expellenda a regno Domini: 'Hittita, Perizzita et Rephaim' significant persuasiones falsi: 'Emorita et Canaanita' significant mala: 'Girgashita et Jebusita' significant ex malis falsa.
