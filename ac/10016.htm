属天的奥秘 第10016节

10016.“给他们包上裹头巾”表源于智慧的聪明。这从“头巾”的含义清楚可知，“头巾”是指源于智慧的聪明（参看9949节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 10016

10016. And shalt bind the tiaras on them. That this signifies intelligence from wisdom, is evident from the signification of "tiaras," as being intelligence from wisdom (see n. 9949).

Elliott(1983-1999) 10016

10016. 'And bind headdresses on them' means intelligence springing from wisdom. This is clear from the meaning of 'headdresses' as intelligence springing from wisdom, dealt with in 9949.

Latin(1748-1756) 10016

10016. `Et alligabis illis tiaras': quod significet intelligentiam ex sapientia, constat ex significatione `tiararum' quod sint intelligentia ex sapientia, de qua n. 9949.
