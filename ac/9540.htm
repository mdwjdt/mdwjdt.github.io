属天的奥秘 第9540节

9540.“抬桌子的”表以便存在并持续存在。这从“抬”的含义清楚可知，“抬”是指持守在一种良善的状态，因而存在并持续存在（参看9500节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9540

9540. To carry the table. That this signifies for comingforth and subsistence, is evident from the signification of "carrying," as being to hold together in a state of good, thus to come forth and subsist (see n. 9500).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9540

9540. 'To carry the table' means in order to bring into being and keep in being. This is clear from the meaning of 'carrying' as holding in a state of good, thus as bringing into being and keeping in being, as above in 9500.

Latin(1748-1756) 9540

9540. `Ad portandum mensam': quod significet ad existentiam et subsistentiam, constat ex significatione `portare' quod sit continere in statu boni, ita existere et subsistere, ut supra n. 9500.
