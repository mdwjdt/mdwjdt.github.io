属天的奥秘 第7339节

7339.“不肯听他们”表不接受，不服从。如前所述（7224，7275，7301节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7339

7339. And he heard them not. That this signifies no reception and no obedience, see also above, n. 7224, 7275, 7301.

Elliott(1983-1999) 7339

7339. 'And he did not hear them' means non-acceptance and disobedience, also as above, in 7224, 7275, 7301.

Latin(1748-1756) 7339

7339. `Et non audivit ad illos': quod significet non receptionem et non oboedientiam, ut quoque supra n. 7224, 7275, 7301.
