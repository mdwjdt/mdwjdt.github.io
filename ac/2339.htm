属天的奥秘 第2339节

2339.“他们这才转向他”表呆在一起。这从前面类似话的含义（2330节）清楚可知，故无需进一步解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 2339

2339. And they turned aside unto him. That this signifies abode, is evident from the signification of the same words above (n. 2330); thus without further explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2339

2339. 'And they turned aside to him' means staying with. This is clear from the meaning of the same words above in 2330 and so without further explanation.

Latin(1748-1756) 2339

2339. `Et declinaverunt ad eum': quod significet mansionem, constat a significatione eorundem verborum supra n. 2330, ita absque ulteriore explicatione.
