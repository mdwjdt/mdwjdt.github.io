属天的奥秘 第9531节

9531.“高一肘半”表就层级而言的完整或完全之物。如前所述（9489节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9531

9531. And a cubit and a half the height thereof, signifies full in respect to degrees (as above, n. 9489).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9531

9531. 'And a cubit and a half its height' means what is complete so far as degrees are concerned, as above in 9489.

Latin(1748-1756) 9531

9531. 'Et cubitus et dimidius altitudo ejus': quod significet plenum quoad gradus, ut supra n. 9489.
