属天的奥秘 第5104节

5104.“我们作了一梦”表预言。这从“梦”的含义清楚可知，“梦”是指预见，因而是指预言，如前所述（5091节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5104

5104. We have dreamed a dream. That this signifies prediction, is evident from the signification of a "dream," as being foresight, and hence prediction (of which also above n. 5091).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5104

5104. 'We have dreamed a dream' means a foretelling. This is clear from the meaning of 'a dream' as foresight and a consequent foretelling, dealt with above in 5091.

Latin(1748-1756) 5104

5104. Somnium somniavimus': quod significet praedictionem, constat ex significatione `somnii' quod sit praevidentia et inde praedictio, de qua supra n. 5091.
