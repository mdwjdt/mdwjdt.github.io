属天的奥秘 第9191节

9191.“那人必死”表诅咒。这从“死”的含义清楚可知，“死”是指诅咒（参看5407，6119，9008节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 9191

9191. Dying he shall die. That this signifies damnation, is evident from the signification of "dying," as being damnation (see n. 5407, 6119, 9008).

Elliott(1983-1999) 9191

9191. 'Shall surely die' means damnation. This is clear from the meaning of 'dying' as damnation, dealt with in 5407, 6119, 9008.

Latin(1748-1756) 9191

9191. Moriendo morietur': quod significet damnationem, constat ex significatione `mori' quod sit damnatio, de qua n. 5407, 6119, 9008.
