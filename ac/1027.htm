1027.“飞鸟”特指其觉知之物。这从前面有关鸟的频繁论述清楚可知（如40，776节）。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1027. The fact that a bird symbolizes our intellectual abilities in particular can be seen from several previous remarks and explanations, like those in 40 and 776.

Potts(1905-1910) 1027

1027. The fowl. That this signifies specifically the things of his understanding, is evident from what has been said and shown before about fowls (n. 40, 776).

Elliott(1983-1999) 1027

1027. 'With birds' means specifically the things of his understanding. This is clear from what has been stated and shown frequently already about birds, as in 40, 776

Latin(1748-1756) 1027

1027. 'Ad avem': quod significet in specie ejus intellectualia constat ab illis quae aliquoties prius de avibus dicta et ostensa sunt ut quae n. 40, 776.
