属天的奥秘 第6131节

6131.“第二年他们又来到约瑟那里”表下一个状态的开始。这从前文清楚可知，因而无需进一步解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 6131

6131. They came unto him in the second year. That this signifies the beginning of the following state, is evident from what goes before, thus without further explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 6131

6131. 'And they came to him in the second year' means the beginning of the next state. This is clear from what comes before this, thus without any further explanation

Latin(1748-1756) 6131

6131. `Et venerunt ad illum in anno altero': quod significet principium status sequentis, constat ex illis quae praecedunt, ita absque ulteriore explicatione.
