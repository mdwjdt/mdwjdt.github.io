属天的奥秘 第6891节

6891.“对他们说”表教导。这从“对他们说”的含义清楚可知，“对他们说”当是摩西所行的时，因摩西代表从神来的律法，故是指教导，如前所述（6879，6881，6883节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6891

6891. And say unto them. That this signifies instruction, is evident from the signification of "saying unto them," when this is done by Moses, by whom is represented the law from the Divine, as being instruction (as above, n. 6879, 6881, 6883).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6891

6891. 'And you are to say to them' means instruction. This is clear from the meaning of 'saying to them' - when it is to be done by Moses, who represents the law from God - as instruction, as above in 6879, 6881, 6883.

Latin(1748-1756) 6891

6891. `Et dicas ad illos': quod significet instructionem, constat (c)a significatione `dicere ad illos' cum hoc a Mosche, per quem repraesentatur lex a Divino, quod sit instructio, ut supra n. 6879, 6881, 6883.
