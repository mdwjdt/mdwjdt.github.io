属天的奥秘 第5742节

5742.“他们出城走了不远”表移除的程度。这从前文清楚可知。

Potts(1905-1910) 5742

5742. They were gone out of the city, not yet far off. That this signifies the amount of removal, may be seen from what has gone before.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5742

5742. They went out of the city, not far distant' means the extent of that removal. This becomes clear from what comes before this.

Latin(1748-1756) 5742

5742. `Illi exiverunt urbe, non elongati': quod significet remotionis quantum, constare potest ex illis quae praecedunt.
