属天的奥秘 第7204节

7204.“救你们脱离他们的苦工”表从征服他们的努力中完全释放出来。这从“苦工”的含义清楚可知，“苦工”是指征服的努力（参看6666，6670，6671节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 7204

7204. And I will liberate you from their service. That this signifies fully from the endeavor to subjugate, is evident from the signification of "service," as being an endeavor to subjugate (see n. 6666, 6670, 6671).

Elliott(1983-1999) 7204

7204. 'And will deliver you from their servitude' means complete release from the endeavour to establish control over them. This is clear from the meaning of 'servitude' or 'service' as an endeavour to establish control over, dealt with in 6666, 6670, 6671.

Latin(1748-1756) 7204

7204. `Et liberabo vos e servitio illorum': quod significet plene a conatu subjugationis, constat ex significatione `servitii' quod sit conatus subjugationis, de qua n. 6666, 6670, 6671.
