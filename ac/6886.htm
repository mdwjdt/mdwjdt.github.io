属天的奥秘 第6886节

6886.“差我到你们这里来”表祂将在他们的教会中。这从“差到你们这里来”的含义清楚可知，“差到你们这里来”是指祂将在属灵教会中（参看6876，6882节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 6886

6886. Hath sent me unto you. That this signifies that He will be in their church, is evident from the signification of "being sent to you," as being that He will be in the spiritual church (seen. 6876, 6882).

Elliott(1983-1999) 6886

6886. 'Has sent me to you' means that He will be in their Church. This is clear from the meaning of 'being sent to you' as the promise that He will be in the spiritual Church, dealt with above in 6876, 6882.

Latin(1748-1756) 6886

6886. `Misit me ad vos': quod significet quod in Ecclesia illorum erit, constat ex significatione `mitti ad vos' quod sit quod erit in Ecclesia spirituali, de qua supra n. 6876, 6882.
