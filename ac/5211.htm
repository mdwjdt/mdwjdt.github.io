属天的奥秘 第5211节

5211.“第二回作梦”表所作的提供。这从“作梦”的含义清楚可知，“作梦”是指所作的提供（参看5195节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5211

5211. And dreamed a second time. That this signifies what was provided, is evident from the signification of "dreaming," as being what is provided (see n. 5195).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5211

5211. 'And he dreamed a second time' means the provision that had been made. This is clear from the meaning of 'dreaming' as provision that has been made, dealt with in 5195.

Latin(1748-1756) 5211

5211. `Et somniavit secundo': quod significet provisum, constat ex significatione `somniare' quod sit provisum, de qua n. 5195.
