属天的奥秘 第4159节

4159.“拉结对她父亲说”表对良善。这从“父亲”的含义和拉班的代表清楚可知：“父亲”是指良善（参看3703节）；拉班，就是此处的“父亲”，是指居间或中间良善，如前所述。

Potts(1905-1910) 4159

4159. And she said to her father. That this signifies to good, is evident from the signification of "father," as being good (see n. 3703); and from the representation of Laban, who is here the "father," as being mediate good, concerning which above.

Elliott(1983-1999) 4159

4159. 'And she said to her father' means to good. This is clear from the meaning of 'father' as good, dealt with in 3703, and from the representation of Laban, to whom 'father' refers here, as intermediate good, dealt with already.

Latin(1748-1756) 4159

4159. `Et dixit ad patrem suum': quod significet ad bonum constat a significatione `patris' quod sit bonum, de qua n. 3703; et repraesentatione `Labanis' qui hic pater, quod sit bonum medium, de qua {1} prius. @1 i saepius$
