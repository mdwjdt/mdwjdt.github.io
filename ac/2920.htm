属天的奥秘 第2920节

2920.“请听”表接受。这从“听”的含义清楚可知，当用作表示同意的答复时，“听”是指接受。

Potts(1905-1910) 2920

2920. Hear us. That this signifies reception, is evident from the signification of "hear us," when it is a mode of answering to express assent, as being reception.

Elliott(1983-1999) 2920

2920. 'Hear us' means reception. This is clear from the meaning of 'hear us', when used as a reply expressing assent, as reception.

Latin(1748-1756) 2920

2920. `Audi nos': quod significet receptionem, constat a significatione `audi nos,' cum est formula responsoria annuentiae, quod sit receptio.
