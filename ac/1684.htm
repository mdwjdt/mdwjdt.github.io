属天的奥秘 第1684节

1684.“在西订谷”表不洁。这从前面（3节）关于西订谷和盐海的阐述清楚可知。

New Century Edition
Cooper(2008,2013)

[NCE]1684. The symbolism of in the valley of Siddim as something unclean can be seen from statements made above at verse 3 about the valley of Siddim and the salt sea [1666].

Potts(1905-1910) 1684

1684. In the valley of Siddim. That this signifies uncleanness, is evident from what was before said (verse 3) concerning the valley of Siddim and the Salt Sea.

Elliott(1983-1999) 1684

1684. 'In the valley of Siddim' means uncleanness. This is clear from what has been stated at verse 3 above about the valley of Siddim and the Salt Sea.

Latin(1748-1756) 1684

1684. 'In valle Siddim': quod significet immundum, constat ab illis quae supra ad vers. 3 de valle Siddim et mari salis dicta sunt.
