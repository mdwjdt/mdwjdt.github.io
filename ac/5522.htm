属天的奥秘 第5522节

5522.“去”表好叫它们由此可以生活。这从“去”的含义清楚可知，“去”是指生活（参看3335，3690，4882，5493节）。

Potts(1905-1910) 5522

5522. And go. That this signifies that so they may live, is evident from the signification of "going," as being to live (see n. 3335, 3690, 4882, 5493).

Elliott(1983-1999) 5522

5522. 'And go' means so that they may thereby live. This is clear from the meaning of 'going' as living, dealt with in 3335, 3690, 4882, 5493.

Latin(1748-1756) 5522

5522. `Et ite': quod significet ut sic vivant> constat ex significatione `ire' quod sit vivere, de qua n. 3335, 3690, 4882, 5493.
