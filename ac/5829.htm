属天的奥秘 第5829节

5829.“直到如今我也没有见他”表因为它已经消失了。这是显而易见的，无需解释。

Potts(1905-1910) 5829

5829. And I have not seen him hitherto. That this signifies because it has vanished, is evident without explication.

Elliott(1983-1999) 5829

5829. 'And I have not seen him since' means because it has disappeared. This is clear without explanation.
